## No investment, tax-free: Perks of Dubai Startup Visa

**ET Online — February 17, 2024 | 4:18 PM IST**

---

## What is a Startup Visa?

Many young entrepreneurs look to **startup visa programmes** in **Canada, Dubai, Ireland,** and other countries in search of more favourable conditions.  

Aware of the potential to draw in foreign talent, these countries provide **simplified procedures, low investment needs,** and alluring benefits like **funding, mentorship,** and **routes to citizenship or permanent residency.**  

These locations stand out as hubs for budding innovators to realise their ambitious ideas as the global startup scene develops, according to **Varun Singh**, MD, **XIPHIAS Immigration**.

---

## Dubai's Startup Visa

As part of the efforts of the **Dubai Future Foundation** to promote an innovative and creative culture in the city, **Dubai introduced the Startup Visa in 2017.**  

The Startup Visa for Dubai gives international business entrepreneurs a number of advantages. Read on to find out.

---

### No investment

As long as applicants have an **inventive and viable business idea** that the **Dubai Future Foundation** has approved, there are **no minimum investment amounts or turnover requirements** for their startups in Dubai.

---

### Tax-free

Dubai provides business owners with a **tax-free environment**, and there is **no corporate, income, or capital gains tax.**  

This enables young entrepreneurs to retain a larger portion of their earnings and **reinvest them in their companies.**

---

### Funding and support

Entrepreneurs can access a range of **funding and support programmes** in Dubai, including:

- The **Mohammed Bin Rashid Innovation Fund**  
- **Dubai SME**  
- **Dubai Startup Hub**  
- **Dubai Future Accelerators**

These initiatives offer mentorship, seed capital, and strategic partnerships to help startups scale quickly.
