# XIPHIAS Immigration: Premier Advisory Firm with Elite Services

**September 11, 2018**

XIPHIAS Immigration was founded in 2008 by Mr. Varun Singh. They started with a small office in Banshankari with 5 people and hundreds of clients. The industry was very new and needed organized players like them, with everything having a legal binding. Over the years, XIPHIAS has grown from Banashankari to Koramangala, from 5 to over 200 employees and from hundreds to thousands of happy clients.

XIPHIAS Immigration is an Indian firm specializing in Canadian immigration. Based in heart of Toronto, Canada, XIPHIAS works with Canadian embassies and consulates around the world. The company has helped many clients from around the globe to obtain landed immigrant (permanent resident) status and temporary status by representing them effectively to the citizenship and immigration in Canada. XIPHIAS complies with high standards of ethics and professionalism. Prior to accepting a case, the company goes thorough reviewing the credentials to ensure that it stands a reasonable chance of being accepted by the Government of Canada. In the event that an application does not meet the criteria for success, they inform their prospective client in an honest manner to avoid clients’ wasting their time and hard-earned money. Varun Singh founded with the vision and mission to provide opportunity for those who have dreams to work and settle abroad.
