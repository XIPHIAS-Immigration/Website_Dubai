## Canada, Dubai, Ireland: The new startup visa destinations for young entrepreneurs

**By ET Contributors**  
**Last Updated:** Feb 05, 2024 | 01:40 PM IST  

Young entrepreneurs, grappling with funding shortages and talent deficits, turn to startup visa programs in Canada, Dubai, and Ireland. These countries offer streamlined processes, minimal investment requirements, and perks like funding and support. Canada's Startup Visa Program grants permanent residency with no investment requirement.

In the realm of entrepreneurship, young visionaries often grapple with obstacles in their home countries, from funding shortages to talent deficits. Seeking more favourable conditions, many turn to startup visa programs in countries like Canada, Dubai, and Ireland. Recognising the potential of attracting foreign talent, these nations offer streamlined processes, minimal investment requirements, and enticing perks such as access to funding, mentorship, and pathways to permanent residency or citizenship. As the global startup landscape evolves, these destinations stand out as hubs for aspiring innovators to bring their visionary ideas to life.

---

## Canada: A land of opportunities and diversity

Canada launched its Startup Visa Program in 2013, which is the first of its kind in the world, linking immigrant entrepreneurs with experienced private sector organisations that have expertise in working with startups.  

Canada’s startup visa program offers the following benefits to foreign entrepreneurs:  

**Permanent residency:** Canada grants permanent residency to successful applicants and their family members, with no conditions attached to the success of their business.  

**Low investment requirement:** Canada does not require a minimum investment amount from the applicants, as long as they secure a commitment from a designated Canadian angel investor group or venture capital fund to invest in their business idea.  

**Access to resources and networks:** Canada offers a vibrant and diverse startup ecosystem, with many incubators, accelerators, co-working spaces, events, and competitions that support and connect entrepreneurs with mentors, investors, customers, and peers.

---

## Dubai: A hub of innovation and excellence

Dubai launched its Startup Visa in 2017, which is part of the Dubai Future Foundation’s initiatives to foster a culture of innovation and creativity in the city.  

Dubai’s startup visa offers the following benefits to foreign entrepreneurs:  

**No investment requirement:** Dubai does not require applicants to have a minimum investment amount or a minimum turnover for their startups, as long as they have a viable and innovative business idea that is approved by the Dubai Future Foundation.  

**Access to funding and support:** Dubai offers access to various funding and support programs for entrepreneurs, such as the Dubai Future Accelerators, the Dubai SME, the Dubai Startup Hub, and the Mohammed Bin Rashid Innovation Fund.  

**Tax-free environment:** Dubai offers a tax-free environment for entrepreneurs, with no income tax, corporate tax, or capital gains tax. This allows entrepreneurs to keep more of their profits and reinvest them in their businesses.

---

## Ireland: A gateway to Europe and beyond

Ireland launched its Startup Entrepreneur Programme (STEP) in 2012, which is designed to attract high-potential startups that can create jobs and contribute to the Irish economy.  

Ireland’s startup visa offers the following benefits to foreign entrepreneurs:  

**Low investment requirement:** Ireland requires applicants to have a minimum investment amount of €50,000 for their startups, which is equivalent to about Rs 4.5 crore in Indian rupees.  

**No job creation requirement:** Ireland doesn’t require applicants to create a specific number of jobs for their startups, as long as they can demonstrate that their startups have the potential to create jobs in the future.  

**Access to resources and networks:** Ireland offers a supportive and collaborative startup ecosystem, with many organisations, programs, and events that help and connect entrepreneurs with mentors, investors, customers, and peers.
