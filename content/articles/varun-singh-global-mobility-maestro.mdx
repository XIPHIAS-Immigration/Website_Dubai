# Varun Singh – The Global Mobility Maestro

It all began with a question. Sitting in a cramped office space in Bangalore back in 2008, **Varun Singh** stared at a world map tacked on the wall. Red pins marked countries he had studied—Canada, Australia, the UK, the U.S., New Zealand. Each pin wasn’t just a destination; it was an opportunity.  

He asked himself: *“Why should crossing borders be so hard for the ones who deserve it the most?”*  

With savings, a laptop, and a belief in transparency, Varun founded **XIPHIAS Immigration**, named after the swordfish, symbolizing speed, precision, and power. From that modest office, he built a global brand by counseling clients personally, studying legal frameworks, and forging international partnerships.

---

## Early life & formative years

Born and raised in India, Varun’s early years were shaped by aspiration and curiosity for global affairs.  

- **XLRI Jamshedpur**: honed his skills in talent management.  
- **IIM Bangalore**: sharpened his strategic and leadership abilities.  

Through internships and corporate roles, he gravitated toward **cross-border opportunities**—trade, regulation, and global mobility. Relationships with lawyers, consultants, and immigration experts laid the foundation for XIPHIAS.

---

## Journey to leadership

Varun officially founded **XIPHIAS Immigration Private Limited in 2009**, driven by a vision of transparency and excellence.  

Key pillars of the early years:  
- **Multidisciplinary team**: legal experts, immigration specialists, global outreach professionals.  
- **Proprietary eCRM portal**: allowed clients to track their migration journey in real time.  
- **Pre-assessment model**: rigorous eligibility checks and bespoke consultation.  
- **Transparency first**: no shortcuts, no hidden clauses, and full disclosure of documentation.  

This ethos differentiated XIPHIAS in a crowded industry.

---

## Milestones & achievements

Under Varun’s leadership, XIPHIAS achieved:  

- **Accreditations**: ICC RC recognition in Canada, MARA accreditation in Australia.  
- **Awards**: Global Business Leadership Award, India 5000 Best MSME Award, APJ Abdul Kalam Excellence Award, Asia Fastest-Growing Immigration Award, Best Immigration Service Provider (South India).  
- **Recognition**: Named among India’s *Top Five Most Promising Immigration Consultants*.  
- **Personal honors**: Entrepreneur of the Year 2015, Consultant of the Year (2018, 2021), Fastest-Growing Indian Company Excellence Award.  

> Beyond trophies, the real success is measured in **thousands of client success stories** worldwide.

## What sets Varun Singh apart

### Strategic learning & certifications
Varun is a **Fellow and CPD-accredited expert with the Investment Migration Council (IMC)**, staying at the forefront of policy and compliance.  

### Tech-enabled, human-centric service
XIPHIAS’s **eCRM system** empowers clients with real-time updates and document tracking—balanced with personal counselors for bespoke guidance.  

### Culture of integrity and care
- **Zero information leakage**  
- **Full documentation transparency**  
- **Counselling-first ethos**  

### Extensive global network
Strong connections with **embassies, governments, real estate developers, and legal advisors** across Canada, Australia, the UK, the US, and New Zealand.  

### CSR & sustainability
From supporting **natural farmers in Chikkaballapur** to team culture-building, Varun integrates **social impact into business growth**.
