## Tax-free income and pro-business ecosystem  

One of Dubai’s strongest draws is its **tax-free income policy**. Residents do not pay personal income tax, helping them retain more earnings.  

The city also fosters a **pro-business environment**:  

- **Free zones** with 100% foreign ownership  
- **Zero corporate tax** incentives  
- Simplified regulatory procedures  

This ecosystem supports startups, SMEs, and multinational corporations, fueling Dubai’s growth as a global business hub.

## A multicultural haven  

Dubai thrives on its **multicultural identity**, attracting expats from all over the world.  

- Rich mix of **traditions, cuisines, and cultures**  
- Vibrant leisure scene: malls, fine dining, beach resorts, desert adventures  
- Strong cultural heritage: traditional markets, art, and festivals  

This cultural blend makes Dubai one of the most inclusive global cities.
