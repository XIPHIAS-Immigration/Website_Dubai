# XIPHIAS Investment Migration Inc.: Fulfilling Global Aspirations with Varun Singh at the Forefront  

*By WorldBizInsider | August 6, 2025 | Lounge Interview, Press Release*  

---

## Building bridges, not walls  

There are visionaries who choose to build bridges rather than walls. An insight into the arena of  Varun Singh, MD at XIPHIAS Investment Migration Inc.  Under his stewardship, XIPHIAS has risen to be recognized as a  global leader in investment migration , helping individuals overcome barriers and achieve their aspirations.  

For Varun, immigration is not just business—it is a  life-changing journey  for individuals, families, and businesses across continents. His decision to build XIPHIAS was rooted in integrity, excellence, and empowerment.  

---

## A legacy of belief and bravery  

Founded in 2009, XIPHIAS has emerged as  India’s leading immigration law firm , specializing in citizenship, residency, and investment migration programs for countries including the USA, UK, Canada, Germany, Malta, and Cyprus.  

- Branch offices in  India, Dubai, Canada, and Australia   
- Associations with leading legal and corporate networks worldwide  
- Services spanning  citizenship by investment, residency by investment, skilled migration, and business immigration   

Varun Singh, a  mechanical engineer turned entrepreneur , has made XIPHIAS a “ world’s gate-keeper of growth and freedom .” His strategy combines foresight, compliance, and ethical advisory as the pillars of success.

## Strategy & compliance powering dreams  

In an industry clouded by false promises, Varun grounded XIPHIAS in  law and transparent advisory .

- Philosophy: migration is about  mobility + financial stability, education, healthcare, and quality of life
