# Leading Brands in India 2025  
*Brands that made a difference*  

**Last Updated:** September 11, 2025, 17:12 IST

## Proxgy: IoT innovation from India

**Proxgy** is a Gurugram-based startup known for blending **patented IoT hardware with AI-driven tech solutions** across industries.  

- Founded in 2020 by **Pulkit Ahuja**, a Shark Tank India S1 graduate.  
- Secured over **USD 5 Million funding** across rounds.  
- Winner of the **Global SuperNova Challenge (GITEX Africa 2023)**.  
- Holds **70+ patents** with products like SmartHat, AirShifter, Sleefe, Lockator, and BirdBox.  
- Recognitions include **CES Innovation Award**, **Graham Bell Award**, and **European Product Design Award**.  

---

## DPDzero: Reinventing collections with AI

**DPDzero** is a Human + AI-powered collections platform reshaping India’s **$6 trillion credit economy**.  

- Helps lenders recover efficiently with **AI + empathy**.  
- Trusted by **L&T Finance, Tata Capital, Moneyview, Paysense**.  
- Backed by **GMO VenturePartners, SMBC Asia Rising Fund, Blume Ventures, and India Quotient**.  

**Founder & CEO Ananth** said:  
> “Collection has long been the ignored stepchild of lending with low tech, low intelligence, and worryingly, low ethics. We are building the collections company India has been waiting for.”

## XIPHIAS Investment Migration: Global mobility brand

**XIPHIAS Investment Migration Inc.** is India’s **most trusted global mobility brand**, founded by **Varun Singh**.  

- Helped **6,000+ families relocate** to Canada, Australia, Europe, the Caribbean, and the UAE.  
- HQ in India with hubs in **Dubai (DMCC)** and **Canada (Toronto & Brampton)**.  
- Services include **Permanent Residency, Golden Visas, Work Permits, Global Business Establishment**.  
- Recognized globally for **ethics, transparency, and professionalism**.  

> Under Varun Singh’s leadership, XIPHIAS is not just consultancy—it is a **global gateway** for Indian families and investors.

## Akansha Rathi & Associates (ARACS): Compliance excellence

**Akansha Rathi & Associates** is a **leading company secretary advisory firm** based in Mumbai & Navi Mumbai.  

- **20+ years of legacy** serving 1,000+ clients with turnover up to ₹1000 crores.  
- Specializes in **startup compliance, fundraising, IPO readiness**.  
- Works with **regulated entities and foreign companies** entering India.  
- Founder **Akansha Rathi** is a certified **PoSH trainer**, having conducted 100+ sessions.  

Key differentiators: domain expertise, wide service breadth, par-excellence quality, and tech-driven zero non-compliance.

## Techno Exponent: Human architects of digital change

Founded in Kolkata, **Techno Exponent** has grown into a **global tech powerhouse** over 14 years.  

- Founders: **Dr. Sabyasachi Saha, Avoy Debnath, Jyoendrisa Tagore Saha**.  
- Expanded worldwide with offices in **Miami, Delhi, Dubai, Singapore**.  
- Clients include **Disney, AB InBev, Amazon, Warner Bros., NASA Engineers**.  
- Expertise in **AI/ML, blockchain, SaaS, cloud, web & mobile dev, IT staffing**.  
- Awarded **AI/ML Solution Provider of the Year 2024**.  
- Maintains a **98% client retention rate**.  

> Techno Exponent creates digital trends, proving that when **vision meets execution, the future is inevitable**.
