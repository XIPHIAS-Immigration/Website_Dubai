### What Is France’s Startup Visa? From Duration To Advantages, Here’s All About It

## Here’s All You Need To Know About France’s Startup Visa

*France’s Startup Visa*  
*Picture credit – Canva*

Launched exclusively for overseas talent, France’s startup visa prioritises simple processes to help people settle there and flourish in their entrepreneurship journeys. Enthusiasts associated with the world of technology and tech fields are welcome to the country to begin something new. The visa program aims to attract three kinds of tech people from different nations, according to a report by *The Financial Express*. The options are for founders, investors, and employees in the tech industries.

In conversation with *FinancialExpress.com*, **Varun Singh**, XIPHIAS Immigration MD, stated that this grand program is here to enable a structured procedure to influence people to launch businesses in France. The chances and facilities of this visa program impact remarkably for developing startups, exploring ideas, and simultaneously helping France to grow and evolve as a hub for new businesses and similar opportunities.

---

Applicants with approved France’s startup visa can stay in the European country for **four years**. This is the maximum stay duration for international citizens residing there. They need to renew their visas annually. It is a key addition to every entrepreneur’s life in France as this plays a huge role in getting a permit for residence.

---

## About The Program’s Advantages

People who are enthusiastic to launch startups in France and capture a hold in overseas industries can surely make the best use of this startup visa. There are multiple benefits and conveniences of this program. One of the crucial factors is allowing entrepreneurs’ family members (spouses and children up to 18 years of age) there. In addition, they can take part in activities in agricultural, commercial, and industrial fields.

People can apply for a multi-year residence permit within the initial two months of shifting to France. Also, families can apply for a multi-year permit for residence, according to a report by *Wise*.
