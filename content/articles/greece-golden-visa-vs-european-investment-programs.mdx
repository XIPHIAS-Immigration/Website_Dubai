## Greece Golden Visa Explained: Strengths and Limitations

### Key Advantages

The Greece Golden Visa remains attractive for practical reasons:

- Lower entry threshold compared to most EU programs  
- No minimum stay requirement — ideal for global investors  
- Full Schengen access from day one  
- Family inclusion: spouse, children, dependent parents  
- Residency renewable as long as investment is maintained  

For investors seeking **flexibility and capital preservation**, Greece offers a compelling balance.

### Limitations You Must Understand

- Citizenship is **not automatic** — requires long-term residence  
- Investment thresholds have increased in prime zones  
- Real estate market saturation can affect rental yields  
- Program is heavily **real estate-dependent**, limiting diversification  

If your sole goal is **fast EU citizenship without relocation**, Greece may not be optimal.
