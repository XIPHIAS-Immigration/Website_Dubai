## Investment Migration Pathways — 4 Different Routes (Know What You’re Applying For)

Many people search for investment migration companies in India without knowing which pathway applies to them. These are not interchangeable.

### 1. Residency by Investment Programs

- Residency status through a qualifying investment  
- Renewal and stay rules apply  
- Often linked to real estate or funds  

### 2. Citizenship by Investment Programs

- Direct citizenship through government programs  
- Strict due diligence  
- High documentation scrutiny  

### 3. Investor Visa Pathways

- Business or capital investment routes  
- Requires business intent or expansion  
- Performance conditions may apply  

### 4. Strategic Business Relocation Programs

- Company setup + investment + residency  
- Requires structured planning  

A qualified investor visa consultant in India should map your profile to the right category, not push a trending program.
