### Overview
Nauru’s **Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program** provides a fast, secure path to a Nauruan passport. Citizenship is **lifetime, inheritable, and does not require residency**. Applicants gain **visa-free travel to 86 countries**, including Singapore, Hong Kong (SAR China), UAE, and the United Kingdom. The program is family-friendly, allowing inclusion of **spouse, children, siblings, and parents/grandparents**.  

### Investment Overview
The program offers a **single investment option via a one-time donation**. Minimum amounts start from **USD 105,000 for a single applicant**, with higher contributions for families. This **non-refundable donation route** is simple, government-backed, and allows full family inclusion. Typical processing time is **3–4 months**, subject to due diligence.  

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Applicant must be **18 years or older**.  
- Must have a **clean criminal record**.  
- Provide **verifiable proof of legal source of funds**.  
- Eligible family members include **spouse, children under 30, siblings, and parents/grandparents over 55**.

### How the Investment Route Works
1. **Consult with XIPHIAS** to determine eligibility.  
2. Prepare and **submit required documentation**.  
3. Complete **due diligence** for all adult applicants.  
4. **Make the donation** based on family size.  
5. Take the **oath of allegiance** and receive the **Nauru passport**.  

### Investment Risks and Compliance
- The **donation is non-refundable**.  
- Processing may experience delays due to **enhanced due diligence**.  
- **KYC verification and citizenship interview** are required for all adult applicants.  

### Why Nauru
- **Safe, politically stable, and tax-friendly** Pacific nation.  
- **No residency requirement**; dual citizenship allowed.  
- Family-friendly: include **spouse, children, parents, and siblings**.  
- **Quick and straightforward** citizenship processing.  

### FAQ
- **How long does the process take?** 3–4 months.  
- **Can I include family?** Yes, spouse, children under 30, siblings, and parents/grandparents over 55.  
- **What is the minimum investment?** USD 105,000 for a single applicant.  
- **Are there taxes on citizenship?** No taxes are applied.  
- **Is an interview required?** Yes, for all principal applicants.  
- **Can I hold dual citizenship?** Yes.
