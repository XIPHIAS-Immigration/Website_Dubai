### Overview
The Sustainable Island State Contribution (SISC) is the fastest and most straightforward route to obtain St. Kitts & Nevis citizenship. Established in 1984, the program allows investors to make a non-refundable donation to the government, with thorough due diligence and a typical processing timeline of 3–6 months. Citizenship is lifetime, inheritable, and includes family members.

### Investment Overview
The SISC route requires a minimum contribution of USD 250,000 for a family of up to four. Additional fees apply for extra dependents: USD 50,000 per additional adult (18+) and USD 25,000 per additional minor (&lt;18). This route does not involve property ownership or business investment, making it simple and predictable for applicants seeking a donation-based pathway.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Clean criminal record and no sanctions or watchlist restrictions  
- Verified source of funds for the contribution  
- Ability to provide all KYC and supporting documents  
- Willingness to attend personal interview and complete due diligence  

### Featured Real Estate Projects
While the SISC route does not require real estate investment, applicants interested in combining investment with lifestyle opportunities can explore government-approved real estate developments separately for future planning or diversification.

### How the Investment Route Works
1. Engage a licensed agent to assess eligibility and conduct initial due diligence.  
2. Collect and submit all required documents, including proof of identity, source of funds, and civil status certificates.  
3. Attend a personal interview and complete background verification.  
4. Receive Approval in Principle (AIP) from the Citizenship by Investment Unit.  
5. Make the SISC contribution and submit proof of payment.  
6. Take the oath and receive your St. Kitts & Nevis passport.

### Comparison of SISC, PBO, and Real Estate Routes
The St. Kitts & Nevis CBI program offers three main investment pathways. While SISC and PBO are **donation-based**, the Real Estate option is **asset-backed**, providing a tangible investment with resale opportunities. All routes provide **fast processing (3–6 months), family inclusion, and visa-free travel to over 150 countries**.

### Investment Risks and Compliance
- Applications with unclear or unverifiable funds may be rejected.  
- Non-compliance with document submission, KYC, or due diligence procedures can delay or invalidate the application.  
- All applicants undergo AML, CTF, and CIU regulatory checks to ensure full compliance with local and international law.

- Fast, reliable, and globally recognized CBI program  
- No residency requirements during or after application  
- Visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to ~154 countries  
- Tax advantages on inheritance/capital gains/worldwide income  
- Politically stable, safe, and family-friendly environment  

### FAQ
**Q:** Can I include family members?  
**A:** Yes. The SISC contribution covers the main applicant, spouse, children under 26, and qualifying parents/grandparents (55+).  

**Q:** How long does the process take?  
**A:** Typically 3–6 months, depending on due diligence and interview schedules.  

**Q:** Is there a residency requirement?  
**A:** No, applicants are not required to reside in St. Kitts & Nevis.  

**Q:** What are the fees involved?  
**A:** Fees include the SISC contribution, due diligence, application, and passport issuance, varying by family size and ages.
