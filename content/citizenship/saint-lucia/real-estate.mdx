### Overview
The Real Estate route allows investors to acquire property in **CIU-approved developments or designated private homes** in Saint Lucia. Applicants hold the property for a **minimum of 5 years**, while obtaining citizenship for themselves and eligible dependents. This route is ideal for families seeking **asset-backed investment and global mobility**.

### Investment Overview
- Approved Development Shares: **USD 300,000** minimum for main applicant + dependents  
- Private Homes: **USD 600,000** minimum for main applicant + dependents  
- Government/Processing Fees: Main applicant USD 25,000; Spouse USD 15,000; Per child USD 10,000  
- Due Diligence Fees: Main applicant USD 8,000; Each dependent 16+ USD 5,000  
- Passport Issuance Fee: USD 350 per person  
- **Holding Period:** Minimum 5 years required to maintain eligibility  

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Do you have capital for USD 300,000–600,000 plus fees?  
- Are you comfortable with the required 5-year holding period?  
- Can you provide complete KYC/AML documents (IDs, proof of funds, police certificates)?  
- Are you aware of post-approval fees (government, processing, passport issuance)?  

### Featured Real Estate Projects
- **Harborview Suites (Condo/Share Units):** Minimum USD 300,000, 5-year holding period  
- **Coral Bay Residences (Single-Family Homes):** Minimum USD 600,000, 5-year holding period  

### How the Investment Route Works
1. Engage XIPHIAS for consultation and eligibility screening  
2. Select an approved property and prepare all required documents  
3. Submit application to the Citizenship by Investment Unit (CIU)  
4. Complete due diligence and personal interview  
5. Make the property investment payment  
6. Receive the citizenship certificate and apply for passports for all approved applicants

### Investment Risks and Compliance
- Market-dependent; resale or rental yields are **not guaranteed**  
- Full compliance with CIU, nationality, and sanctions regulations is **mandatory**  
- Multi-layer due diligence applies to all applicants and dependents  

### Why Saint Lucia
- Visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to **145+ countries** (incl. UK, Schengen Area, Hong Kong, Singapore)  
- No residency requirement before or after citizenship  
- Stable economy and strong governance  
- Family-friendly: spouse, children, parents, and siblings can be included  

### Why Choose This Route
- Asset-backed investment with potential rental or resale value  
- Single application covers main applicant and family members  
- End-to-end support from XIPHIAS — application to passport issuance  
- Supports Saint Lucia’s national development projects
