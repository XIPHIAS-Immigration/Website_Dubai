### Overview
The National Economic Fund (NEF) route provides a **direct and secure pathway to Saint Lucia citizenship** through a one-time, non-refundable government contribution. Applicants and eligible dependents can obtain citizenship **without any residency requirement**, making it ideal for families and investors seeking global mobility.

### Investment Overview
- **Minimum NEF Contribution**: USD 240,000 for the main applicant and up to three dependents  
- **Additional Fees**: Due diligence (USD 8,000 for main applicant; USD 5,000 per dependent) and application processing (USD 2,000 per person)  
- **Add-ons**: Spouse, additional dependents under/over 18, and newborns (≤12 months)  

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Clean background & no sanctions for all adult applicants  
- Budget for minimum NEF contribution and associated fees  
- Ability to supply full KYC and supporting documents (IDs, proof of funds, civil status, police certificates)  

### Featured Real Estate Projects
*Not applicable — NEF is a donation-based program.*

### How the Investment Route Works
1. Engage an authorised agent for consultation and document preparation  
2. Submit NEF application to the Citizenship by Investment Unit (CIU)  
3. Complete multi-layer due diligence and personal interview  
4. Receive Approval-in-Principle from CIU  
5. Make the NEF contribution to secure citizenship  
6. Obtain the certificate of citizenship and apply for passports

### Investment Risks and Compliance
- NEF contribution is **non-refundable**  
- Applicants must comply with nationality, sanctions, and CIU regulations  
- Multi-layer due diligence is mandatory for all applicants and dependents  

### Why Saint Lucia
- Visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to **145+ countries** (incl. UK, Schengen Area, Singapore, Hong Kong)  
- No residency requirement before or after citizenship  
- Stable economy and strong governance  
- Family-friendly program covering spouse, children, parents, and siblings
