### Overview
The **Turkey Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program** provides a **fast and secure route to Turkish citizenship**, allowing applicants to obtain a **Turkish passport in as little as 6 months**. Investors can choose from **multiple investment options**, including **real estate, business, bank deposits, government bonds, and funds**. Citizenship is **lifetime, inheritable, and allows dual nationality**, with **no prior residency or language requirements**. Families, including **spouse, children under 18, and parents**, can be included under a single application.

### Investment Overview
- **Real Estate Investment:** Minimum **USD 400,000** in government-approved property. Family inclusion applies. Potential rental income of 3–7% annually.  
- **Bank Deposit / Government Bonds:** Minimum **USD 500,000** in deposits or bonds. Low-risk, government-backed option.  
- **Venture Capital / Fund Investment:** Minimum **USD 500,000** in approved funds or venture capital projects. Potential high-growth returns.  
- **Private Pension Plan Investment:** Minimum **USD 500,000** for at least three years. Long-term stable investment.  
- **Job Creation / Employment Investment:** Creation of at least 50 jobs in Turkey. Citizenship includes applicant and family members.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- **Capital & Fees Ready:** Applicants must have sufficient funds for investment, due diligence, passport, and legal fees.  
- **Clean Background & KYC:** All adult applicants must have a clean criminal record and pass thorough due diligence.  
- **Interview Readiness:** Applicants should be comfortable with remote or in-person interviews if requested.  
- **Family Inclusion:** Spouse, children under 18, and parents can be included in the application.

### Featured Real Estate Projects
- **Istanbul Luxury Apartments:** Government-approved residential units with rental pool options.  
- **Bosphorus View Condos:** Premium condos with high resale potential and legal security.  
- **Antalya Resort Villas:** Holiday homes in prime locations with full property title rights.  

### How the Investment Route Works
1. **Initial Consultation:** Schedule a consultation with XIPHIAS to determine eligibility and the optimal investment path.  
2. **Eligibility & Pre-Screen:** Complete pre-screening and submit initial documentation for due diligence.  
3. **Application Submission:** Submit your Citizenship by Investment application to Turkish authorities.  
4. **Due Diligence:** Undergo enhanced background and financial checks for all adult applicants.  
5. **Investment Completion & Citizenship Issuance:** Complete the property purchase or selected investment, register ownership if applicable, and receive the Turkish passport upon government approval.

- Investments are subject to **market and property risks**; returns are not guaranteed.  
- Non-compliance with Turkish CBI rules or incomplete documentation may lead to disqualification.  
- Enhanced due diligence is mandatory for all adult applicants.

### FAQ
**Q:** What is the minimum investment for Turkey real estate citizenship?  
**A:** USD 400,000 for a single applicant. Family-inclusive applications do not require a higher minimum.  

**Q:** Can family members be included?  
**A:** Yes. The program covers **spouse, children under 18, and parents**.  

**Q:** How fast is the process?  
**A:** The program typically takes **around 6 months** from application to passport issuance.  

**Q:** Are there taxes on citizenship?  
**A:** Turkey does not impose special taxes for citizenship. Regular property or income taxes may apply depending on investment type.  

**Q:** Do I need to reside in Turkey?  
**A:** No prior residency is required.
