### Overview
The Turkey Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program provides a fast and secure path to a Turkish passport. Applicants invest in government bonds, include eligible family members, and gain visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to 113 countries. Citizenship is lifetime and inheritable, with no prior residency or language requirements.

### Investment Overview
Invest a minimum of USD 500,000 in Turkish government bonds for three years. Due diligence, processing, and passport fees apply per applicant. The process typically completes in around 6 months, offering a fast and reliable CBI route. The program allows inclusion of spouse, children under 18, and parents.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Be at least 18 years old
- Clean criminal record and KYC documentation ready
- Capital for investment + fees available (minimum USD 500,000)
- Optional family inclusions: spouse, children under 18, parents

### Featured Real Estate Projects
- Not applicable for this route; investment is in government bonds
- Provides government-backed security and stable returns

### How the Investment Route Works
1. Engage XIPHIAS to assess eligibility and guide investment
2. Submit application and complete due diligence
3. Make the government bonds investment for three years
4. Receive citizenship certificate and apply for Turkish passports

### FAQ
**Q:** What is the minimum investment for Turkey government bonds citizenship?  
**A:** USD 500,000 for a single applicant, held in government bonds for a minimum of three years.

**Q:** Can family members be included?  
**A:** Yes. The program covers your spouse, children under 18, and parents as eligible dependents.

**Q:** How fast is the process?  
**A:** Around 6 months from application to passport issuance.

**Q:** Are there taxes on citizenship?  
**A:** Turkey does not impose special taxes for citizenship, but normal banking and interest income taxes may apply.

**Q:** Do I need to reside in Turkey?  
**A:** No prior residency is required.
