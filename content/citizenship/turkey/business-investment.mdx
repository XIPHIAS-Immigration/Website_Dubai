### Overview
The Turkey Citizenship by Investment program provides a fast and secure path to Turkish citizenship. Through business investment, applicants can obtain a lifetime, inheritable passport with visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to 113 countries. This route is family-friendly, covering spouse, children under 18, and parents. Processing typically takes around 6 months, making it one of the fastest CBI programs globally. Applicants do not need prior residency or language skills.

### Investment Overview
Applicants can invest a minimum of USD 500,000 in a Turkish business to qualify for citizenship. Family members are included under one application: spouse, children under 18, and parents. No prior residency is required. Due diligence, government, and passport fees apply per applicant. Other investment options in Turkey include real estate, bank deposits, government bonds, and fund investments, each offering similar family inclusion and processing timelines.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Clean criminal record: All adult applicants must have no criminal history and pass enhanced due diligence.
- Sufficient funds: Capital for business investment plus associated fees (due diligence, passport, legal) must be available.
- KYC documentation readiness: Valid identification, proof of funds, civil status documents, police clearance, and medical certificates must be prepared.
- Family inclusions: Spouse, children under 18, and parents can be included.

### Featured Investment Opportunities
- Private business ventures: Direct equity investment or capital contribution in a registered Turkish business.
- Employment-based investments: Businesses that meet job creation criteria under CBIU regulations.
- Key features: legally compliant, government-regulated, and potential for growth and profitability in dynamic sectors.

### How the Investment Route Works
1. Initial Consultation: Engage XIPHIAS to assess eligibility and identify suitable business investments.
2. Pre-Screen & Documentation: Submit personal and financial documents for thorough due diligence.
3. Application Submission: Authorized agent files the CBI application with Turkish authorities.
4. Investment & Compliance: Complete the required business investment and demonstrate compliance with regulatory requirements.
5. Citizenship Approval & Passport Issuance: Upon approval, take the oath and receive Turkish passports for all eligible family members.

### FAQ
Q: What is the minimum investment for Turkey business citizenship?  
A: USD 500,000 for a single applicant; the same minimum applies regardless of family size.

Q: Can family members be included?  
A: Yes, the program covers spouse, children under 18, and parents as dependents.

Q: How fast is the process?  
A: Typically around 6 months from application to passport issuance.

Q: Do I need to reside in Turkey?  
A: No prior residency is required.

Q: Are there taxes on citizenship?  
A: Turkey does not impose special taxes for citizenship, but standard business or income taxes may apply depending on your investment.
