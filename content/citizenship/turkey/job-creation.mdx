### Overview
The **Turkey Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program** allows applicants to obtain citizenship by **creating employment opportunities** in Turkey. The **Job Creation route** requires applicants to generate **at least 50 full-time jobs**. Citizenship is **lifetime, inheritable, and allows dual nationality**, with **no residency requirements**, and includes **spouse, children under 18, and parents**.

### Investment Overview
- **Job Creation / Employment Investment:** Create at least 50 full-time jobs in a registered Turkish business. Family members are included.  
- **Other CBI Options:** Real estate, bank deposits, government bonds, funds, private pensions—each with specific capital investments.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- **Capacity & Readiness:** Ability to create minimum 50 jobs with required operational plan and financial capacity.  
- **Clean Background & KYC:** All adult applicants must have a clean record and pass due diligence.  
- **Interview Readiness:** Comfortable with remote or in-person interviews.  
- **Family Inclusion:** Spouse, children under 18, and parents can be included.

### How the Investment Route Works
1. **Initial Consultation:** Schedule a consultation with XIPHIAS to discuss eligibility and optimal job creation plan.  
2. **Eligibility & Pre-Screen:** Submit business plan, financials, and initial documentation for due diligence.  
3. **Application Submission:** Submit Citizenship by Investment application to Turkish authorities.  
4. **Due Diligence:** Complete background, financial, and business plan checks.  
5. **Job Creation & Citizenship Issuance:** Implement job creation plan. Upon verification and approval, receive Turkish citizenship and passports.

### Investment Risks and Compliance
- Ensure lawful and verifiable source of funds for business operations.  
- Compliance with labor laws and job creation verification is mandatory.  
- Non-compliance or failure to meet job creation targets may result in disqualification.  
- Enhanced due diligence is required for all adult applicants.

### Why Turkey

- **Fast processing**: citizenship can be obtained in ~6 months.  
- **Dual nationality** allowed; citizenship is **lifetime and inheritable**.  
- **Visa-free access to 113 countries**.  
- Contributes to **social and economic development** of Turkey.
