### Overview
The Turkey Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program offers a secure and fast path to a Turkish passport. Investors can make a fixed-term bank deposit to qualify, with processing typically completed in around 6 months. Eligible family members—including spouse, children under 18, and parents—can be included. Citizenship provides dual nationality, visa-free travel to 113 countries, and is lifetime and inheritable, with no prior residency or language requirements.

### Investment Overview
Turkey offers several CBI routes, including:  
- Bank Deposit: Minimum USD 500,000 for a 3-year term; family members included.  
- Real Estate Investment: Minimum USD 400,000 in government-approved property.  
- Government Bonds / Fund Investment: Minimum USD 500,000; includes spouse, children under 18, and parents.  
- Business Investment / Fixed Capital: Minimum USD 500,000; family included.  
All options require due diligence and passport fees per applicant.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Applicant must have a clean criminal record.  
- Capital for bank deposit and associated fees must be available.  
- KYC documents ready for all adult applicants.  
- Optional family inclusions: spouse, children under 18, and parents.

### How the Investment Route Works
1. Initial Consultation: Engage XIPHIAS to determine eligibility and optimal CBI route.  
2. Eligibility & Pre-Screen: Complete pre-screening and submit documentation.  
3. Application Submission: XIPHIAS submits the CBI application to Turkish authorities.  
4. Due Diligence: Background and financial checks conducted on all adult applicants.  
5. Bank Deposit & Citizenship Issuance: Make the 3-year bank deposit, receive government approval, take the oath, and obtain passports for all eligible applicants.

- All transactions must comply with Turkish government regulations and banking laws.
