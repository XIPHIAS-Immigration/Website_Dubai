### Overview
The Turkey Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program provides a fast and secure path to a Turkish passport. Applicants can invest in **government-approved venture capital or real estate funds**, include eligible family members, and gain **visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to 113 countries**. Citizenship is **lifetime and inheritable**, with **no prior residency or language requirements**.

### Investment Overview
Turkey offers four main CBI investment options:

- **Real Estate:** Minimum USD 400,000; includes spouse, children under 18, and parents  
- **Bank Deposit:** Minimum USD 500,000; includes spouse, children under 18, and parents  
- **Government Bonds:** Minimum USD 500,000; includes spouse, children under 18, and parents  
- **Fund Investment:** Minimum USD 500,000; includes spouse, children under 18, and parents

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Be at least 18 years old  
- Clean criminal record and pass due diligence screenings  
- Sufficient capital to make the fund investment and cover all fees  
- KYC documentation ready (passport copies, birth/marriage certificates, bank statements)  
- Family inclusions: spouse, children under 18, parents

### How the Investment Route Works
1. Engage an authorized agent (e.g., XIPHIAS)  
2. Submit application and complete enhanced due diligence  
3. Make the fund investment for three years  
4. Receive citizenship certificate and apply for passports

- Enhanced due diligence is required for all adult applicants
