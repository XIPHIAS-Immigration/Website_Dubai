### Overview
Vanuatu’s Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program offers a **fast, secure, and family-friendly path** to a second passport through a **one-time donation to the Vanuatu Development Support Program (VDSP)**. Citizenship is **lifetime and inheritable**, requires **no residency or language tests**, and provides **visa-free access to ~117 countries**. Typical processing is **1–3 months**.

### Investment Overview
- **VDSP Donation Route:**  
  - Single applicant: **USD 130,000**  
  - Married couple: **USD 150,000**  
  - Three-person family: **USD 165,000**  
  - Family of four: **USD 180,000**  
  - Additional family member: **USD 10,000** each  
  - Eligible dependents: spouse, children under 25, parents 65+  
  - Due diligence, processing, and passport fees apply per person

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Age **18+**  
- **Clean criminal record**  
- **Funds available** for donation and fees  
- **KYC / due diligence** documentation ready  
- Optional family inclusions: spouse, children under 25, parents 65+

### How the Investment Route Works
1. **Initial Consultation:** Confirm eligibility and route.  
2. **Pre-Screen & Docs:** Submit documentation for due diligence.  
3. **Application Submission:** Filed with the Vanuatu Citizenship Office.  
4. **Due Diligence:** Enhanced checks on all adult applicants.  
5. **Donation & Issuance:** Pay the donation, take the oath, receive passports.

### Investment Risks and Compliance
- Use **lawful funds** and satisfy due diligence.  
- Donation is **non-refundable**.  
- Disqualification: criminal history, illegal funds, or failed due diligence.

### Why Vanuatu
- **1–3 month** processing  
- **Tax-friendly:** no income, wealth, or inheritance taxes  
- **Family-friendly** inclusions (spouse, children under 25, parents 65+)  
- **Stable** governance; UN & Commonwealth member  
- **Visa-free** access to ~117 countries

### FAQ
**Q:** Minimum investment?  
**A:** **USD 130,000** for a single applicant (higher for families).

**Q:** Can I include family?  
**A:** Yes — spouse, children under 25, and parents 65+.

**Q:** How fast?  
**A:** Typically **1–3 months** to approval.

**Q:** Taxes?  
**A:** Vanuatu has no income, wealth, or inheritance taxes.

**Q:** Residency required?  
**A:** **No**.
