### Overview
São Tomé & Príncipe offers a fast and affordable Citizenship by Investment program. Investors can obtain citizenship through a non-refundable contribution to the National Transformation Fund (NTF) or by investing in approved real estate projects. Citizenship is typically granted within 6 weeks, with no residency requirement, allowing for lifetime inheritance and dual nationality.

### Investment Overview
Applicants can choose between:
- **National Transformation Fund (NTF)**: Minimum non-refundable contribution of USD 90,000 for a single applicant. Family contributions vary based on number of dependents.
- **Approved Real Estate**: Minimum investment starts at USD 325,000 for development shares or condominiums, and USD 600,000 for single-family homes. All investments must be in CIU-approved projects.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Clean criminal record and no sanctions or watchlist restrictions.  
- Ability to make the required investment and cover associated fees.  
- Willingness to provide full KYC documentation for all dependents.  
- Optional inclusion of spouse, children under 30, parents/grandparents over 55, and siblings.  

### Featured Real Estate Projects
- CIU-approved developments with robust legal and governance frameworks.  
- Premium private homes in São Tomé & Príncipe’s most sought-after locations.  
- Investment opportunities with resale potential after the mandatory hold period.

### How the Investment Route Works
1. Engage an authorised agent to initiate your application.  
2. Submit documentation for due diligence and verification.  
3. Make the required contribution to NTF or purchase approved real estate.  
4. Receive citizenship certificate and apply for passports for all approved family members.

### Investment Risks and Compliance
- Ensure funds come from lawful sources to pass government due diligence.  
- Adverse background or failure to meet compliance requirements can lead to disqualification.  
- Real estate investments carry standard market and liquidity risks; mandatory hold periods apply.

### Why São Tomé & Príncipe
- Fast processing within 6 weeks.  
- Lifetime, inheritable citizenship.  
- Dual nationality allowed with visa-free travel to 60+ countries.  
- Stable governance and favorable tax environment.

### FAQ
**Q:** How long does the NTF citizenship process take?  
**A:** Citizenship is typically granted within 6 weeks, provided all documentation is complete and due diligence checks are cleared.

**Q:** Who can be included in my NTF application?  
**A:** Eligible dependents include your spouse, children under 30, parents and grandparents over 55, and siblings.

**Q:** Is there a residency requirement?  
**A:** No. There is no minimum stay requirement; you can maintain citizenship without living in the country.

**Q:** What documents are required for the NTF application?  
**A:** Proof of identity, address, police clearance, medical/health certificate, passport photos, and evidence of lawful source of funds.

**Q:** Is the NTF contribution refundable?  
**A:** No. The contribution is non-refundable and made to the National Transformation Fund.

### Why Choose This Route
- Simplified and direct path to citizenship.  
- Family-friendly inclusions without additional residency requirements.  
- Transparent government and due diligence processes.  
- Multiple investment options to suit different budgets and goals.

### Indicative Budget
- **NTF Contribution:** USD 90,000+ for single applicants. Family packages up to USD 95,000.  
- **Real Estate Investment:** From USD 325,000 for developments or condos, USD 600,000 for single-family homes.  
- **Government Fees:** Main applicant USD 25,000; spouse USD 15,000; each child USD 10,000.  
- **Due Diligence Fees:** Main applicant USD 10,000; each dependent 16+ USD 7,500.  
- **Application & Passport Fees:** USD 250 per person (application); USD 350 per person (passport issuance).
