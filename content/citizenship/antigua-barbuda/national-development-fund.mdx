### Overview
The National Development Fund (NDF) donation route is Antigua & Barbuda’s most direct and streamlined path to citizenship. Applicants make a one-time contribution to the government-approved fund, with eligible family members included in a single application.

### Investment Overview
Contribute USD 230,000 for a family of up to four, or USD 245,000 for families of five or more. Additional costs include **due diligence, government processing, and passport fees**, scaled according to family size. No ongoing residence is required, except for a **five-day visit within the first five years**.

Cash flow and exit
- The donation is **non-refundable**; no capital return or income is generated.
- There is no exit strategy; the route is purely philanthropic and designed for citizenship acquisition.

Safeguards and compliance
- All applications must go through **CIU-approved channels** with full **proof of funds documentation**.
- Adult applicants undergo **enhanced due diligence**, including background checks, source-of-funds verification, and remote interviews.

Indicative budgeting
Applicants should plan **USD 250,000+** to cover the NDF contribution, due diligence, government, and passport fees for a single applicant. Family size increases total costs proportionally.
