### Overview
The **Business Investment** route grants Egypt citizenship through a **local business investment**. Applicants invest USD 100,000 to the state treasury and USD 350,000 in a new or existing Egyptian business. This option suits entrepreneurs seeking to expand operations in Egypt or the MENA region. Processing typically takes **6–9 months**, subject to due diligence.

### Investment Overview
- **Minimum entry:** USD 450,000 (100k state + 350k business).  
- **Family inclusion:** Spouse and children under 21 can be included.  
- **Cash flow & exit:** Returns depend on business performance; exit governed by business regulations.  
- **Compliance:** All funds must be transferred via approved channels; full source-of-funds verification required.

### Why Egypt
- **Direct path to citizenship** — subject to Ministry approval and due diligence.  
- **No ongoing residence requirement** — only a **short visit during onboarding** may be requested.  
- **Investment flexibility** — choose between business, real estate, or donation routes.  
- **Family coverage** — include eligible dependants in a single application.  
- **MENA regional base** — strategic location, business opportunities, and connectivity.  

> *Note:* Business rules and third-country visa policies can change. Confirm current terms during onboarding.
