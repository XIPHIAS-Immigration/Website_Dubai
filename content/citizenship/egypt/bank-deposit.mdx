### Overview
The **Bank Deposit Route** allows investors to secure Egyptian citizenship by depositing USD 500,000 into an approved government bank for three years. This path is simple, secure, and suitable for investors seeking a low-maintenance route while including their spouse and children under 21.

### Investment Overview
- **Minimum deposit:** USD 500,000 in an approved Egyptian government bank.  
- **Holding period:** 36 months; principal is returned in local currency after completion.  
- **Processing time:** Typically 6–9 months, subject to due diligence.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Clean criminal record and no sanctions for all adult applicants.  
- Ready to deposit USD 500,000 plus associated fees.  
- Can provide full KYC and supporting documents.

### How the Bank Deposit Route Works
1. Confirm eligibility with XIPHIAS.  
2. Prepare and submit all required documentation.  
3. Undergo due diligence and remote interview.  
4. Deposit USD 500,000 in an approved government bank.  
5. Receive official Egyptian citizenship and passport.

### Investment Risks and Compliance
- Principal is returned after 3 years in local currency; no interest accrual.  
- All adult applicants undergo multi-layer due diligence and remote interview.  
- Compliance with Egyptian nationality, legal, and CBI regulations is mandatory.

### FAQ
- **What is the minimum deposit for Egypt CBI?**  
  USD 500,000 in an approved government bank for a single applicant; thresholds increase for family inclusion.
- **Can family members be included?**  
  Yes, spouse and children under 21 can be included. Fees vary depending on number of dependents.
- **How long does the Bank Deposit CBI process take?**  
  Typically 6–9 months, depending on due diligence and document completeness.
- **Is residency required for the Bank Deposit route?**  
  No residency is required before or after citizenship.
- **Is the deposit refundable?**  
  Yes, principal is returned after 3 years in local currency; no interest.
- **What due diligence is required?**  
  All adult applicants undergo multi-layer due diligence including background checks, proof of funds, and a remote interview.
- **Are there additional fees apart from the deposit?**  
  Yes, due diligence fees, government processing fees, and passport issuance fees apply per CBI rules.
