### Overview
The Egypt Donation Citizenship by Investment (CBI) route provides a **fast, secure, and non-refundable pathway to Egyptian citizenship**. Applicants make a **contribution to the state treasury**, include eligible family members under a single application, and gain **visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to 51 countries**. The process is **transparent, compliant, and efficient**, offering lifetime citizenship with no residency requirement.

### Investment Overview
Applicants can obtain citizenship through a **non-refundable donation starting from USD 250,000** for a single applicant. Contribution thresholds increase for family inclusion. **Government processing, due diligence, and passport issuance fees** are applied per Egyptian CBI rules. This option is ideal for investors seeking **speed and simplicity** without property ownership.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Clean criminal record and no sanctions for all adult applicants.  
- Ready for minimum donation (USD 250,000) plus associated fees.  
- Can provide full KYC and supporting documents.  

### How the Donation Route Works
1. Decide on the donation amount based on family size.  
2. Prepare and submit your application with supporting documents.  
3. Undergo due diligence and remote interview.  
4. Make the non-refundable contribution to the state treasury.  
5. Receive the official citizenship decree and Egyptian passport.

### FAQ
- **What is the minimum donation for Egypt CBI?**  
  A non-refundable contribution starting at **USD 250,000 for a single applicant** is required. Thresholds increase for family inclusion.  

- **Can family members be included?**  
  Yes, spouse and children under 21 can be included. Fees vary depending on number and age of dependents.  

- **How long does the Donation CBI process take?**  
  Typically 6–9 months, subject to document completeness and successful due diligence.  

- **Is residency required for the donation route?**  
  No. Egypt does not require physical residence before or after citizenship is granted.  

- **Is the donation refundable?**  
  No. The contribution is fully non-refundable.  

- **What due diligence is required?**  
  All adult applicants must undergo **multi-layer due diligence**, including background checks, proof of funds, and a personal interview.  

- **Are there additional fees apart from the donation?**  
  Yes. Applicants must also pay **government processing fees, due diligence fees, and passport issuance fees** per Egyptian CBI regulations.
