## About Dominica
Dominica is one of the Caribbean’s most stable and trusted destinations for Citizenship by Investment (CBI). The country offers a strong due diligence framework and reliable processing timelines, enabling investors and their families to obtain full citizenship, a Dominican passport, and visa-free access to 145 countries. Dominica provides lifetime citizenship with family inclusion, global mobility, and a secure pathway to a second passport.
