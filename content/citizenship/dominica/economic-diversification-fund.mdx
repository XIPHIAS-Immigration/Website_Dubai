# Dominica Economic Diversification Fund (EDF) Citizenship by Investment (CBI) Program

## Overview
The  Dominica Economic Diversification Fund (EDF)  Citizenship by Investment (CBI) route offers a straightforward and efficient pathway to  full citizenship  in the Commonwealth of Dominica. By making a  non-refundable donation  to the national development fund, applicants gain  visa-free access  to over 140 countries, including the  Schengen Area , the  UK , and  Hong Kong . This route provides  lifetime citizenship  for the investor and eligible family members, with no residency requirements.

The process is  fast ,  transparent , and fully compliant with  CBIU regulations , making it a preferred option for those seeking  second citizenship  and global mobility.

## Investment Overview
Applicants must make a  non-refundable contribution  to the  Economic Diversification Fund (EDF) , with a minimum donation of  USD 100,000  for a single applicant. Donations increase based on family size, ensuring  family inclusion  with minimal additional costs.

### Key Benefits:
-  Visa-free travel  to over 140 countries.
-  Non-refundable donation  supporting national development.
-  No residency requirements  before or after citizenship is granted.
-  Family-friendly : Includes spouse, dependent children, and parents in a single application.

 Note : All fees, including government processing, due diligence, and passport fees, apply per CBIU regulations.

## Quick Eligibility Check
To be eligible for the Dominica EDF CBI route, applicants must:
-  Have a clean background , with no sanctions or criminal records.
- Be ready to make the required  donation  and pay associated fees.
- Provide  full KYC documentation  and supporting documents.

## How the EDF CBI Route Works
1.  Choose the EDF Route : Select the  Economic Diversification Fund  and submit your application with the necessary documents.
2.  Undergo Due Diligence : All adult applicants and dependents aged 16+ undergo a  comprehensive background check .
3.  Make the Donation : Complete the  non-refundable donation  to the EDF to contribute to Dominica's development.
4.  Certificate of Naturalization : Upon successful due diligence, receive the  Certificate of Naturalization .
5.  Apply for Passports : Submit your passport application and take the  Oath of Allegiance .

### Comparison with EDF Contribution
Unlike the Economic Diversification Fund (EDF) route, which is a non-refundable donation, the real estate option is  asset-backed , offering a tangible investment with resale opportunities subject to CIU regulations. Both routes provide  fast processing (3–6 months), full family inclusion, and visa-free travel to over 140 countries .

## Investment Risks and Compliance
-  Non-refundable donation : The  EDF donation  is non-refundable and cannot be recovered.
-  Due diligence : All applicants and dependents aged 16+ must undergo a multi-layered due diligence process, including  background checks ,  financial verification , and a  personal interview .
-  Government compliance : Full adherence to  Dominican nationality laws ,  legal regulations , and  CBIU compliance  is mandatory.

## Why Dominica’s EDF Route?
Dominica’s  CBI program  is one of the Caribbean’s oldest and most trusted, providing applicants with:
-  Lifetime citizenship , which extends to the applicant’s family (spouse, children, and parents).
-  Visa-free access  to over 140 countries, including popular destinations such as the  Schengen Area ,  UK , and  Hong Kong .

## FAQ

### What is the minimum donation for Dominica CBI via EDF?
Applicants must donate a minimum of  USD 100,000  for a single applicant. Donations increase depending on the number of family members included.

### Can I include my family in the EDF application?
Yes, the EDF route allows you to include  spouse, dependent children, and parents . Additional fees apply for each family member.

### How long does the Dominica EDF CBI process take?
The processing time is typically  3–6 months , subject to document completeness and successful due diligence checks.

### Are there residency requirements for the EDF route?
No, there are no residency requirements for  Dominica’s EDF Citizenship by Investment program .

### Is the EDF contribution refundable?
No, the contribution to the  Economic Diversification Fund  is  non-refundable  and cannot be returned.

### What due diligence is required?
All adult applicants and dependents aged 16+ must undergo a  comprehensive due diligence process , including background checks, financial verification, and a personal interview.

### Are there additional fees apart from the donation?
Yes, applicants must also pay  government processing fees ,  due diligence fees , and  passport issuance fees , as per CBIU rules.

### Can I resell my EDF donation?
Since the EDF contribution is  non-refundable , it cannot be resold. Unlike the  real estate route , there are no liquidity or resale options with the EDF.
