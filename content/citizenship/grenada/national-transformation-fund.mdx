### Overview
The National Transformation Fund (NTF) route provides a fast and secure path to Grenada citizenship. Investors make a one-time non-refundable contribution to the government-backed fund, with full family inclusion and no residency requirement. This route offers visa-free travel benefits, U.S. E-2 investor visa eligibility, and a streamlined application process.

### Investment Overview
The NTF donation requires a minimum contribution of USD 150,000 for a single applicant. Fees for additional family members apply, covering government processing, due diligence, and passport issuance. The process typically takes 4–6 months, depending on document readiness and due diligence outcomes.

### Quick Eligibility Check
- Applicant must be 18+ with a clean criminal record.
- Lawful source of funds and verifiable financial standing required.
- Good health and basic medical compliance.
- Eligible family members can include spouse, children up to 30, parents over 55, and siblings.

### FAQ
**Are fees flat for families?**  
No. The NTF donation bands differ by family size (e.g., single vs couple/family), and due-diligence/passport fees scale by age and number of dependants.

**Is an interview mandatory?**  
A short virtual interview may be requested for adult applicants as part of enhanced due diligence.

**How long does the NTF process take?**  
Typically 4–6 months for well-prepared applications, depending on document readiness and due diligence outcomes.

**Can I include my parents or siblings?**  
Yes. Eligible dependents include spouse, children up to 30, parents 55+, and siblings. Additional fees apply per dependant.
