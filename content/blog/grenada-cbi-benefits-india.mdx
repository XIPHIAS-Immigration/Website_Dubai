Grenada, a beautiful **Caribbean island nation**, is known for its fast economic growth, political stability, and world-famous spices, earning it the nickname **"Island of Spice."** Its **Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program** offers Indian investors and global high-net-worth individuals a fast-track pathway to **second citizenship**, global mobility, and international business opportunities.

## Why Invest in Grenada Citizenship by Investment?

Grenada CBI provides:

- **No residency requirement**—you don’t need to live in Grenada
- No travel required during the application process
- **Dual citizenship allowed**
- **Visa-free travel** to 141+ countries including the UK, Schengen Area, China, and Brazil
- Eligibility for the **USA E-2 Investor Visa** to start a business in the U.S.
- Inclusion of **dependent children and parents**
- No global income tax
- **Lifelong citizenship** with the ability to pass it to future generations

## Benefits of Grenada Citizenship by Investment

- Legally established and globally recognized program
- Include multiple family members in the application
- Quick and streamlined process
- No interview, language, or prior business experience required
- Flexible investment options for individuals and families

## Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for Grenada CBI:

1. Minimum net worth:
   - **US $200,000** for National Transformation Fund (NTF) contribution
   - **US $350,000** for real estate investment
2. Proof of legitimate source of funds
3. Clean criminal record
4. Good health

## Investment Options

- **National Transformation Fund Donation**: Contribute a minimum of US $200,000 (single applicant).
- **Real Estate Investment**: Invest at least US $350,000 in a government-approved project, held for a minimum of 5 years.

## FAQs

**Who can apply for Grenada CBI?**
High-net-worth individuals and their families meeting investment and eligibility criteria.

**What is the minimum investment?**
$200,000 for the NTF option, or $350,000 for real estate investment.

**How long does the process take?**
Typically 3–6 months from application to passport issuance.

**Can families be included in the application?**
Yes, spouse, dependent children, and parents can be included.
