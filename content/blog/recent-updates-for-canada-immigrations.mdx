## Recent Updates for Canada Immigrations
Canada is fast recovering from the economic crisis it faced during the Corona pandemic. However, there could be several changes brought into the **immigration programs**. In its pursuit to invite the right foreign nationals to contribute to economic development, Canada has introduced multiple updates to its immigration policies.

Here are a few of the latest updates from the **Canada immigration table**:

1. **CRS Points Updates:** IRCC introduced changes in the **Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS)** points on 20th October 2020. Profiles in the **Express Entry pool** will now be auto-updated every 2 weeks.
2. **Draw Cancellation:** This auto-update process resulted in the cancellation of the **Express Entry draw** scheduled for 28th October 2020.
3. **French Language Bonus:** Applicants fluent in **French** or those willing to learn will earn an extra 50 points. Even applicants with limited English proficiency but good French skills will receive an additional 25 points in their CRS score.
