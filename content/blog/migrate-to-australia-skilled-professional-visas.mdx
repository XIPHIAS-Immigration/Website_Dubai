## Visa Class 189 — Skilled Independent (Permanent)
**VISA CLASS 189** or **Skilled Independent Visa** provides one of the best modes to relocate to the country. This visa is for all those **skilled professionals** who would want to relocate to **Australia** in pursuit of better opportunities there. This visa provides the successful immigrant **permanent residency** in **Australia**. A successful immigrant can **live, work and study** anywhere in **Australia**. The immigrant will be eligible for **citizenship** in **Australia** after a period of **4 years**. The successful immigrant can **rent or buy properties** in **Australia**. They have the right to study in any part of **Australia** and any course that they deem would be necessary for their growth.

## Subclass 491 — Skilled Work Regional (Provisional)
Similar to this program is **Australia Subclass 491**. Any of the **immigration** programs require that the applicant’s profile is part of **Australia’s SkillSelect** portal. While submitting the application, the applicant will have to submit an **Expression of Interest (EOI)** in which the applicant will have to mention his/her interest in working in **regional Australia**. Only when this condition is adhered to, then the profile will be considered for further processing for **Subclass 491**. **Australia 491 Visa** points are based on the factors which include **age**, **education**, **adaptability**, **experience** and **English language** efficiency. However, the basic points required to qualify here are **60 against 100** which for **189 visa** it is **65 points against 100**.

### Points Factors (illustrative)
- **Age**
- **Educational qualifications**
- **Skilled work experience**
- **English language proficiency**
- **Adaptability** (e.g., study/work in regional **Australia**)
- **Nomination/Sponsorship** where applicable

## Subclass 190 — Skilled Nominated (Permanent)
The other program that currently is most favored to **migrate to Australia** is **Subclass 190** or **Skilled Nominated Visa** program. **190 Visa** cost is more or less the same as any other visa cost to relocate to **Australia**. However, what makes this program special is, it becomes a lot easier to earn **Australia PR** with the help of a **nomination** earned from either the **state** or an **employer** based in **Australia**.

## SkillSelect Profile Validity & Management
Any of the profiles submitted into the **SkillSelect** portal will remain there for a period of **2 years**. During this period, the applicant will have the access to his/her profile to make any changes required. However, every change made should always be supported by the required documents. If during this period, the profile gets selected and the applicant becomes a successful immigrant, then the profile will automatically cease to exist in the portal. If the applicant does not respond to the calls of the sent invitation for more than 2 times, the application will be removed from the portal.

## FAQs

**What is the difference between Subclass 189, 190, and 491?**
**Subclass 189** is a **Skilled Independent** permanent visa; **Subclass 190** is **Skilled Nominated** (state nomination) permanent visa; **Subclass 491** is **Skilled Work Regional (Provisional)** with pathways to **Australia PR**.

**Do I need an Expression of Interest (EOI) for these visas?**
Yes. You submit an **EOI** through **SkillSelect** and may receive an **invitation** to apply based on your **points** and occupation demand.

**What are the key factors in the points test?**
**Age**, **English** ability, **education**, **skilled work experience**, and **other factors** like **state nomination** or **regional study**.
