## What is Greece Golden Visa? Complete Guide for Indians

If you are an Indian investor or a family exploring overseas residency options, you may have come across the term what is Greece Golden Visa. This guide explains the Greece Golden Visa program in a simple, structured way so that even a first-time reader can fully understand how it works, what it offers, and what it does not.

## What is Greece Golden Visa?

The [Greece Golden Visa](https://xiphiasimmigration.com/residency/greece) is a residency-by-investment program introduced by the Greek government in 2013. It allows non-EU citizens, including Indians, to obtain a Greek residence permit by making a qualifying investment in Greece.  

This program is governed by the Greek Ministry of Migration & Asylum. It provides legal residency status, not citizenship.  

In simple words:  
You invest in Greece  
You get a residence permit  
You and your family can legally stay in Greece and travel in Schengen  

## Minimum Investment Requirement

The minimum investment for the Greece Golden Visa starts from €250,000.

## Who Can Apply for Greece Golden Visa 

Indian citizens are fully eligible to apply for the Greece Golden Visa, provided they meet the programme conditions.

### Basic eligibility criteria for Indians

You can apply if you:

- Are a non-EU / non-EEA citizen (Indians qualify)  
- Are 18 years or older  
- Have a clean criminal record  
- Can make a qualifying investment from legal, verifiable sources  
- Hold valid health insurance covering Greece  
- Can prove the lawful source of funds  

### Family members who can be included

One of the reasons the Greece Golden Visa for Indians is often discussed is family inclusion.  

The main applicant can include:

- Spouse  
- Children under 21 (can usually be extended up to age 24 if studying)  
- Parents of the main applicant  
- Parents of the spouse  

All family members receive residence permits with the same validity as the main applicant.

## Greece Golden Visa Investment Options

Greece offers multiple investment routes under its residency-by-investment framework. These options are defined by Greek law and administered by the Ministry of Migration & Asylum.

### 1. Real estate investment

This is the most commonly used option by Indian applicants.

**Minimum Investment:**  
Starts from €250,000  

You may qualify by:

- Purchasing residential or commercial property in Greece  
- Leasing property under long-term agreements (in specific cases)  
- Investing in properties converted from commercial to residential use (subject to conditions)  

### 2. Capital investment routes

**Minimum Investment:**  
Starts from €250,000+  

Less commonly used but legally available options include:

- Investment in Greek companies  
- Purchase of government bonds  
- Investment funds registered in Greece  
- Bank deposits under specific frameworks  

Most Indian families prefer real estate because it is tangible and easier to understand compared to financial instruments.

## Benefits of Greece Golden Visa for Indians

[The Greece Golden Visa](https://xiphiasimmigration.com/residency/greece) benefits are practical rather than promotional. Understanding them clearly helps set realistic expectations.

### Legal Residency in Greece

You receive a Greek residence permit, allowing you and your family to live in Greece if you choose.

### Schengen Travel

Holders can travel visa-free across the Schengen Area for up to 90 days in any 180-day period, providing easy access to multiple European countries.

### No Minimum Stay Required

Unlike many residency programs, you do not need to live in Greece. You can maintain your residence permit while staying in India or elsewhere.

### Family Inclusion

Your spouse, children, and dependent parents can be included under a single investment, with all family members receiving residence permits.

### Renewable Permit

The 5-year residence permit is renewable as long as the qualifying investment is maintained and programme conditions are met.

### No Language or Education Requirements

There is no Greek language test and no minimum education or work background required. Residency is based purely on the qualifying investment.

## FAQ

**1. What is the Greece Golden Visa?**  
It is a residency-by-investment program allowing Indians to get a Greek residence permit through qualifying investments. It provides residency and Schengen travel but not citizenship automatically.  

**2. Who can apply from India?**  
Indians 18+ with a clean record and legal funds for investment can apply. Family members (spouse, children, parents) can also be included.  

**3. What are the investment options and minimum amounts?**  
Real Estate: from €250,000, varies by location  
Capital Investments: from €250,000+, includes funds, bonds, or company investments  

**4. How long does it take, and how long is it valid?**  
Processing: ~12 months  
Permit validity: 5 years, renewable  
Stay requirement: None  

**5. Can my family travel with me?**  
Yes. The family gets residence permits. Holders can travel visa-free in the Schengen Area (90 days per country).
