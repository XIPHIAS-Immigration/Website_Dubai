## 6 Factors to Consider When Hiring an Immigration Consultant

**1. Track Record and Experience**
When choosing an immigration firm, going with a healthy **track record** and experience in handling different types of cases under various programs is important. A good track record shows that you can rely on **immigration services** for guidance and assistance throughout the immigration process.

**2. Credentials and Expertise**
The immigration consultancy you choose should have all the necessary **credentials** to offer services and assist you in your immigration process. Choosing a **certified immigration consultant** and **licensed immigration consultant** with in-depth knowledge and expertise in various immigration programs such as **investment migration**, **work visa**, and **study visa** can help you get all your immigration assistance in one place.

**3. Fee and Pricing**
Consider the **fees** of different programs depending on the country you choose to immigrate to get a better idea of the immigration firm you choose. This ensures that you get the best service and assistance that fits your **budget**. You can compare the prices of different programs to find the one that is suitable for you.

**4. Range of Services**
The various types of **services** offered by an immigration consultancy are an important factor. It should include services such as **assessment and eligibility checking**, assistance with **language tests**, **documentation**, **due diligence checks**, and **post-landing services**. Aspirants should choose immigration consultants who offer a comprehensive range of services and assistance to get the best support.

**5. Location and Branches**
For better assistance, choose an immigration consultancy with branches across several major locations globally. The **ease of access** ensures a hassle-free and smoother immigration journey.

**6. Written Agreement**
Choosing an immigration consultancy that provides a **written agreement** is a must as it ensures transparency in the services offered. This ensures you are dealing with a **reliable and trustworthy immigration consultancy** capable of preparing your case according to legal requirements.

Keeping the above factors in consideration can save aspirants a lot of **time and resources**. Ensure that the **immigration consultants in India** you choose are genuine and reliable.
