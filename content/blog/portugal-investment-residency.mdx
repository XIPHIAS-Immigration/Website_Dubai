## Portugal Investment Programs

### 1. Portugal Golden Visa – Real Estate Investment
- Invest **€500,000 in new approved real estate** or
- Invest **€350,000 in buildings older than 30 years**, with renovation
- Obtain residency through the **Golden Visa Program**

### 2. Business Investment
- Invest in a **running enterprise** or **start a new business**
- Must create **at least 10 local jobs**
- Qualifies for residency through Golden Visa

### 3. Cultural Donation
- Donate **€250,000** to organizations supporting **cultural heritage and arts**
- Qualifies for residency under Golden Visa

### 4. Portugal Angel Visa Program
- Invest in **R&D projects** in Portugal or France
- Minimum investment of **€350,000**
- Residency eligibility through Angel Visa

### 5. Company Shares Investment
- Acquire shares of approved companies
- Minimum investment of **€350,000**
