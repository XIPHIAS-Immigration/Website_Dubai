## What is a European Golden Visa?

A **European Golden Visa** is a **residency-by-investment program**. By investing in real estate, business, or government-approved funds, you gain **residency rights** and may eventually apply for **citizenship**.

**Benefits include:**

- Long-term residency in Europe
- Visa-free travel across the **Schengen Zone**
- Access to **quality education, healthcare, and lifestyle**
- Opportunities to invest or start **businesses in Europe**
