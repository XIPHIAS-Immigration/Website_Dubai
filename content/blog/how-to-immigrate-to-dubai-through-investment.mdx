## Main Investment Visa Options in Dubai

### 1. Dubai Golden Visa (10-Year Residency)
The UAE Golden Visa is a long-term residency visa granted to investors, entrepreneurs, skilled professionals, and exceptional talents.

**Eligibility:**

- Invest in **real estate worth AED 2 million** or more (~USD $545,000)
- Own shares in a UAE company or establish a company with capital investment
- Valid health insurance and proof of income

**Benefits:**

- **10-year renewable residency visa**
- Sponsor your **family and domestic workers**
- No local sponsor required
- Freedom to **live, work, and study in UAE**

### 2. Property Investor Visa (2–5 Years)
Available to individuals who invest in residential property in Dubai.

**Eligibility:**

- Own property worth at least **AED 750,000** (~USD $204,000)
- Property must be **completed** (not under construction)
- Joint ownership allowed (spouses)

**Benefits:**

- **2 to 5-year residency**
- Renewable as long as you hold the property
- **Family sponsorship**

### 3. Business/Startup Visa via Dubai Future District Fund or Other Programs
Dubai supports innovation and entrepreneurship through dedicated investment funds and startup accelerators.

**Eligibility:**

- Establish a **business** or invest in a qualified **startup**
- Be endorsed by an approved **UAE incubator or accelerator**
- Submit a **viable business plan**

**Benefits:**

- **5-year renewable visa**
- **Family sponsorship**
- Pathway to the **Dubai Golden Visa**

## Step-by-Step Process: How to Immigrate to Dubai Through Investment

1. **Choose Your Investment Path**
   Decide whether you'll invest in **real estate**, set up a **company**, or contribute to an **investment fund/startup**.

2. **Gather Documents**
   - Passport copy
   - Investment proof (property title deed, company license, etc.)
   - Bank statements
   - Health insurance
   - Passport photos
   - Good conduct certificate (if required)

3. **Submit Application**
   Apply through the **General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA)** or **Dubai Land Department** for property-related visas.

4. **Undergo Medical Tests and Biometrics**
   Standard UAE residency process includes a **medical check** and **Emirates ID registration**.

5. **Receive Residency Visa**
   Once approved, you'll receive your **residency permit** and **Emirates ID**.
