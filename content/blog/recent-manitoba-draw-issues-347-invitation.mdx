## Recent Manitoba Draw Issues 347 Invitation

September 19 2019 11:28:01 PM

The **Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program (MPNP)** led its most up-to-date draw on 12th September. The draw welcomed 347 **skilled workers** and **international students** to apply for a provincial nomination.

The MPNP has issued more than 700 invitations under the following streams in less than a month:

- **Skilled Workers in Manitoba**
- **International/Universal Education Stream**
- **Overseas Skilled Workers**

Manitoba has issued 6,157 invitations so far in 2019, covering all three sub-streams.

Out of the 347, 45 invitations were issued to **Express Entry candidates**, and 254 invitations went to the **Skilled Workers in Manitoba stream**. Foreign students and skilled foreign workers who are already employed in a full-time, permanent job in Manitoba can apply under this category.

The candidates with a minimum score of 545 were filtered in the Skilled Workers in Manitoba stream.

53 invitations were issued under the **Skilled Workers Overseas** classification through the Strategic Recruitment Initiative, according to CIC News. The lowest scoring applicant in this category had a score of 706.

Under the **International Education Stream**, 40 invitations were issued by the MPNP. There are three sub-classifications under this stream:

- **Graduate Internship Pathway**
- **Professional Employment Pathway**
- **Student Entrepreneur Pathway**

## FAQs

1. **What is the Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program (MPNP)?**
A. MPNP is a **provincial immigration program** that allows Manitoba to nominate skilled workers, international graduates, and other candidates to apply for **Canada PR**.

2. **How many invitations were issued in the recent draw?**
A. A total of **347 invitations** were issued across **Express Entry**, **Skilled Workers in Manitoba**, and **International Education Stream**.

3. **Who qualifies for the Skilled Workers Overseas stream?**
A. Skilled foreign workers with relevant work experience and education outside Canada may apply through this stream. Applicants are evaluated based on **score criteria**.

4. **What are the sub-classifications under the International Education Stream?**
A. There are three pathways: **Graduate Internship Pathway**, **Professional Employment Pathway**, and **Student Entrepreneur Pathway**.
