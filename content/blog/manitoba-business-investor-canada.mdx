**Canada**, one of the most sought-after countries for global entrepreneurs, invites skilled immigrants and investors through its **Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP)**. The **Manitoba Business Investor Program** under **MPNP** offers pathways for **entrepreneurs** and **farm investors** to invest, establish businesses, and pursue **Permanent Residency (PR)** in Canada.

## Manitoba Entrepreneur Program

Manitoba, known for its **rivers and vast farmland**, has introduced the **Manitoba Business Investor Stream** to attract selected entrepreneurs. Applicants must meet eligibility criteria and demonstrate the intention to open or invest in a business in Manitoba. There are two key pathways under the **Manitoba Entrepreneur Program**:

### Entrepreneur Pathway

This pathway replaced the previous **Business Stream** and is designed for experienced business owners.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

1. Minimum **3 years of business experience** within the last 5 years
2. Ownership of at least **33.3% of the business**
3. Achieve **Canada language proficiency benchmark** of 5
4. Minimum age: **25 years**
5. **Investment Requirements**:
   - **CAD $250,000** if investing in Manitoba Capital
   - **CAD $150,000** if investing outside Manitoba Capital
6. Business must create at least **1 local job**
7. Mandatory business visit to Manitoba within 1 year before submitting **Expression of Interest (EOI)**
8. Minimum **personal net worth of CAD $500,000**

**Process Overview:**

1. Submit **Expression of Interest (EOI)** and, if accepted, sign a **Business Performance Agreement**
2. Obtain **temporary work permit** and arrive in Manitoba to invest or operate the business within **24 months**
3. Submit **business progress reports** to Manitoba immigration
4. Receive **provincial nomination** and apply for **Permanent Residency (PR)** through **IRCC**

### Farm Investor Pathway

Designed for individuals with **farming experience**, this pathway requires:

- Sufficient funds to purchase **farm land** in Manitoba
- Experience relevant to the **Manitoba farm industry**
- Minimum investment: **CAD $300,000**

Other eligibility requirements mirror the **Entrepreneur Pathway**.

## FAQs

**Who can apply for the Manitoba Business Investor Program?**
Entrepreneurs and farm investors with relevant experience and required **investment capital**.

**What is the minimum investment for the Entrepreneur Pathway?**
- **CAD $250,000** in Manitoba Capital
- **CAD $150,000** outside Manitoba Capital

**How does the Farm Investor Pathway differ?**
It requires proven **farming experience** and a minimum **CAD $300,000** investment in farm land.

**Can I apply for Permanent Residency through this program?**
Yes, after receiving **provincial nomination**, you can apply for **PR through IRCC**.
