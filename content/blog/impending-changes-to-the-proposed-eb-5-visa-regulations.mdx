**EB 5 Visa Updates:** As has been in a proposal for a long time now, the imminent **regulation changes** for the **EB5 US-based Investor VISA Program** have completed their review on the 28th of June for **Modernization of Regulation** and are now waiting for publication.

We at this stage, cannot define in exact terms what the changes are, however, we can anticipate an **increase in the amount of investment** expected from each investor and a change in the **Targeted Employment Area (TEA)** definitions.

This is now waiting for **Final Publication** in the **Federal Register** as **Final Regulation**, which would mean the implementation of the changes.

Once the **Final Publication** happens, we will have **30 - 60 days of grace period** before the actual application of the regulation.

During the grace period, it has been anticipated by **Law experts** that the **USCIS** would receive 4,000 or more applications.

## FAQs

**1. What is the EB5 Investor Visa?**
- The **EB5 Visa** is a U.S. investor visa that allows foreign nationals to obtain **permanent residency** in the United States by investing in a U.S. business.

**2. What are the proposed changes to the EB5 Visa regulations?**
- The **proposed changes** include an increase in the required investment amount and modifications to the **Targeted Employment Area (TEA)** definitions.

**3. When will the regulation changes for the EB5 Visa be implemented?**
- The changes will be implemented after the **Final Publication** in the **Federal Register**, with a grace period of 30-60 days before application.

**4. How many applications are expected to be filed during the grace period?**
- It is expected that **USCIS** will receive **4,000 or more applications** during the grace period.
