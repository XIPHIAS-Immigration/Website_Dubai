## France Talent Passport Program Options

Investors with business experience can enter France through the Talent Passport Program by pursuing one of the following pathways:

### 1. Start or Acquire a Business in France
Investors must prepare a detailed business plan outlining the business model, value to France, and project characteristics. Businesses can be commercial, artisanal, or industrial.

- Minimum investment: €30,000
- Requirement: Master’s degree or equivalent, plus 5 years of professional experience
- Visa: **Talent Passport Visa (long-stay)**
- Residence Permit: Multi-year permit within 2 months of arrival, valid for 4 years (renewable)

Investors can also apply for a **12-month Passport Talent Visa**, validated within 3 months of arrival if an extended stay is not required.

### 2. Direct Economic Investment
Investments include:
- Equity capital (10% or more)
- Real estate or capital gains
- Reinvested earnings from subsidiaries
- Inter-affiliate loans

Visa and residence permit procedures are similar to the business investment pathway.

### 3. Innovative Economic Project Recognized by a Public Body
Investors present a fully justified project recognized and approved by a French public body.

- Visa: Talent Passport under Skilled Residence Permit for Innovative Projects
- Residence Permit: Multi-year, valid for 4 years
- Validation: Long-term visa option available for stays under 12 months

Financial subsistence proof is required for the investor and dependents.

### 4. Corporate Appointment in a French Company
Senior management personnel opening or joining a French branch can apply. Requirements:
- At least 1 year in senior management
- Authority as a legal representative
- Competitive pay scale

Visa: Talent Passport Visa under Skilled Residence Permit as a company representative
Residence Permit: Multi-year, valid for 4 years (renewable)

## FAQs

**Who is eligible for the France Talent Passport Program?**
Investors or senior management professionals with business experience, a master’s degree (or equivalent), and financial capacity to invest in France.

**What are the investment options under the program?**
Starting/acquiring a business, direct economic investment, innovative projects recognized by a public body, or corporate appointments.

**What is the minimum investment to qualify?**
€30,000 for business startups; other investments vary based on project or corporate requirements.

**How long is the residence permit valid?**
Multi-year residence permits are valid for 4 years and are renewable.

**Can investors bring family members?**
Yes, dependents can apply alongside the primary applicant.
