## Relocate to Australia for Better Opportunities

Australia is renowned for its beauty, adventurous lifestyle, strong economy, and stable government. With well-organized infrastructure and a thriving work culture, Australia continues to attract skilled professionals from around the world. The country provides multiple immigration pathways to fill human resource shortages, including direct **permanent residency programs** for skilled workers.

### Occupations in Demand

Australia publishes a national occupational list highlighting professions currently in demand. Skilled professionals sought include:

- Software professionals
- Doctors and nurses
- Teachers and physical trainers
- Chartered accountants
- Other skilled professionals

Australia offers multiple pathways for skilled migration through the **Australia Immigration programs**.

### Australia PR Pathways

**Sub-Class 189 (Skilled Independent Visa):**
Applicants without a job offer in Australia can apply for **permanent residency** under the Sub-Class 189 visa. This visa allows living, working, and studying anywhere in Australia.

**Sub-Class 190 (Skilled Nominated Visa):**
Skilled professionals nominated by a state or employer can apply under the Sub-Class 190 visa. Nominees must commit to working with the nominating state or employer to obtain **Australia PR**.

**Sub-Class 491 (Skilled Regional Provisional Visa):**
This pathway targets professionals wishing to work in regional Australia. Nominees must work for the regional sponsor for a specified period to qualify for **permanent residency**.

### SkillSelect Portal and Eligibility

Applicants create a profile on the **SkillSelect portal**, valid for up to 2 years, and can update it multiple times. Eligibility is assessed based on:

- **Age**
- **Education**
- **Work experience**
- **Language proficiency**
- **Adaptability**

A minimum score of 65/100 is required for **Sub-Class 189** or **Sub-Class 190**, and 60/100 for **Sub-Class 491**.

Expressions of Interest (EOI) are submitted through the portal along with supporting documents. Working with a **MARA-registered immigration firm** ensures proper guidance, document verification, and increased chances of success.

## FAQs

1. **What is the minimum score required for Australia PR?**
A. For **Sub-Class 189** and **190**, a minimum of 65 points is required. For **Sub-Class 491**, a minimum of 60 points is needed.

2. **Do I need a job offer to apply for Australia PR?**
A. Not for **Sub-Class 189**. However, **Sub-Class 190** and **491** require nomination by a state, employer, or regional authority.

3. **What documents are required to support my application?**
A. Essential documents include **education certificates, work experience letters, language test results (IELTS/PTE), and identity documents**.

4. **What is SkillSelect?**
A. **SkillSelect** is the Australian immigration portal where applicants submit their profiles, update information, and express interest in visas.

5. **Why work with MARA-registered consultants?**
