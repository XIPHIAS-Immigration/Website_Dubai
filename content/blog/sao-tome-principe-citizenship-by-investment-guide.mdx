## Understanding the Sao Tome Citizenship Process

Getting a **Sao Tome passport** is simple and clear. The steps are:

1. **Submit your application:** Apply through the **Citizenship Investment Unit (CIU)** and pay the required fee.
2. **Preliminary approval:** The **CIU** reviews your application and **approves in principle**.
3. **Make your investment:** Complete the chosen **donation** or **approved investment** option.
4. **Receive your documents:** Get your **passport**, **national ID**, and **Certificate of Registration**.
