### Who Qualifies as an Outstanding Specialized Talent?

- **Doctors & Scientists:** Licensed professionals approved by UAE authorities.
- **Specialists in Scientific Fields:** Experts in AI, biotech, big data, software, genetics, and more.
- **Engineers & Tech Specialists:** Professionals meeting UAE innovation talent criteria.
- **Inventors & Innovators:** Patent holders or endorsed innovators.
- **Artists & Creative Professionals:** Portfolio-backed creators in culture, art, or media.
- **Executives:** Senior professionals with ≥5 years of experience and minimum salary criteria.
- **Athletes:** Elite athletes endorsed by sports authorities.
- **Doctoral Degree Holders:** PhDs in priority fields like science, tech, and engineering.
- **Outstanding Students:** High achievers with GPA ≥3.8 from recognized universities.

## Dubai Golden Visa Requirements & Eligibility

**Doctors & Scientists**
- Ministry of Health or Emirates Scientists Council approval.
- Valid **passport** and **health insurance** (1-year minimum, renewable).

**Innovators & Creative Professionals**
- Endorsements from Culture & Arts Departments or Ministry of Economy.
- Passport and health insurance required.

**Executive Leaders**
- Recognized university degree.
- ≥5 years senior-level experience.
- Minimum salary AED 50,000 with employment contract.
- Passport and insurance documents mandatory.

**Specialists & Athletes**
- Specialized degree/certification recognized by UAE authorities.
- Athletes: recommendation from General Sports Authority.

**PhD Holders & Engineers/Scientists**
- Approved PhD in priority disciplines.
- Academic credentials and valid work contract required.
- Passport and health insurance mandatory.

**Outstanding Students**
- High school achievers: 5-year Golden Visa; may sponsor parents/siblings.
- University students: 10-year Golden Visa; may sponsor family members.

## Benefits of Dubai Golden Visa for Specialized Talents

- **Long-term residency** (5–10 years) without a sponsor.
- **Family sponsorship** for spouses, children, and domestic aides.
- **Flexibility to travel** without visa cancellation.
- **Tax-free living** (no personal income tax).
- **Access to innovation-driven ecosystem** and cultural benefits.

## How to Apply for the Dubai Golden Visa

1. **Check eligibility** via the ICP portal.
2. **Secure endorsements** from authorities (Ministry of Health, Education, etc.).
3. **Prepare documents**: passport, CV, nomination letters, academic records.
4. **Submit nomination request** if required.
5. **Apply through** ICP (UAE Smart Portal), GDRFA (Dubai), or TAMM (Abu Dhabi).
