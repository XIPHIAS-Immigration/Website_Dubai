Are you an **Indian investor** looking for better global mobility, a secure backup plan for your family, or a smoother way to expand your business internationally? The **[Grenada Citizenship by Investment (CBI)](https://www.xiphiasimmigration.com/grenada-citizenship-by-investment-immigration.html)** program offers a fast, flexible, and reliable pathway to **second citizenship**.

This small Caribbean island provides one of the **world’s most attractive citizenship options**, drawing thousands of investors annually, especially from India. Here’s why Indian nationals are increasingly choosing Grenada.

## What is Grenada Citizenship by Investment?

Since 2013, [**Grenada’s CBI program**](https://www.xiphiasimmigration.com/grenada-citizenship-by-investment-immigration.html) has allowed **global investors** to support Grenada’s growth and, in return, gain **full citizenship**.

- **Processing Time:** 3–6 months
- **Visa-Free Travel:** 145+ countries, including Schengen, UK, China
- **No Residency Requirement**
- **Citizenship for Life**, passed to future generations

## Why Indians Are Choosing Grenada

Key reasons for the rising demand among Indian investors:

- Indian passport ranks **80th globally**, limiting global mobility
- Access to the **USA E-2 Investor Visa**
- No language test or interview required
- Dual citizenship allowed under specific conditions (OCI for India)

## Top Benefits of Grenada Citizenship

### 1. US Access via E-2 Visa
Grenada is the only **Caribbean country** with an **E-2 Investor Treaty** with the U.S., enabling citizens to **live and work in the U.S.** by setting up a business.

> Fact: Over 40% of Grenadian E-2 visa holders in 2023 were originally Indian nationals.

### 2. Visa-Free Travel to 145+ Countries
Citizens can travel freely to:

- UK
- Schengen countries
- China
- Singapore
- Russia

Perfect for frequent travelers, business owners, and families seeking international education.

### 3. Zero Tax on Global Income
No wealth tax, inheritance tax, capital gains tax, or foreign income tax, making it ideal for high-net-worth Indians.

### 4. Family Inclusion
Covers spouse, dependent children up to 30, parents/grandparents, and siblings (single, childless, 18+).

## Investment Options for Grenada Citizenship

To qualify, choose one of these paths:

### A) Government Donation
- **Amount:** $200,000 (single applicant)
- Non-refundable contribution to the **National Transformation Fund**

### B) Real Estate Investment
- **Amount:** Minimum $220,000 in a government-approved project
- **Holding Period:** 5 years
- Suitable for ROI through rentals or resale

## Grenada Citizenship for Indian Nationals
Advantages over other Caribbean/EU programs:

- **E-2 Visa access to the USA** (unique to Grenada)
- No personal visit or interview required
- Fast route to global mobility and U.S. residency
- Alternative to EB-5 or H-1B visa programs

> Pro Tip: Many Indian entrepreneurs use Grenada passports to **launch U.S. businesses** under the E-2 visa within months.

## FAQs

**Who can apply for Grenada CBI as an Indian national?**
Indian investors with required net worth, good character, and willingness to make a qualifying investment.

**What is the minimum investment for Indians?**
$200,000 for government donation or $220,000 for real estate investment.

**How long does it take to obtain Grenada citizenship?**
Typically **3–6 months** from submission to passport issuance.

**Can families be included?**
Yes, spouse, children, parents, and eligible siblings can be included.
