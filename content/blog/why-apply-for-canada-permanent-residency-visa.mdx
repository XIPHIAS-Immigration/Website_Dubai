## Provides High Standard of Living and Top Citizenship Options

Candidates seek a **better quality of life** and **comfortable lifestyle** when choosing to settle abroad. According to the **US News and World Report**, **Canada ranks No.1 in standard of living** and **No.2 for global citizenship**.

These rankings make Canada one of the most attractive countries for immigrants seeking long-term settlement and **Canadian citizenship**.

## Canada Invites Over 300,000 Migrants Each Year

Each year, **Canada Immigration** invites more than **300,000 candidates** to apply for **Canada PR** and settle permanently.

If your profile meets eligibility requirements and demonstrates strong skills, you stand a high chance of obtaining a **Canada Permanent Residency Visa** through one of the official pathways.

## Canada Uses a Point-Based Immigration System

**Canada** uses the **Express Entry System** — an online, point-based immigration mechanism launched in **January 2015**. It’s a **merit-based system** where applicants are evaluated based on factors such as **age, education, experience, language proficiency, and adaptability**.

Candidates with the **highest CRS (Comprehensive Ranking System) scores** receive invitations to apply for **Canada PR**. The Express Entry system is recognized as one of the **fastest and most efficient** immigration programs in the world.

## Canada PR Processing in 6–8 Months Through Express Entry

The **Canada Express Entry** system processes most **PR applications** within **6–8 months**.

However, the processing time may vary depending on the applicant’s **documentation, verification, and profile details**. Applicants who maintain accuracy and completeness in their submissions are likely to receive faster outcomes.

## Impressive Immigration Policy and Opportunities

Due to **low population growth**, Canada welcomes a wide variety of skilled and semi-skilled workers to fill gaps across different industries.

The **Canadian government** has designed systematic **immigration policies and programs** to attract qualified candidates from around the world. This proactive approach strengthens Canada’s workforce and economy.
