## Introduction

The **Australia Skilled 482 Visa** is a **Temporary Skilled Shortage (TSS) visa** that enables skilled professionals to **work in Australia**. It has three main streams — **Short-Term**, **Medium-Term**, and **Labour Agreement** — designed to fill skill gaps in the Australian workforce.

The visa is **valid for 2 to 4 years**, depending on the chosen stream, and requires **sponsorship from an approved Australian business**. After 3–4 years of residence, eligible applicants can transition towards **Australian permanent residency and citizenship**.

---

## Categories / Streams

### Short-Term Stream
- Live and work in Australia.  
- Valid stay: **2 years minimum**, extendable to **4 years** if **International Trade Obligation (ITO)** applies.

### Medium-Term Stream
- Live and work in Australia.  
- Valid stay: **up to 4 years**.  
- Provides a pathway to **permanent residency**.

### Labour Agreement Stream
- For skilled workers nominated by **employers under a labour agreement** with the Australian Government.  
- Valid stay: **up to 4 years**.  

### Subsequent Entrant
- For **family members** of subclass **457 or 482 visa holders**.  
- Valid stay: as per the **primary visa holder’s** validity.  

**Processing Time:** Approximately **30–47 days**.

---

## Requirements for Australia Skilled 482 Visa

Applicants must:

- Be **nominated by an approved sponsor** for a skilled position.  
- Have the **required skills and qualifications** for the role.  
- **Demonstrate English language proficiency** through approved testing.

## FAQs

**1. Who can apply for the Australia Skilled 482 Visa?**  
Skilled workers nominated by an **approved Australian employer**.

**2. What are the streams under the TSS 482 Visa?**  
**Short-Term**, **Medium-Term**, **Labour Agreement**, and **Subsequent Entrant** (for family members).

**3. Can my family accompany me to Australia on this visa?**  
Yes, **spouse and unmarried children under 18** can apply as **dependents**.
