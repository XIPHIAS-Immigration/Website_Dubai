## Introduction

**The European Investment for Permanent Residency VISA** Programs cover every variety of investment plan for cross-border investors, meeting the requirements of **High Net Worth Individuals**. **Investment VISA** in Europe provides numerous benefits beyond guaranteed return on investment.

Europe is one of the most **united continents**, with countries cooperating through initiatives like the European Union. To encourage **foreign direct investment**, European countries have created programs offering **Permanent Residency** to investors in real estate, business, or government-approved projects.

Investors gain **visa-free travel** across the **European Union and Schengen Area**, along with the ability to **work, study, and expand business opportunities**. Immediate family members can also obtain residency with similar benefits. Children below age limits defined by each country can join as **Permanent Residents**, enjoying similar rights as citizens.

Most European countries are developed, offering **high living standards, industrialized and tech-driven economies, and investor-friendly tax regimes**. Residency programs are often renewable and, in some cases, lead to **citizenship**.

## Popular European Investment Residency Programs

### Portugal Investment Programs
Portugal offers two distinct programs:

- **Portugal Golden VISA** – Residency through **real estate investment**.
- **Portugal Angel VISA Program** – Investment in **Research & Development projects**. Investors can track project progress and invest in real estate or R&D projects with lower investment amounts.

---

### France Investor Program
Known as the **France Talent Passport Program**, it allows investment in **commercial or industrial assets** or companies (minimum 30% stake), providing **Permanent Residency for 10 years**.

---

### Germany Investment Program
Germany allows investors to invest in **real estate or business**.

- Initial **D-Visa** valid for 90 days
- Renewable **Permanent Residency permit** for 5 years
- Citizenship eligibility after 8 years

---

### Greece Golden VISA Program
Investors can obtain residency through **real estate investment**.

- Affordable program with **beautiful landscapes and rich history**
- Tourism-driven economy provides additional business opportunities

---

### Ireland Investment Visa Program
Investors gain **Permanent Residency in Ireland** and may qualify for **US EB-5 Green Card** due to bilateral agreements.

- Real estate investment maintained for at least 5 years

---

### Other European Countries Offering Residency Programs
Hungary, Malta, Cyprus, Switzerland, Latvia, Spain, Monaco, Lithuania, and others provide **Permanent Residency** through investment.
