## Introduction

## SkillSelect and Expression of Interest (EOI)

Australia’s **SkillSelect portal** is the first step for most skilled migrants. Applicants submit an **Expression of Interest (EOI)** with supporting documents.

- Profiles remain in the **SkillSelect pool for 2 years**.
- Points are allocated based on **age, education, work experience, adaptability, and language proficiency**.
- EOIs are **not visa applications**; they express interest in migrating.

Selection is based on **points and the current occupation list** in Australia. Only shortlisted candidates receive invitations to apply for a visa.

## Key Visa Programs

#### Sub Class 189: Skilled Independent Visa
- For skilled professionals **without a sponsor or nominator**.
- Applicants must be **under 45 years of age**.
- Points-tested program (max 100 points) based on **age, education, work experience, adaptability, and language**.
- Allows permanent residency, work, study, and freedom to choose employers.
- **Processing time:** 11–13 months.

#### Sub Class 190: Skilled Nominated Visa
- For professionals **nominated by a state or employer**.
- Job must be in-demand and unfilled by local residents.
- Points-tested program (max 100 points).
- Permanent residency granted; eligibility to apply for **citizenship after 4 years**.
- Applicant must initially work for the nominating employer.

#### Sub Class 491: Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa
- For professionals nominated by **state, territory, or eligible relative**.
- Valid for **5 years**, extendable, with work and study rights in the nominating region.
- Leads to permanent residency and citizenship eligibility after 4 years.

## FAQs

**1. What is the Sub Class 189 visa?**
A points-based visa for skilled professionals without sponsors, granting PR with freedom to work and study anywhere in Australia.

**2. Who is eligible for the Sub Class 190 visa?**
Professionals nominated by a state, territory, or employer to fill in-demand jobs.

**3. What is the Sub Class 491 visa?**
A provisional regional visa for nominated skilled professionals, leading to permanent residency and citizenship eligibility.

**4. Can business investors apply for PR in Australia?**
Yes, through specific **business investment visa programs**, assisting in economic contribution.

**5. Can PR holders sponsor family members?**
Yes, under select immigration streams, PR holders can invite spouses, partners, and dependent children.
