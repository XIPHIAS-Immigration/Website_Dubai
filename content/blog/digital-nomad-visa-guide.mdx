## What is a Digital Nomad Visa?

A **digital nomad visa** allows remote workers and freelancers to legally live in another country while continuing their online jobs. Each country has its own rules regarding proof of remote work, minimum income, and health insurance.

**XIPHIAS Immigration** can help you check eligibility and organize documents to increase your chances of approval.

## Top Countries Offering Digital Nomad Visas

### Brazil: Work from Rio or São Paulo
Live and work remotely for up to a year. Applicants must show proof of employment and income.
Enjoy beachfront cafés and samba evenings while working efficiently.

### Croatia: Live Near the Adriatic Sea
Stay up to a year with proof of income and insurance. Perfect for those seeking history, coastlines, and coworking spaces.

### Germany: Work Freely in Berlin
The [German Freelance Visa](https://www.xiphiasimmigration.com/germany-skilled-visa.html) lets you stay and work legally. Berlin is ideal for culture and productivity.

### Portugal: Lisbon or Porto
Stay up to a year with renewal options. Enjoy affordable living, relaxed lifestyle, and strong digital infrastructure.

### Spain: Sunshine, Culture, and Remote Work
Cities like Barcelona and Madrid offer digital nomad visas. Proof of income, health insurance, and valid passport required.

### UAE: Work from Dubai or Abu Dhabi
One-year remote work visa available. Requirements include employment proof, salary verification, and health insurance.

### Italy: Work from Rome or Milan
Italy’s digital nomad visa allows freelancers to work while enjoying iconic cities. Applicants need proof of income, remote employment, and insurance.

## General Digital Nomad Visa Requirements

Most visas require:

- Proof of stable remote income
- Valid health insurance
- Passport with sufficient validity
- Clean criminal record
- Family inclusion allowed in some countries
