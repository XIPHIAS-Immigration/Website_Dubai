## Top Canadian Immigration Programs

**Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)**
Canada's PNP allows provinces to select skilled professionals who fit their workforce needs. Selected candidates receive a **provincial nomination**, which helps them apply for **permanent residency** with **IRCC Canada**. This program is ideal for skilled professionals seeking targeted opportunities in specific provinces.

**Express Entry Program**
Introduced in 2015, the **Express Entry Program** streamlines Canada's immigration process. Candidates are ranked using a scoring system, and high-ranking profiles are prioritized for permanent residency. Express Entry applies to almost all federal immigration programs, ensuring efficient processing and reduced stress for applicants.
