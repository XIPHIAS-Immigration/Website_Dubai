The **Quebec Investor Immigration Program (QIIP)** allows international investors to obtain **Canada Permanent Residency (PR)** by making a **CAD $800,000 risk-free investment**. It is **Canada's only passive investor immigration program** and offers several advantages over similar programs globally.

## Benefits of the Quebec Investor Immigration Program

- **Direct Canada PR:** No probationary or conditional stage; successful applicants receive a **Canada Permanent Resident Visa**, unlike the U.S. EB-5 program, which requires proof of job creation.
- **Guaranteed Investment:** The **CAD $800,000 investment** is submitted through an approved financial intermediary and returned in full after five years. Financing options via intermediaries are available.
- **Family Inclusion:** Spouse or common-law partner and dependent children under 19 may be included, all receiving **Canada PR**. Benefits include universal health care, free public education, and access to world-class universities.
- **Pathway to Citizenship:** Successful applicants can pursue **Canadian citizenship** and a **Canadian passport**. Current residency requirements: four years within six years, potentially reduced to three years within five.

## Requirements

- **Minimum Net Worth:** Applicants must have at least **CAD $1.6 million** in net worth, alone or with a spouse/partner. Assets include property, bank accounts, pension funds, stocks, and shares.
- **Investment Agreement:** Applicants must intend to settle in **Quebec** and sign an agreement to invest **CAD $800,000** with an approved financial intermediary (broker or trust company). The investment may be financed by the intermediary.
- **Management Experience:** Minimum of **two years of management experience** in the past five years, including experience in commercial or government agencies.

## Preparation is Key

The intake period for applications runs from **May 29, 2017, to February 23, 2018**, with a maximum of **1,900 applications**, including **1,330 from China (including Hong Kong and Macao)**. Candidates with **advanced intermediate French** are not subject to the intake cap and receive priority processing.

The reopening of **Canada's only passive investor immigration program** allows investors to take a less hands-on approach compared to other international programs, making it a truly unique pathway to **Canada PR**.

## FAQs

1. **What is the Quebec Investor Immigration Program (QIIP)?**
A. QIIP allows international investors to obtain **Canada PR** by making a **CAD $800,000 risk-free investment** through an approved financial intermediary.

2. **Can family members be included in the application?**
A. Yes, the **spouse or common-law partner** and **dependent children under 19** can be included, all receiving **Canada PR**.

3. **What are the financial requirements?**
A. Applicants must have a **net worth of CAD $1.6 million** and invest **CAD $800,000**, returned after five years. Financing options are available through approved intermediaries.

4. **Is management experience required?**
A. Yes, applicants must have **two years of management experience** in the past five years.

5. **Are there French language requirements?**
A. Applicants with **advanced intermediate French** are prioritized and may submit applications anytime, exempt from intake caps.
