## Why Travel Freedom for Americans Is Changing

In 2025, the **U.S. passport** dropped to 12th place on the Henley Passport Index, its lowest ranking in two decades. **U.S. citizens** can now access 180 destinations **visa-free**, a decline from previous years. Key reasons include:

- **Reinstated visa requirements:** Countries like Brazil, Myanmar, and Papua New Guinea have brought back visa requirements for **U.S. travelers**.
- **Declining visa-free agreements:** Some nations are creating new reciprocal agreements faster than with the U.S., slightly reducing the **U.S. passport** strength.
- **Geopolitical shifts:** Changes in global politics have led to stricter entry policies, limiting **visa-free travel** for **U.S. citizens**.
