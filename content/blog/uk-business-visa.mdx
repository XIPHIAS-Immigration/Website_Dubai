## UK Innovator Visa Program

The UK Innovator Visa Program is for experienced businesspersons looking to invest in the UK. Such applicants can invest in their innovative idea implementation in the UK. Businesses should be viable and scalable in the long run. The investment required to qualify under this program is £50,000. The investor needs to obtain a letter of endorsement from an endorsing body. The investor cannot be a national of the EEA or Switzerland. Through this investment visa program, the investor and immediate family can get to live in the UK for good—first as permanent residents, later as citizens.

The applicant will have to submit a business plan proving the viability and scalability of the business. The plan should be genuine and demonstrate that it meets new and existing market needs with a competitive edge.

- Validity: 3 years (entry on Innovator Visa or switch from a different visa)
- Renewal: Renewable; after 5 years of living in the UK, eligible to apply for Indefinite Leave to Remain (UK Settlement)

## UK Start-up Visa

The UK Start-up Visa replaces the Tier 1 Graduate Entrepreneur Visa. This program can be used by high-potential businesspeople who want to start a new business in the UK.

- No initial investment required (unlike the Innovator route)
- Applicant must be 18+, have a business plan/idea approved by an authorized governing body (endorsement required)
- Must prove English language competence and financial ability to support self and dependents (provide proof)
- Validity: 2 years (not renewable). Applicants can switch to other visa programs to continue living in London/UK

## UK Sole Representative of an Overseas Business Visa

The UK Sole Representative of an Overseas Business Visa helps senior managers or management representatives of an already running overseas business to open a branch in the UK. The branch should align with the parent business.

- The representative cannot work for anybody else
- Stay: 3 years, extendable by 2 years
- Eligible to apply for Indefinite Leave to Remain after 5 years in the UK
- Must provide documents proving relationship with the business, English language competency, and domain experience
- Family (immediate) can accompany

## FAQs

**Q1. What is the difference between the UK Innovator Visa and the UK Start-up Visa?**
A. The Innovator Visa typically requires £50,000 investment and an endorsed, scalable business, with a route to settlement. The Start-up Visa requires no initial investment, is valid for 2 years, and allows switching to other routes later.

**Q2. Do I need an endorsement for these UK business routes?**
A. Yes. Both Innovator and Start-up routes require endorsement by an authorized body based on innovation, viability, and scalability. The Sole Representative route requires documented proof of your position and company authority.

**Q3. Can my family accompany me to the UK on these visas?**
A. Yes. Dependents (spouse/partner and eligible children) can usually accompany under Innovator, Start-up, and Sole Representative routes, subject to meeting maintenance and documentation requirements.

**Q4. When can I apply for Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)?**
A. Under the Innovator route, you may be eligible for ILR after 5 years (subject to meeting program criteria). The Sole Representative route also leads to ILR after 5 years of qualifying residence.
