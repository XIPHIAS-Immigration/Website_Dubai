## Popular Investment Migration Programs

Migration for investment has shaped societies and economies globally. Investors contribute to the prosperity of their adopted countries while benefiting from **residency and citizenship advantages**. Studies show migrants form 3% of the global population but contribute **9% to global GDP**, making investment migration a win-win proposition.


### Investment Options

- **Investment in New Business** – Investors create employment and contribute to local economies. Popular destinations: UK, USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand.
- **Investment in Startups** – Entrepreneurial investors can launch innovative businesses. Examples: Canada Start-Up Visa, UK Innovator Visa.
- **Investment in Real Estate** – Investors acquire properties in countries like Portugal, Turkey, Spain, Greece, Malta, or Caribbean nations (Dominica, St. Lucia, St. Kitts and Nevis, Grenada, Antigua & Barbuda).

## Leading Investment Migration Programs by Country

### Canada
- **Canada Investor Visa** – Permanent Residency for investors and family. Citizenship eligibility after 3 years.
- **Canada Start-Up Visa** – For immigrant entrepreneurs establishing innovative businesses.
- **Saskatchewan Immigrant Nominee Program** – Invest in local businesses or startups, reside in Saskatchewan.
- **Ontario Immigration Program** – Start or purchase businesses in Ontario, then apply for permanent residency.

### USA
- **EB-5 Immigrant Investor Program** – Invest in a business to create jobs and obtain a **Green Card**.
- Investment minimum: USD 1,050,000 (Non-Targeted Areas) or USD 800,000 (Targeted Areas).
- New Integrity Fund fee: USD 1,000. Program allows Green Card application even if program expires.

### Australia
- **Subclass 188 Business Innovation & Investment Visa** – Valid for 4 years; eligible for permanent residency.
- **Streams:**
  - **Business Innovation** – Nomination by state/territory for entrepreneurs.
  - **Investors** – AUD 2.5M investment in Australia.
  - **Significant Investors** – AUD 5M investment in Victoria.
  - **Entrepreneurs** – Startup founders supported via Victorian Government nomination.

### Germany
- **Investor Residency Program** – Invest in regional development funds for temporary residency.
- Temporary permit for 5 years; permanent residency after 3 years. Citizenship eligibility after 8 years.
- Benefits include **high living standards, free education, healthcare, and excellent infrastructure**.

## FAQs

**1. What are the main investment migration programs worldwide?**
- Canada Investor Visa, Canada Start-Up Visa, EB-5 USA, Australia Subclass 188, Germany Investor Residency.

**2. Who is eligible for investment migration programs?**
- High-net-worth individuals willing to invest in business, startups, or real estate, and meet the country-specific criteria.

**3. How long does it take to get permanent residency?**
- Varies by program; Canada and Germany allow permanent residency after 3 years of residence, Australia after 4 years (Subclass 188), and EB-5 USA provides a Green Card upon investment.

**4. Can family members benefit from investment migration?**
- Yes, most programs extend residency and future citizenship benefits to spouses and dependent children.
