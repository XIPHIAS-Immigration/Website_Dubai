## Official Canada PR Documents Required - Core List

According to the official guidelines from Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC), applicants must submit supporting documents related to identity, education, work experience, and background as part of their permanent residence application.


## Identity Documents Checklist

These establish who you are and must be consistent across all records.

### Required:

- Valid passport (all pages with stamps where applicable)  
- Birth certificate (recommended, and required when proving relationship to dependents)  
- Name change affidavit (if your name differs across documents)  
- Marriage certificate (if married)  
- Divorce decree (if applicable)  

### Important tips:

- A passport should ideally have strong remaining validity.  
- Name spelling must match across passport, education, and work records.  


## Education Documents + ECA Checklist

For Canada PR, your education must be assessed and verified.

**Canada PR education documents ECA checklist:**

- Degree certificates  
- Consolidated mark sheets/transcripts  
- Individual semester mark sheets (if required by assessor)  
- ECA report copy  
- ECA reference number  

Some applicants upload only degree certificates and miss transcripts when required by the ECA organization, which can lead to additional document requests.

## Language Test Documents (Mandatory)

Applicants must submit valid language test results.

### Required:

- IELTS General Training or CELPIP General (English)  
- TEF/TCF (French, if applicable)  
- Test Report Form (TRF) number  
- Test must be valid (within 2 years)  

## Work Experience Documents Required for Canada PR From India

### Required:

Experience letter on company letterhead, including job title, detailed duties, employment dates, working hours per week, salary, and employer contact details.

### Optional but recommended supporting documents (especially for Indian applicants):

- Joining/offer letter  
- Salary slips  
- Promotion letters  
- Form 16 / ITR  
- EPF statements  
- Company ID card  

## India-Specific Supporting Documents Many Applicants Miss

These documents are not mandatory but can strengthen your application, particularly if your primary experience letter lacks detail or your employer cannot be fully verified.

**Canada PR supporting documents, India optional but powerful:**

- Form 16  
- Income Tax Returns  
- EPF statements  
- Bank salary credit statements  
- Appointment confirmation emails  
- Company appraisal letters  

These are especially useful if:

- Your experience letter is basic  
- The company is small or closed  
- Salary was partly variable  

## Express Entry vs PNP — Document Checklist Differences

Many confuse these streams and upload the wrong documents.

| Document                 | Express Entry | PNP            |
| ------------------------ | ------------- | -------------- |
| Nomination certificate   | Not required  | Required       |
| Settlement plan          | Not required  | Often required |
| Province job offer proof | Optional      | Often required |
| Province forms           | No            | Yes            |

If you’re using a canada pr pnp document checklist, make sure province-specific forms are included.

## Canada PR Proof of Funds Documents India

Proof of funds is required for Federal Skilled Worker (FSW) and Federal Skilled Trades (FST) applicants, but is not required for Canadian Experience Class (CEC) candidates or applicants with valid arranged employment in Canada.

Typical proof of funds documents include:

- Official bank balance letter  
- Recent 6-month bank statements  
- Fixed deposit certificates (if applicable)  
- Gift deed and donor bank trail (if funds are gifted)   

## Canada PR PCC Documents India

Police clearance is mandatory.

**Canada PR PCC documents India:**

- Police Clearance Certificate issued by the Passport Office or an authorized police authority  
- Or Regional PCC, where applicable  
- PCC must match passport details  
- Address history must align  

## Medical Documents — What to Prepare

Medical results are submitted electronically by panel physicians. Most applicants complete the medical exam only after receiving instructions from IRCC, although some may choose to do an upfront medical.

## Canada PR Document Format Rules (Critical for Approval)

Even correct documents get rejected due to the wrong format.

**Canada PR documentation checklist — format rules:**

- PDF preferred  
- Merge multi-page documents into one file  
- Respect file size limits  
- Color scans where stamps exist  
- Clear resolution — no blur  

Certified translation is required for any document not in English or French, along with the translator’s affidavit where applicable.

## Canada PR Document Checklist After ITA

After Invitation to Apply, you’ll upload:

- All identity documents  
- Education + ECA  
- Work experience proves  
- Proof of funds  
- PCC  
- Medical receipt  
- Travel history details  
- Address history  
- Digital photos  

Time is limited, prepare these before ITA.

## Top Canada PR Document Mistakes That Cause Delays

These canada pr document mistakes appear repeatedly:

- Experience letters without duties  
- Duties not matching NOC  
- Missing salary proof  
- ECA number mismatch  
- No bank seal on the funds letter  
- Cash salary with no banking trail  
- Name variation across records  
- Marriage proof confusion  
- Uploading the wrong file in the wrong slot  
- Untranslated regional documents

## FAQs
**How many documents are required for Canada PR from India?**  
There is no fixed number; most applicants submit 15–30 combined documents across identity, education, work, funds, and background.

**Is Aadhaar required for Canada PR?**  
Aadhaar is not required for Canada PR. It may be used as an additional supporting identity document if relevant, but IRCC primarily relies on passports and civil status documents.

**Is notarization required for Canada PR documents?**  
Only for specific affidavits or translations, not for every document.

**Can Form 16 be used for Canada PR?**  
Yes, as supporting employment and income proof, not as a replacement for experience letters.

**Do I need a PCC for Canada PR?**  
Yes, police clearance is mandatory for adult applicants.
