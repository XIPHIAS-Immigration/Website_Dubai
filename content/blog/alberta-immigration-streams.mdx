Alberta provides its nomination for all successful candidates shortlisted to migrate through its **Alberta Provincial Nomination Program**. Alberta is one of the most prosperous provinces in **Canada**, rich in natural resources and renowned national parks. Alberta invites **skilled professionals** and **business investors** to migrate through the following 5 streams:

- **Alberta Opportunity Stream**
- **Alberta Express Entry Stream**
- **Self-Employed Farmer Stream**
- **International Graduate Entrepreneur Immigration Stream**
- **Foreign Graduate Start-Up Visa Stream**

Each of these pathways is tailored for **targeted migrants** who can actively contribute to **Canada’s economic and cultural growth**. Alberta works closely with the **Federal Government** to facilitate the immigration process.

**Alberta Opportunity Stream:**
Designed for **foreign skilled professionals** or **business investors** already present in Canada on a temporary visa, including **international graduates**. Applicants must demonstrate **language skills, education, work experience, and economic self-sufficiency**.

**Alberta Express Entry Stream:**
Linked to the **Federal Express Entry Pool**, this stream allows Alberta to nominate eligible candidates, giving **600 extra CRS points**, improving the chances of obtaining **Canadian PR**. Applicants must **intend to live in Alberta** for a specified period. In November 2021, 100 nominations were issued through this stream.

**Self-Employed Farmer Stream:**
For **foreign farmers** intending to establish a farm in Alberta. Applicants must demonstrate **financial stability, farming expertise, and management skills**, working with Alberta’s **Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry**.

**International Graduate Entrepreneur Immigration Stream:**
For **foreign graduates** from Alberta post-graduation institutions. Candidates first establish in Alberta on a **temporary work permit** and then apply for **permanent residency**.

**Foreign Graduate Start-Up Visa Stream:**
For **entrepreneurs educated outside Canada** aiming to set up a **start-up business** in Alberta. Applicants submit an **Expression of Interest** through the **Alberta portal**.

## FAQs

**Which Alberta streams are available for skilled professionals and business investors?**
- Alberta Opportunity Stream
- Alberta Express Entry Stream
- Self-Employed Farmer Stream
- International Graduate Entrepreneur Immigration Stream
- Foreign Graduate Start-Up Visa Stream

**Who can apply under the Alberta Opportunity Stream?**
Foreign skilled professionals or business investors currently in Canada on a temporary visa, including international graduates.

**How does the Express Entry Stream work in Alberta?**
Eligible candidates from the **Federal Express Entry Pool** are nominated by Alberta, receiving **600 CRS points** to enhance PR eligibility.

**What is required for the Self-Employed Farmer Stream?**
Applicants must have **farming expertise, management skills, and financial stability** to establish a farm in Alberta.

**Can international graduates apply for Alberta PR?**
Yes, through the **International Graduate Entrepreneur Immigration Stream** or the **Foreign Graduate Start-Up Visa Stream**.
