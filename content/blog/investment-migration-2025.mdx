## The New Landscape of Investment Migration (2021–2025)

Over the past four years, residency and citizenship programs have undergone their biggest reset in decades. Governments are shifting from volume-driven models to those prioritizing **credibility**, **transparency**, and **economic contribution**.

Key global developments include:

- **Spain** has discontinued its Golden Visa, signaling a new EU-wide stance on property-led residency.
- **UK** closed its long-running **Tier 1 Investor Visa**.
- **Ireland** shut down its Immigrant Investor Programme.
- **Bulgaria** and **Montenegro** ended their CBI pathways to align with EU recommendations.
- **Portugal** removed real estate from the Golden Visa, redirecting investment toward funds, innovation, and cultural projects.
- **Turkey** increased its CIP real estate threshold from $250,000 to **$400,000** (June 2022).

These shifts send a clear message:

> **“The programs that remain are the ones built on credibility and compliance.”**

## Rising Standards: Due Diligence, Screening & Compliance

Countries across the world have strengthened background checks and documentation requirements to safeguard their reputations.

- Caribbean nations such as **Dominica**, **Grenada**, and **St. Lucia** now enforce mandatory interviews and multi-layered due diligence.
- The **UK** has imposed travel restrictions on Dominica and Vanuatu passport holders.
- Global **AML**, **OECD**, and financial transparency rules now influence every residency and citizenship application.

In 2025:

> **“Citizenship shortcuts no longer exist — your financial integrity is now the deciding factor.”**

## Evolution of the Malta Investment Ecosystem (2021–2025)

Malta continues to stand out as one of the most structured investment migration ecosystems, but its framework has evolved.

- The **MEIN citizenship route** has been discontinued and replaced with a highly selective, merit-based pathway.
- The **Malta Permanent Residence Programme (MPRP)** now offers:
  - A **temporary residence permit** for faster relocation  
  - The ability to earn **rental income** from qualifying real estate  
  - More flexible documentation and processing requirements  

These updates reflect the global direction of investment migration:

> **“Successful programs now balance transparency, accountability, and long-term economic benefit.”**
