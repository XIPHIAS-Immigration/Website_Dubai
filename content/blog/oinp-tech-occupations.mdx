## Overview
The most recent **Ontario PNP Tech Draw** was held on **1st August 2019** with a CRS score of **435**, where **1773 applications** received **Notifications of Interest** under **IT occupations for Canadian immigration**. This was the second draw after the new stream launched in **July 2019**, targeting **Information Technology Skilled Professionals**.

The number of invitations has **increased rapidly** with a decreasing **CRS score**. The first draw on **12th July 2019** had **1623 invitations**, while the second draw increased to **1773 invitations**. This trend indicates **higher approvals** for **IT-skilled professionals** in upcoming draws.

## In-Demand Occupations and NOC Codes
- **NOC 0213** – Computer and Information Systems Managers  
- **NOC 2147** – Computer Engineers  
- **NOC 2172** – Database Analysts and Data Administrators  
- **NOC 2173** – Software Engineers and Designers  
- **NOC 2175** – Web Designers and Developers  

If your profile aligns with these **NOC codes** and relevant experience, this is an **ideal time to apply**.

## About the Ontario PNP Tech Stream
The **Ontario PNP Tech Stream** assesses **qualified IT profiles** from the **Express Entry pool**. Selected candidates receive a **Notification of Interest** following the **OINP Tech Express Entry draw**.

This program is designed to **attract IT-skilled professionals globally** and address **labour shortages in Canada**. By **2021**, approximately **220,000 skilled workers** were projected to be needed in the **Information and Communication Technology sector**.
