## Investment Options Under the Global Investor Program

The **GIP** offers three main ways to qualify for [**Singapore Permanent Residency**](https://www.xiphiasimmigration.com/) **by Investment**:

**Option A:** Invest **SGD 10 million** in a new or existing Singapore-based business. Beyond the investment, the business must create jobs for Singaporeans and actively contribute to the local economy.

**Option B:** Invest **SGD 25 million** in an approved Singapore-based fund. These funds support local enterprises and help align your investment with **Singapore’s** broader economic development goals.

**Option C:** Establish a single-family office with assets of at least **SGD 200 million**. At least **SGD 50 million** must be deployed in Singapore investments such as local companies, bonds, or private equity. The family office must also employ Singaporean professionals to meet the program’s criteria.

Acquiring **Singapore PR by Investment** and eventually citizenship is a structured but rewarding journey. Here’s how it unfolds:

2. **Approval in Principle:** If the application meets all criteria, the **Singapore Economic Development Board (EDB)** issues a conditional approval, confirming your eligibility for **Singapore PR by Investment**.

5. **Citizenship Application:** After maintaining PR status for at least two years, applicants can apply for **Singaporean citizenship**, unlocking full rights and global mobility benefits.
