Getting **Monaco’s Permanent Residency VISA** also leads to becoming a **permanent resident of Europe (European PR)**. ## Residency Stages & Timeline

There will be **3 stages** in obtaining the **residency permit**. In the **first stage**, the investor gets a **temporary residency permit** for **12 months** time period. It would be necessary for the investor to **live in Monaco** for a period of at least **3 months** in the **12 months’** time. The investor will be **renewing** the **temporary residency permit** for **3 years** in total. Only after which, the investor will be granted a proper **ordinary residency card**. This is the **second stage** of the visa process. This **residency card** would be valid for **3 years**, post which the card should be **renewed**. This card can be renewed as many times as possible.

## Privileged Residency (10-Year Card)

Once the investor has earned at least **3 temporary residency permits** and **2 ordinary residency cards**, he/she becomes eligible to apply for **privileged residency permit** which allows the investor **residency for 10 years** in **Monaco**. However, to **maintain** this **privileged residency permit**, the investor will have to **reside in Monaco** for a period **no less than 183 days every year**.
