## FAQs

**What is investment migration for startups?**
It’s using **residency by investment** or **citizenship by investment** pathways to gain **global mobility**, access **new markets**, and attract **international talent** for a **startup**.

**How does investment migration help with hiring?**
Being present in pro-innovation jurisdictions eases **work permits**, widens the **talent pool**, and strengthens your employer brand for **tech hiring**.

**Is investment migration the same as a startup visa?**
Not exactly. **Startup visas** are activity-based routes for innovators; **investment migration** is capital- or contribution-based (**RBI/CBI**) that can complement a **startup** strategy.

**Which programs suit tech founders?**
It depends on your goals—**residency by investment** for **mobility** and **market access**, or **startup/innovator visas** for operating locally. Speak with advisors to map program fit to your **funding**, **team**, and **market** plan.
