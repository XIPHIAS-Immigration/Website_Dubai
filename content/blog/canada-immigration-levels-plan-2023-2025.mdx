## Canada Immigration Levels Plan 2023–2025

**Canada** recently unveiled its **Immigration Levels Plan 2023–2025**. In **2024**, the target rises to **485,000** newcomers and in **2025** to **500,000**. In **2021**, **405,000** immigrants were welcomed (a record), and by the end of **2022**, **Canada** expected to welcome **432,000** newcomers.

Canada’s immigration goals guide how many permanent residents it plans to admit each year—balancing **economic growth**, **family reunification**, and **refugee/protection** commitments.


## Express Entry & PNP Targets Will Rise

Most new permanent residents arrive through **economic-class** programs, notably **Express Entry** and the **Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)**.

### Express Entry (principal applicants + family)
- **82,880** in **2023**  
- **109,020** in **2024**  
- **114,000** in **2025**

### Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)
- **105,500** in **2023**  
- **110,000** in **2024**  
- **117,500** in **2025**

The **PNP** remains a leading **economic immigration** pathway, with growing allocations alongside **Express Entry** to meet provincial labour-market needs.


## What This Means for Applicants

- More invitations across **Express Entry** and **PNP** streams  
- Strong profiles (**CLB**, **ECA**, **work experience**) and **province-specific strategies** matter more than ever  
- Consider **PNP** to gain **+600 CRS points** and strengthen your **Express Entry** prospects

## FAQs

**Q1. Does a higher overall target guarantee an ITA?**  
No. Higher targets help, but selection still depends on **CRS** competitiveness (or meeting **PNP** criteria). Strengthen **language**, **education (ECA)**, and **experience** to improve your chances.

**Q2. Which is faster—Express Entry or PNP?**  
It depends on your profile and provincial priorities. A **PNP nomination** adds **+600 CRS points**, often expediting an **ITA** through **Express Entry**.

**Q3. Are spouses and dependents included in the targets?**  
Yes. **IRCC** counts **principal applicants**, **spouses/partners**, and **dependent children** toward the annual targets.

**Q4. If I don’t qualify for Express Entry, can I still apply?**  
Potentially. Many **PNP** streams target **in-demand occupations**, **work/study ties**, or **employer support**. A tailored provincial strategy can open alternatives to **Express Entry**.
