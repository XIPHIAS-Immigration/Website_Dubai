**Canada** has placed focus on **reuniting parents and grandparents** with its residents and citizens. The **Parents and Grandparents Program (PGP)**, introduced in 2011, allows **parents and grandparents of Canadian residents/citizens** and their spouses to obtain **Permanent Residency (PR)** in Canada.


## Program Overview
- Canada allowed **10,000 applications** for parents and grandparents in 2020
- **Expression of Interest (EOI)** submissions occur to shortlist sponsors
- Selected sponsors receive an **Invitation to Apply (ITA)** for PR
- Sponsors must respond within **60 days** with completed applications

## Eligibility Criteria
1. **Age:** Sponsors must be **18 years or older**
2. **Status:** Sponsors must be **legal residents or citizens of Canada**
3. **Financial Proof:** Sponsors must demonstrate **economic stability** to support parents and grandparents


## Application Process
1. **Submit Expression of Interest** – Between October 13th and November 3rd, 2020
2. **EOI Filtering** – IRCC eliminates duplicates and shortlists sponsors
3. **Invitation to Apply** – Only selected sponsors receive the ITA
4. **Submit Complete Application** – Within **60 days** of invitation


## Other Canadian Immigration Pathways
- **Express Entry System** – Filters applications for skilled professionals, international students, and business entrepreneurs
- **Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)** – Each of Canada’s ten provinces runs independent programs to accommodate various immigrants
- **Investment and Business Immigration** – Entrepreneurs can migrate through investment pathways

## FAQs

**1. Who can apply under the Parents and Grandparents Program (PGP)?**
- Canadian residents or citizens can sponsor their **parents and grandparents**, including spouses.

**2. How many applications are accepted each year?**
- Typically, **10,000 applications** are accepted under PGP annually, but this may vary.

**3. What is the EOI and Invitation to Apply process?**
- Sponsors submit an **Expression of Interest (EOI)**, IRCC shortlists applicants, and selected sponsors receive an **Invitation to Apply** for PR.
