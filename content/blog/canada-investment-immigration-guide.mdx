## Overview of Canada Investment Immigration Program
Since the inception of the **Canada Investment Immigration Program**, many Indians have invested in the country to gain **permanent residency**. Canada selects individuals who can contribute both **economically** and **culturally**, offering a stable business environment and global market access.

As of 2020, Canada ranked 13th globally in economic size and continues to attract **foreign investments**. Many Fortune 500 companies have established **R&D units** in Canada, leveraging favorable regulations and a skilled workforce.

## Trade Agreements & Market Access
Canada has **15 trade agreements with 51 countries**, providing access to over **1.5 billion consumers**. Key agreements include:

- **CUSMA (Canada-United States-Mexico Agreement)**: Modernized North American trade.
- **CETA (Canada-EU Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement)**: 98% tariff reduction for Canadian exports to the EU.
- **CPTPP (Comprehensive and Progressive Agreement for Trans-Pacific Partnership)**: Duty-free access to 11 countries including Australia, Japan, Singapore.
- **EFTA & Other Agreements**: Agreements with Iceland, Norway, Switzerland, Ukraine, Chile, Israel, Korea, etc.

These agreements provide **low-risk global market access** for investors under the **Canada Investment Program**.

## Why Invest in Canada?
- **Skilled Workforce**: 60% of Canadians aged 24–65 have high-quality education.
- **Low Taxes**: Marginal Effective Tax Rate at 13.7%, lowest in the G7.
- **Political Stability**: Among the most stable G7 countries.
- **Banking & Safety**: 6 major banks listed among the safest globally; low corruption.
- **Innovation & Technology**: Government support for research, AI, and tech development.
- **Quality of Life**: Affordable living, excellent healthcare, free education till 18, multicultural inclusivity.

## FAQs
**1) Who can apply for Canada Investment Immigration?**  
Individuals with sufficient capital, business experience, and willingness to invest in Canada can apply for the **Canada Investor Program**.

**2) What are the main benefits of this program?**  
Applicants can obtain **permanent residency**, access global markets, enjoy a stable economy, and benefit from Canada’s low-tax environment.

**3) How long does it take to get approval?**  
Processing varies based on the program and documentation, usually between **12–18 months**.

**4) Can family members join the main applicant?**  
Yes, the **spouse and dependent children** can be included under the Canada Investment Immigration program.
