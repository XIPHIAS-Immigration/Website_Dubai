**British Columbia** held the most recent draw on **17th September 2019**. In this draw under **Express Entry**, **Skills Immigration**, and **Entrepreneur Stream**, a total of **519 invitations** were issued.

The draw was the **36th draw** of the **BC Provincial Nominee Program (BC PNP)**. Up until this point in 2019, BC PNP has issued a total of **5,499 ITAs**.

- **Express Entry & Skills Immigration**: 481 invitations
- **Entrepreneur Stream - Base**: 30 invitations
- **Entrepreneur Stream - Regional Pilot**: 8 invitations
---
## Who can apply?
- **Skilled and semi-skilled professionals** in high-demand occupations can apply under the **Skills Immigration category**.
- Candidates with a valid profile in the **Express Entry pool** may apply under the **BC Express Entry sub-category**.
- Qualified applicants should be willing to **live in BC permanently** and meet **BC PNP requirements**.

**Cut-off scores**
- Express Entry & Skills Immigration: **92–101 points**
- Entrepreneur category: **108–115 points**

**Entrepreneur category requirements**:
- Must have **board and business experience**
- Minimum **net worth**:
  - Regional Pilot: $300,000
  - Base category: $600,000

The **BC SIRS (Skills Immigration Registration System)** manages all provincial nominee categories. Applicants must create accounts in SIRS, and each candidate is scored based on **education, work experience, language proficiency**, and other factors.
