## What is EU Citizenship by Descent?

**EU citizenship by descent** is a pathway to obtain a European passport through family lineage rather than residence, employment, or investment. Countries in Europe often allow citizenship claims if you can prove a direct ancestral connection.

**Benefits include:**

- **Visa-free travel** across the EU and Schengen area
- The ability to **work and study** anywhere in Europe
- Access to **social benefits and healthcare**

## Which Countries Offer Citizenship Through Ancestry?

Popular countries include:

- **Ireland:** Citizenship via parent or grandparent
- **Italy:** Allows claims through multiple generations
- **Poland:** Citizenship based on descent from Polish ancestors
- **Portugal & Spain:** Claims from parents or grandparents depending on circumstances

Understanding each country’s rules is crucial for a successful application.

## Who is Eligible?

Eligibility depends on direct ancestry. Most countries require proof that **one of your parents or grandparents** was a citizen. Some countries consider great-grandparents.

**Required documents:**

- Birth certificates linking you to your ancestor
- Marriage certificates if surnames differ
- Proof that the ancestor retained citizenship at your birth
