## Germany Residency by Investment Program

**Germany Residency by Investment Program** is an initiative taken up by the **German Government** to encourage more **entrepreneurs** with vivid ideas and **investors** to invest in **Germany** either in an already-running business or in a new business venture.

### About Germany

**Germany** is one of those rare countries that has tried to retain most of its nature intact and is yet one of the world's strongest growing economies. A **Western European country**, it houses world-famous economically important cities such as **Frankfurt, Berlin, and Munich**. It has huge agricultural lands, **vineyards, banks, and skyscrapers**. **Germany** is also the **automobile hub** of the world and houses the **European Central Bank**.

### 21 AufenthG Act (German Residence Act)

**Entrepreneurs, business investors, and self-employed individuals** can earn their **residence permit** through this act by either investing in a **running business** (acquiring a business) or by establishing a **new German company**. Individuals wanting to invest in **real estate in Germany**, under privileged cases, can also apply under this act.

To be eligible to apply under this act you should:

1. Have a minimum investment of **EUR 300,000 – 350,000**
2. Be a **foreign national** not living in Germany at the time of application
3. Provide documentation, such as a **business plan**, showing your investment will have a positive effect on the **German economy**

### Who Can Apply

- **Foreign entrepreneurs** who want to set up a **start-up business** in **Germany**
- **Foreign entrepreneurs** who want to set up an **overseas branch** in **Germany**

**Freelancers, directors, managers, and legal representatives** of business partnerships and stock corporations can apply through this act as long as they do not have employee status.

Such investors qualify for a **long-term visa** and **temporary residence permit** to live in **Germany**. The longevity of this visa is **2 to 3 years**, which can be extended further. After 3 years of living in **Germany**, one qualifies for a **permanent settlement permit**. To qualify for the **permanent settlement permit**, the applicant needs to visit **Germany** at least once every year during the 3 years.

### Section 9, AufenthG (Permanent Settlement Permit)

The **German Permanent Residency Visa** or **Permanent Settlement Permit** is granted for 3 years. **High-net-worth individuals** who have demonstrated subsistence qualify for this program.

### German Citizenship

Individuals who have continuously lived in **Germany** for **8 years** qualify for **naturalization** for **citizenship** in **Germany**. Such individuals cannot have any criminal record and should show sufficient finances to support themselves, along with **German language fluency**.

## FAQs

**Who is eligible for the Germany Residency by Investment Program?**
**Foreign entrepreneurs** and **investors** who wish to establish or invest in a **business in Germany** can apply.

**What is the minimum investment required?**
The minimum investment required ranges between **EUR 300,000 – 350,000** depending on the type of **business**.

**How long before I can apply for permanent residency?**
Applicants can apply for a **Permanent Settlement Permit** after **3 years** of living in **Germany** under the residency program.

**When can I apply for German citizenship?**
Individuals can apply for **naturalization** after **8 years** of continuous residence, meeting **financial, language, and legal requirements**.
