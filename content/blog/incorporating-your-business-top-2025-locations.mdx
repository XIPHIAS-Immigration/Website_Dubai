## United Kingdom – The Global Financial Hub

The UK has reshaped itself post-Brexit, but **London remains a top global financial hub**. For 2025, two visa routes stand out for **entrepreneurs and overseas businesses**:

### UK Expansion Worker Visa – Expand Your Business in the UK

The **UK Expansion Worker Visa** is ideal for overseas firms establishing their first **UK office**. It allows companies to bring key staff to the UK to manage operations, build teams, and grow **business presence** efficiently by incorporating.

### Self-Sponsorship Visa – Entrepreneurs Controlling Their Future

The **Self-Sponsorship Visa** is designed for **entrepreneurs** who want full control over their UK venture without relying on an employer sponsorship. It offers a pathway to **long-term residency** while managing your own business strategy.
