Popularly known as the **Spice Island**, Grenada is one of the most attractive waterfronts in the Caribbean. Sailing, diving, luxury dining, stunning beaches, and vibrant festivals make it a preferred destination for high-net-worth investors seeking **second citizenship**.

## Requirements of Grenada Citizenship by Investment

Grenada's **Citizenship by Investment Program** was launched in 2013 under the **Grenada Citizenship by Investment Act**. Applicants must meet one of the investment options along with these eligibility criteria:

- Be of **outstanding character**
- Hold **no criminal record**
- Have **excellent health**
- Possess a **high personal net worth**

### Investment Options

The [Grenada Citizenship by Investment](https://www.xiphiasimmigration.com/grenada-citizenship-by-investment-immigration.html) program offers two main routes:

#### 1. National Transformation Fund Donation

- **Minimum Contribution:** US$150,000 for main applicant; US$200,000 for a family of four
- **Additional Dependents:** US$25,000 each
- Donations are transferred in two installments

#### 2. Real Estate Investment

- **Mount Cinnamon Shares:** Minimum US$350,000 for a family of four; US$25,000 per additional dependent
- Covers government, application, passport, and escrow fees
- **Mount Cinnamon Freehold:** Limited 25 whole-ownership units at US$450,000; 5-year mandatory holding in hotel rental pool

## Advantages of Grenada Citizenship

- Fast processing within **4 months**
- Dependent children under 26 included
- Dependent parents over 55 included
- **No physical residency requirements**
- No interview, education, or managerial experience required
- Visa-free travel to **100 countries**, including Schengen zone and China
- E-2 treaty with the **United States** for business opportunities
- **No tax on worldwide income**

## FAQs

**Who is eligible for Grenada Citizenship by Investment?**
High-net-worth individuals who meet the character, health, and financial criteria, and are ready to make the qualifying investment.

**What is the minimum investment required for citizenship?**
US$150,000 for the National Transformation Fund donation, or US$350,000 in real estate investment for a family of four.

**How long does the Grenada Citizenship process take?**
The entire process typically takes **3 to 4 months** from submission to receipt of the second passport.

**Does Grenada Citizenship offer visa-free travel?**
Yes, citizens can travel to **over 100 countries**, including the Schengen zone and China, with the Grenada passport.
