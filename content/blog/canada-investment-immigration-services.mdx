## Why Canada for Investment Immigration

Canada offers political stability, strong GDP growth, and multiple **federal and provincial pathways** for entrepreneurs who can **create jobs**, invest in **businesses/farms**, and meet compliance requirements.

## Canada Start-Up Visa (SUV)

Canada invites **innovative founders** to launch businesses and generate **Canadian jobs**.

**Highlights:**
- Prepare and get approval for a **business plan**.
- Obtain a **work permit** after support from a **designated organization**.
- **Within 1 year**, set up and operate the business.
- Maintain **50% ownership** and **active management**.
- Keep robust **documentation** for program compliance.


## Manitoba — Entrepreneur / Farm Investment

**Highlights:**
- **Pathways:** Entrepreneur or Farm Investment.
- **Personal net worth:** **CAD $500,000**.
- **Initial investment:** CAD $250,000 (Winnipeg/capital) or CAD $150,000 (rural).
- **Agricultural investment:** CAD $300,000 minimum.
- Conduct thorough **business research**.


## Prince Edward Island (PEI) — Work Permit Route

**Highlights:**
1. Own **33%–100%** of a PEI business.
2. Obtain a **temporary work permit**; may need **exploratory visit**.
3. Prove **net worth:** CAD $600,000.
4. **Escrow deposit:** CAD $200,000 (refundable).
5. **Minimum business investment:** CAD $150,000.
6. Submit **EOI** via official portal.

---

## Saskatchewan — SINP Entrepreneur & Farm

**Highlights:**
1. **EOI → Temporary Work Permit → Business Performance Agreement (18 months) → Nomination**.
2. **Net worth:** CAD $500,000.
3. **Investment:** CAD $300,000 in major centers; CAD $200,000 in rural areas.

---

## Quebec — Investor Program (QIIP)

**Highlights:**
1. **Net worth:** CAD $2,000,000 (legal proof required).
2. **Investment:** CAD $1,200,000 (government-guaranteed, 5 years).
3. Must intend to **settle in Quebec** and meet program-specific criteria.

---

## British Columbia (BC) — Entrepreneur Immigration

**Highlights:**
1. **Net worth:** CAD $600,000 (cities) or CAD $300,000 (rural).
2. **Minimum investment:** CAD $200,000.
3. Own **33%** of business or invest **CAD $1,000,000**.
4. Create **at least one job** for a Canadian/PR.
5. **XIPHIAS** assists with documentation and compliance.

---

## New Brunswick (NB) — Entrepreneur Program

**Highlights:**
1. **Net worth:** CAD $600,000 (CAD $300,000 liquid).
2. **Investment:** CAD $250,000.
3. **Escrow deposit:** CAD $100,000.
4. Comply with **Business Performance Agreement** to get nomination.

---

## Ontario (OINP) — Entrepreneur Stream

**Highlights:**
1. **Net worth:** CAD $1,500,000 (major cities); CAD $800,000 elsewhere.
2. **Investment:** CAD $1,000,000 (major cities); CAD $500,000 elsewhere.
3. Submit **EOI**, sign **Business Performance Agreement**, receive **nomination** for PR.
4. **XIPHIAS** provides end-to-end support, including **post-landing assistance**.

## General Eligibility

- **Age:** 18–55 years.
- **Experience:** 3+ years in last 5 years (management/ownership).
- **Language:** Meet **CLB** in English/French.
- **Background:** Clean criminal record; medical clearance.
- **Education:** At least **higher secondary** (or Canadian equivalent).
- **Adaptability:** Ability to **settle in Canada**.

---

## Benefits

- Obtain **Canadian PR** and eventual **citizenship (~4 years)**.
- Family inclusion; children get **public education**.
- Access **Medicare** and high living standards.
- Proximity to **USA** for expanded business opportunities.
- Eligibility for **Canadian universities** on PR.

## FAQs

**1) SUV vs. Entrepreneur PNP — which should I choose?**  
- **SUV:** Innovative idea + designated organization support.  
- **Entrepreneur PNP:** Buy/start a local business meeting **net worth**, **investment**, and **job creation** targets.

**2) Can my family get PR with me?**  
Yes. Eligible **spouses/partners** and **dependent children** are usually included.

**3) Do I always need an LMIA?**  
Not always. Some **owner-operator/entrepreneur** scenarios have exemptions if you **actively manage** the business.

**4) What documents matter most?**  
Proof of **net worth/source of funds**, **business ownership/management**, **business plan**, **investment**, **police and medical clearance**, plus **language** and **education** verification.
