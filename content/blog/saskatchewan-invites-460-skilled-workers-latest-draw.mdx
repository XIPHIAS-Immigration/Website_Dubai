**Saskatchewan**, on **28th November**, conducted the most recent **Express Entry** and **Occupations In-Demand draw**, inviting **460 skilled workers** to apply for a **provincial nomination** for **Canadian PR**.

The **SINP Express Entry sub-classification** is linked to the **Federal Express Entry Program**.

- **Express Entry applicants**: 200 invitations, provincial nomination grants **600 extra CRS points**, ensuring an **ITA** in the next round.
- **Occupations In-Demand applicants**: 260 invitations, **no job offer required**.

Applicants must have **at least 1 year of work experience** in an eligible skilled occupation related to their field of study.

Since **25th September**, SINP has conducted **15 draws**, issuing **5,689 invitations** to both **Occupations In-Demand** and **Express Entry candidates**. The list of eligible occupations expanded from **19 to over 200**, covering a wide variety of professions.

In the **28th November draw**, applicants from **97+ occupations** were invited.

To apply under **Occupations In-Demand** or **Express Entry**, candidates must submit an **EOI (Expression of Interest)** to SINP, scored out of 100. The **minimum score** in this draw was **69**. Applicants with foreign degrees must have an **educational credential evaluation (ECE)**.

## FAQs

**How many skilled workers were invited in the latest Saskatchewan draw?**
A total of **460 skilled workers** were invited under **Express Entry** and **Occupations In-Demand**.

**Do applicants need a job offer to apply?**
No, applicants do **not need a job offer** to apply under either **Occupations In-Demand** or **Express Entry**.

**What is the minimum score required in this draw?**
The **minimum score** for both categories was **69** in the 28th November draw.

**How are foreign educational credentials evaluated?**
Applicants must have an **educational credential evaluation (ECE)** for degrees or diplomas obtained outside Canada.

**How do I apply for Saskatchewan SINP?**
Submit an **EOI (Expression of Interest)** to SINP. Qualified EOIs are scored, and top candidates are invited to apply for **provincial nomination**.
