# Introduction

Exciting developments are in store for individuals aspiring to secure **Canada PR** through the **Quebec Immigrant Investor Programme (QIIP)**, set to resume operations on **January 1, 2024**. After a temporary suspension in 2019, the QIIP has undergone substantial revisions. Prospective investors must familiarize themselves with the significant changes incorporated into the program's eligibility criteria.

## Key Revisions in the QIIP (Quebec Investment Immigration Program)

**French Language Proficiency:** The revamped QIIP requires applicants to demonstrate **high intermediate proficiency in spoken French**, equivalent to level 7.

**Residency Requirement:** Applicants must reside in **Quebec** for a minimum of 6 months within two years of obtaining a work permit. Additionally, either the principal applicant or their spouse/de facto spouse must reside for another 6 months in Quebec.

**Financial Criteria:**
- Minimum **net worth of CAD $2 million** (including spouse’s assets).
- Mandatory 5-year risk-free investment of **CAD $1 million** with **IQ Immigrants Investisseurs Inc.** within 120 days of the Minister’s request.
- Non-refundable contribution of **CAD $200,000** through a financial intermediary.

**Management Experience:** Applicants must have at least **2 years of management experience** in the 5 years preceding the application.

**Educational Qualification:** Applicants must hold a diploma equivalent to at least a **secondary school diploma in Quebec** obtained before application submission.

### Impact of the Changes

The revised QIIP emphasizes language proficiency, residency requirements, and significant financial commitments. While the program remains a valuable pathway to **Canada PR**, the stricter requirements may influence investor participation and Quebec’s competitiveness internationally.

### Conclusion

With the reopening of the **Quebec Investment Immigration Program**, prospective investors should thoroughly understand the updated criteria. The program underscores Quebec’s commitment to attracting individuals who contribute to the province's linguistic, cultural, and economic development.

## FAQs

1. **When will the Quebec Investment Immigration Program resume?**
A. The **QIIP** is set to resume on **January 1, 2024**.

2. **What are the financial requirements for the QIIP?**
A. Applicants must have a **net worth of CAD $2 million**, make a **CAD $1 million risk-free investment** for 5 years, and a **non-refundable CAD $200,000 contribution** through a financial intermediary.

3. **Is French language proficiency mandatory?**
A. Yes, applicants must demonstrate **high intermediate French proficiency (level 7)**.

4. **How long must I reside in Quebec under QIIP?**
A. The principal applicant must reside **at least 6 months**, and either the applicant or spouse must reside an additional **6 months** in Quebec.

5. **What are the educational and experience requirements?**
A. Applicants must hold a diploma equivalent to **secondary school in Quebec** and have **2 years of management experience** in the 5 years prior to application.
