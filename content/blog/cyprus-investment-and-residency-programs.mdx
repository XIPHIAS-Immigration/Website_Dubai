## Cyprus Investment Pathways

### 1. Cyprus Permanent Residency Program

**Purpose:** Boost foreign direct investment.

**Eligibility & Requirements:**
- Applicants must be **non-Europeans**.
- **Investment in real estate:** Minimum €300,000.
- Initial payment of **€200,000 + taxes** required.
- **Bank deposit:** €30,000 maintained for 3 years.
- Minimum annual income: €30,000 for single applicants, +€5,000 for spouse, +€8,000 per child.

**Process:**
- Processed by **Civil Registry and Migration department**.
- Application verdict in **2 months**.
- No residence requirement, but visit **once every two years**.
- Applies to applicant, spouse, and dependent children under 25.

### 2. Cyprus Citizenship by Investment Program

**Purpose:** Obtain **Cypriot citizenship** through high-value investment.

**Eligibility & Requirements:**
- Investment of **€2.15 million**.
- Additional government contributions: €75,000 × 2.
- Investment options: **real estate, business, alternate funds, or financial assets**.

**Benefits:**
- Live, work, and study anywhere in the **EU**.
- Citizenship can be passed to **future generations**.
- Approval time: **7–8 months**.

## FAQs

**1) What is the Cyprus Investment Program?**
It offers **residency or citizenship** to investors who make qualifying investments in real estate or businesses in Cyprus.

**2) How much investment is required for Cyprus Permanent Residency?**
A minimum of **€300,000** in approved real estate and a **€30,000 bank deposit** for three years.

**3) How long does it take to get Cyprus Citizenship by Investment?**
Typically **7–8 months** after application and verification of the investment.

**4) Can my family be included in the Cyprus investment application?**
Yes, the **spouse and dependent children under 25** can be included.
