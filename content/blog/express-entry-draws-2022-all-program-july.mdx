## Express Entry Draws — July 2022 Overview

On **July 6** and **July 20, 2022**, **Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC)** ran the first **all-program** **Express Entry** draws in 18 months.

- **Invitations to Apply (ITAs):** **3,250** (combined)
- **CRS cut-offs:** **557** and **542** (both **very high**)
- **Program mix (both draws combined):**
  - **PNP:** **1,464** candidates
  - **CEC:** **1,164** candidates
  - **FSW:** **622** candidates

---

## What Is Express Entry?

**Express Entry** (launched **January 2015**) manages applications for **permanent residence** in **Canada** through a points-based system called the **Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS)**. Profiles enter the **EE pool** and top-ranked candidates receive **ITAs** in periodic draws.

### CRS Factors (at a glance)

- **Age** · **Education** (with **ECA** if foreign)
- **Language** results (**IELTS/CELPIP/TEF** → **CLB** scores)
- **Skilled work experience** (Canadian & foreign)
- **Additional points:** **provincial nomination (+600)**, **job offer**, **Canadian study**, **French**, **siblings**, etc.

---

## Programs Managed by Express Entry

### Federal Skilled Worker (FSW)
For candidates with **skilled** experience abroad. Baseline **67/100** on the FSW grid, plus competitive **CRS**.

### Federal Skilled Trades (FSTP)
For designated **skilled trades**. Requires recent **2+ years** experience, **language** thresholds, and either a **1-year job offer** or **certificate of qualification**.

### Canadian Experience Class (CEC)
For applicants with **≥1 year** of **Canadian** skilled work (last **3 years**) and required **language** scores.

---

## Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) & Express Entry

**PNP** lets provinces/territories select candidates to meet **local labour needs**. An EE-aligned **nomination** adds **+600 CRS** points—often guaranteeing an **ITA** in subsequent rounds. Popular options include **OINP (Ontario)**, **BC PNP (British Columbia)**, **MPNP (Manitoba)**, **SINP (Saskatchewan)**, **NSNP (Nova Scotia)** and **AAIP/AINP (Alberta)**.

## FAQs

**1) Why were the CRS cut-offs so high (557 & 542)?**
After a long pause on **all-program** draws, demand in the **EE pool** was high and invitations limited—keeping **CRS** thresholds elevated.

**2) Do I need a job offer to qualify?**
Not for **FSW/CEC**. A **job offer** can add points (and is required for some **FSTP/PNP** streams), but many candidates succeed without one.

**3) How can I raise my CRS quickly?**
Improve **language scores (CLB)**, pursue a **PNP nomination (+600)**, add **Canadian study/work**, or secure an eligible **job offer**.

**4) Can I be considered for multiple programs at once?**
Yes. A single **Express Entry** profile can be considered across **FSW**, **CEC**, **FSTP**, and any EE-aligned **PNP** you pursue.
