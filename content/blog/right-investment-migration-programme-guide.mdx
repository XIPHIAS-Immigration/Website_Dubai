## Investment Migration Options

### Investment in New Business
Countries like UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand welcome individuals who invest in new businesses and create employment. These programs provide residency in exchange for economic contributions.

### Investment in Startups
With the rise of entrepreneurship, countries have introduced visas for innovative business founders. Examples:
- **Canada Start-Up Visa**
- **UK Innovator Visa Program**

### Investment in Real Estate
Many countries offer residency through real estate investments. Benefits include returns on investment, freedom to reside and travel, and visa-free access to multiple countries. Top destinations: Portugal, Turkey, Spain, Greece, Malta, Caribbean residencies (Dominica, St. Lucia, St. Kitts & Nevis, Grenada, Antigua & Barbuda).

## Country-Specific Programs

### Canada
- **Immigrant Investor Program / Canada Investor Visa:** Earn Permanent Residency while investing in Canada. Citizenship eligibility after 3 years.
- **Start-Up Visa Program:** For entrepreneurs starting innovative businesses that create jobs and contribute to the economy.
- **Saskatchewan Immigrant Nominee Program:** Invest or start a business in Saskatchewan to qualify for nomination.
- **Ontario Immigration Program:** Start or purchase a business in Ontario to be nominated for Permanent Residency.

### USA
- **EB-5 Investor Visa:** Invest $800,000–$1,050,000 depending on area designation, create jobs, and obtain Green Card. Includes Integrity Fund fee of $1,000. Program valid through 2027.

### Australia
- **Business Innovation and Investment Visa Subclass 188:** Valid for 4 years; pathways to permanent residency.
  - **Business Innovation Stream:** For entrepreneurs starting or managing a business.
  - **Investor Stream:** Invest AUD 2.5 million in approved investments.
  - **Significant Investor Stream:** Invest AUD 5 million.
  - **Entrepreneur Stream:** Support for startup founders recommended to the Victorian Government.

### Germany
- **Investor Residency Program:** Invest in regional development funds for temporary residency; permanent residency after 3 years and citizenship after 8 years. Access to free education, healthcare, and high quality of life.

## FAQs

**What are the main types of investment migration programs?**
Investment in new businesses, startups, and real estate are the primary pathways.

**Which countries offer investment migration programs?**
Canada, USA, Australia, Germany, UK, Portugal, Turkey, Spain, Greece, Malta, and select Caribbean nations.

**What is the minimum investment for the US EB-5 program?**
$800,000 in Targeted Employment Areas and $1,050,000 in Non-Targeted Employment Areas.

**Can investors gain citizenship through these programs?**
Yes, most programs lead to permanent residency first, with citizenship eligibility after meeting residence and other requirements.
