## FAQs

**1) What is “corporate migration” vs. a normal business relocation?**
Corporate migration combines **immigration strategy** (visas, permits, founder/employee moves) with **entity strategy** (where to incorporate, taxes, hiring), and ongoing **compliance**. It’s a holistic plan for global expansion.

**2) Which routes are popular for startups and tech firms?**
The **Canada Start-Up Visa (SUV)**, **UK Innovator Founder Visa**, and **Intra-Company Transfer (ICT)** options are common, alongside **UAE** free-zone setups and **Singapore** company establishment with founder work authorization.

**3) Do I need revenue or external funding to qualify?**
Not always. Some programs prioritize **innovation** and **endorsement** (e.g., designated organizations for SUV/UK routes), while others emphasize **job creation**, **investment**, or **intra-group transfers**. A route fit assessment is essential.

**4) Can my team move with me?**
Often yes. Many corporate pathways allow **dependent** moves and offer **sponsored work permits** for key hires. The specifics depend on the country and visa class.

**5) How do I stay compliant after landing?**
Plan for ongoing **immigration renewals**, **payroll/tax registrations**, **HR policies**, and **substance requirements**. XIPHIAS provides post-landing compliance support to keep your operation audit-ready.
