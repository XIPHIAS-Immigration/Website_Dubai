The United States has always been a popular destination for **businessmen and professionals** looking to realize their American dream by establishing a company. The **EB-5 Immigrant Investor Program** is one of the most effective ways to achieve this goal. It allows investors and their immediate families to obtain a **U.S. Green Card** and become lawful permanent residents.

Choosing the right investment option is critical. Investors can either make a **Direct EB-5 Investment** or invest through a **Regional Center**. Here’s why **Direct EB-5 Investment** is often the preferred choice for those seeking faster approval, more control, and higher returns.

## What is EB-5 Direct Investment?

A **Direct EB-5 Investment** requires an investor to invest in a real operating business that **creates at least 10 full-time jobs** for U.S. workers. Unlike Regional Center investments, direct investment allows the investor to actively manage the business and withdraw their investment if needed.

**Key points of Direct EB-5 Investment:**

- Faster I-526 approval than Regional Center projects
- Flexibility to start a new business or invest in an existing one
- Full control over day-to-day operations
- Responsibility for creating and maintaining jobs

## Top Benefits of an EB-5 Direct Investment

### 1. Shorter Timelines to Start Your Business
Direct EB-5 investments allow investors to **begin operations almost immediately**. Unlike Regional Center projects that involve lengthy administrative processes, economic reports, and pre-approvals, a Direct Investment can move faster to develop, market, and fund the business.

### 2. Lower Administrative Fees
Regional Center investments require **administrative, legal, and filing fees** in addition to the investment amount. Direct EB-5 investment allows investors to **save on these costs** and allocate the entire capital toward the business.

### 3. Greater Control Over the Business
Direct EB-5 investors **manage the company themselves**, giving them full authority over operations, strategies, and growth. Regional Center investors have very limited influence, usually only voting on major decisions.

### 4. Higher Return on Investment (ROI)
Direct investments offer **unlimited earning potential**, similar to owning a startup or entrepreneurial venture. Regional Center projects tend to generate **lower returns** due to shared ownership and limited control.

## EB-5 Direct Investment vs Regional Center Investment

| Advantages | Direct EB-5 Investment | Regional Center EB-5 Investment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Type of Investment | Direct | Indirect |
| Source of Funds | Investor provides proof of funds | Investor provides proof of funds |
| Job Creation | 10 direct jobs | Direct/Indirect jobs based on formulas |
| Ownership | Investor owns equity | Investor gives a loan for 5 years |
| Management | Full control by investor | Managed by Regional Center |
| Control | Complete control | Minimal control |
| Experience Required | Must have relevant experience | No experience required |
| Timeline | 12–18 months | 18–24 months |
| ROI | Higher, due to active management | Lower, limited by project structure |

## FAQs

**Q1: What is the minimum investment for EB-5 Direct Investment?**
A: The minimum investment is $1,050,000 for most projects, or $800,000 in targeted employment areas.

**Q2: How long does it take to get a Green Card through EB-5 Direct Investment?**
A: Direct EB-5 investments typically take 12–18 months for I-526 approval, faster than Regional Center investments.

**Q3: Can family members be included in an EB-5 Direct Investment application?**
A: Yes, spouses and unmarried children under 21 can obtain Green Cards along with the primary investor.

**Q4: Is there more control in Direct EB-5 than Regional Center investment?**
A: Yes, investors in Direct EB-5 have full control over business operations and decision-making.
