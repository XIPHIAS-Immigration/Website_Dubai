## Introduction
The **UK Tier 1 Entrepreneur Visa** is designed for **entrepreneurs and businesspeople** planning to **invest in the UK**. Eligible investment ranges from **£50,000 to £200,000** and must be held in an **authorized bank or capital firm**, with funds **free to invest in the UK**. Applicants may also use funds owned by a **spouse/partner**.

This visa allows you to **set up a new business or invest in an existing UK business**, provided you are from **outside the EEA and Switzerland** and meet the eligibility criteria.

The **UK Investor Visa** is intended for **high-net-worth individuals** who wish to **migrate with family**. Only a limited number of visas are granted annually, and a strong track record is required for success. **Family members**—spouse, partner, and children under 18—can be included in the application.

For visa **extension**, investors must demonstrate **investment funds of at least £2 million** in the UK over the first visa period.

---

## Permanent Residence with Tier 1 Visa UK

Depending on the **amount invested**, investors can apply for **permanent residence (ILR)** in the UK:

- **£2 million investment** → PR after **5 years**
- **£5 million investment** → PR after **3 years**
- **£10 million investment** → PR after **2 years**

## UK Business Visa Overview

Non-UK residents from **outside the EEA or Switzerland** can apply for a **UK Business Visa** for **short-term business activities**. This visa is not available for applicants who already **qualify for British citizenship or dual nationality**.

Other long-term business visa options fall under the **Tier 1 visa UK** category.

---

## UK Business Visa Requirements

Applicants must meet the following criteria:

- Be **outside the EEA or Switzerland**
- Be at least **18 years old**
- Provide **evidence of financial support** during the visit
- Show **bank statements** for the last 3 months
- Provide an **invitation or training confirmation letter**
- Submit an **original passport**
- Include **appointment confirmation and application form copies**
- Provide **authorized company representative contact details**
- Show **proof of earnings**

## FAQs

**1. What investment funds qualify for the UK Tier 1 Entrepreneur Visa?**  
Funds between **£50,000–£200,000**, held with an **authorized bank/capital firm**, freely available in the UK, and verifiably owned by you or an eligible **spouse/partner**.

**2. Can family members be included in the application?**  
Yes, **spouse/partner** and **children under 18** can be included, subject to documentation and maintenance requirements.

**3. What documents support a UK Business Visa application?**  
**Bank statements (3 months)**, **invitation/training letters**, **passport**, company contact info, proof of earnings, and purpose of visit.

**4. How does investment affect permanent residence timelines?**  
**£2m → PR after 5 years**, **£5m → PR after 3 years**, **£10m → PR after 2 years**, subject to all program requirements.
