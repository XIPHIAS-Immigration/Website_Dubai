**Manitoba**, a beautiful province in **Canada**, offers its **Business Investor Stream (BIS)** under the **Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program (MPNP)** to attract high-net-worth individuals. This program allows entrepreneurs and farm investors to invest, establish businesses, and secure **Canada Permanent Residency (PR)**.

## Manitoba Business Investor Stream

Manitoba’s **Business Investor Stream** has two primary pathways:

1. **Entrepreneur Pathway**
2. **Farm Investor Pathway**

### Entrepreneur Pathway

The **Entrepreneur Pathway** is designed for experienced business owners seeking to invest in Manitoba.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- Minimum **3 years of business experience** in the last 5 years
- Ownership of at least **33.3% of the business**
- Achieve Canada **language benchmark** for PR
- Minimum age: **25 years**
- **Investment Requirements**:
  - **CAD $250,000** in Manitoba Capital
  - **CAD $150,000** in other regions of Manitoba
- Personal net worth: **CAD $500,000**
- Business must create at least **1 local job**
- Submit **medical certificates** and **police clearance**

**Process Overview:**

1. Enter a **Business Performance Agreement** with the Manitoba government
2. Obtain a **temporary work permit** for 1 year
3. Comply with program regulations and submit **progress reports**
4. Receive **provincial nomination**
5. Apply for **Canada PR** through **IRCC**

### Farm Investor Pathway

The **Farm Investor Pathway** is for individuals with proven **farming experience**.

**Requirements:**

- Minimum **3 years farming experience** in the last 5 years
- Investment in Manitoba **farm land** (minimum **CAD $300,000**)
- Provide similar documentation as required for the **Entrepreneur Pathway**

### Benefits of Canada PR through Manitoba BIS

- Eligibility for **Canada Citizenship** in 4 years
- Access to **North American markets** for business growth
- Opportunity to live, work, and study in **Canada**
- Family members can also apply for PR
