# Overview: UK Investment Immigration & Business Routes

# UK Innovator Visa Program

## Eligibility Requirements
- Must not be part of the European Economic Area or Switzerland
- Minimum age: 18 years
- Sound health and certificate of good character
- Meet English language requirements
- Proof of financial self-sufficiency

## Investment & Financial Requirements
- Minimum investment: £50,000
- Self-sufficiency: Bank balance of £945 for 90 consecutive days (independent of investment)

## Endorsement by Authorized Bodies
- Submission of business idea to endorsing bodies
- Business idea must be new and show promise for growth

## Processing Time and Decision
- Process duration: 3 months
- Decision communicated within 3 weeks

## Validity, Extension & Settlement
- Visa validity: 3 years
- Can be extended/renewed multiple times
- Eligible for settlement after 3 years

## Business & Family Benefits
- Set up one or multiple businesses
- Work for your own business
- Bring family to the UK
- Cannot work for others or claim public funds

# UK Start-up Visa Program

## Eligibility Requirements
- Must not be part of the UK, EEA, or Switzerland
- Requires letter of endorsement from a UK higher education institution or approved business organization

## Business Idea Requirements
- Idea must be new, original, innovative, and scalable
- Must demonstrate English proficiency

## Financial Maintenance
- Maintain bank balance of £945 for 90 consecutive days to prove self-dependence

## Processing Time and Decision
- Process duration: 3 months
- Decision communicated within 3 weeks

## Validity, Extension & Switching
- Visa validity: 2 years
- Cannot be renewed; may switch to other visa routes after 2 years

## Business & Family Benefits
- Can bring family to the UK
- Can work as an employee while setting up a start-up

# UK Sole Representative Business Program

## Purpose and Scope
- Set up a subsidiary of your overseas business in the UK
- Long-term assignment; media and broadcasting representatives can also apply

## Eligibility & Work Experience
- Must have worked for the overseas business for several years prior to application

## Processing Time, Fees & Decision
- Process duration: 3 months
- Decision communicated within 3 weeks
- Application fee: £610

## Validity and Extension
- Visa validity: 3 years
- Can be extended by 2 more years

## Work & Family Benefits
- Can work for your company
- Bring family to the UK
- Cannot seek employment outside your company
- Cannot claim public funds or switch visa type

# FAQs

## Q1. What is the minimum investment required for the UK Innovator Visa?
A. £50,000 in investment funds, endorsement, English proficiency, and maintenance requirements

## Q2. Does the UK Start-up Visa require an initial investment?
A. No, but endorsement, innovative business idea, English proficiency, and maintenance funds are required

## Q3. How long is the Sole Representative visa valid and can it be extended?
A. Valid for 3 years; can be extended by 2 more years

## Q4. Can my dependents accompany me on these visas?
A. Yes, spouse/partner and eligible children can accompany applicants on Innovator, Start-up, and Sole Representative visas
