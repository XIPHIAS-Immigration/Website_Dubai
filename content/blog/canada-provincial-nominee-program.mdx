## Overview
**Canada provinces** offer various immigration streams through the **Canada Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)**. This program allows aspirants to gain provincial nominations, boosting their eligibility for **Canada Permanent Residency (PR)**. Each province has defined immigration streams. Working with **ICCRC registered consultants** ensures updated guidance on PNPs to help you relocate faster.

## Provincial Nominee Programs by Province
### Alberta Provincial Nominee Program (AINP)
**Alberta** offers multiple streams under the **Alberta Immigration Nomination Program (AINP)**:
- **Alberta Opportunity Stream** – for those with a work permit in Alberta
- **Alberta Express Entry Stream** – selects candidates from the **Express Entry** pool based on provincial requirements

### British Columbia Provincial Nominee Program (BC PNP)
**BC PNP** has three main streams:
- **BC Skilled Worker Program** – for skilled professionals in management, technology, trade, and other occupations
- **BC Express Entry Program** – for candidates in the Express Entry pool meeting provincial criteria
- **BC PNP Tech Pilot Program** – targets technology professionals

### Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program (MPNP)
**Manitoba PNP** offers three pathways:
- **Skilled Worker Overseas Pathway** – similar to **Express Entry**
- **Manitoba Express Entry Pathway** – selects candidates from the Express Entry pool
- **Human Capital Pathway** – for skilled professionals with a valid job offer  
Minimum score required: 60 points

### New Brunswick Provincial Nominee Program (NB PNP)
NB PNP provides two streams:
- **Express Entry Labour Market Stream**
- **Skilled Workers with Family Support Stream**

### Newfoundland & Labrador PNP
Three pathways:
- **Newfoundland Labrador Express Entry Skilled Worker**
- **Skilled Worker Category**
- **International Graduate Category**  
All require a valid job offer in the province

### Nova Scotia PNP
Streams include:
- **Nova Scotia Demand Express Entry Stream**
- **Nova Scotia Experience Express Entry Stream**  
Both are connected to the **Express Entry System**

### Ontario Provincial Nominee Program (OINP)
Ontario accepts applications under:
- **Ontario Express Entry Stream**
- **International Student Category**
- **Business Category Stream**

### Prince Edward Island PNP (PEI PNP)
The **smallest province** offers immigration streams for skilled professionals, usually aligned with **Express Entry**

### Saskatchewan Immigration Nominee Program (SINP)
Saskatchewan selects skilled professionals through:
- **SINP Express Entry Stream** – for candidates directly expressing interest in Saskatchewan

## FAQs
**1) What is the Canada Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)?**  
PNP allows provinces to nominate skilled professionals to increase eligibility for **Canada PR**

**2) Which provinces participate in PNP?**  
**Alberta, British Columbia, Manitoba, New Brunswick, Newfoundland & Labrador, Nova Scotia, Ontario, Prince Edward Island, Saskatchewan**, and others

**3) Do I need a job offer to apply for PNP?**  
Some streams require a valid **job offer**, while others rely on Express Entry profiles

**4) Can international students apply for PNP?**  
Yes, certain streams like **Ontario International Student Category** or **Nova Scotia Experience Express Entry Stream** accept international graduates

**5) How does Express Entry connect with PNP?**  
Some provincial streams select candidates directly from the **Express Entry pool**, improving chances for **Canada PR**
