## What is the EB-5 Program?

The **EB-5 program**, started in 1990, allows foreign investors to **invest in a U.S. business** and obtain **permanent residency** for themselves and their families. The investment must create a **minimum of 10 full-time jobs** for U.S. workers.

### Eligibility Requirements:

- Minimum investment of **USD $900,000** in a **Targeted Employment Area (TEA)** or **USD $1,050,000** in a non-TEA area.
- Proof of a **good personal and financial background**.
- Age **21 years or older**.

### Where Can You Invest?

- **Real Estate Projects** in the USA
- **Running or Starting a Business** in the USA

## FAQs

**Q1: What is the minimum investment required for EB-5?**
A: $900,000 in a TEA or $1,050,000 in a non-TEA area.

**Q2: Can my family relocate with me under EB-5?**
A: Yes, spouses and children under 21 are included.

**Q3: How long does it take to get a Green Card through EB-5?**
A: Typically a few months for Direct EB-5 investments once I-526 is approved.

**Q4: Do I need to prove English proficiency?**
A: No, EB-5 does not require language proficiency.
