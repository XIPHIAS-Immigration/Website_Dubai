### Overview
Spain’s **Digital Nomad Visa** lets non-EU professionals live in Spain while working for foreign companies or clients. With a simple process, low income threshold, and access to European quality of life, it’s one of the most appealing remote work visas in the EU.

### Why Spain’s Digital Nomad Visa
- Work remotely while living legally in Spain  
- Include dependents in the same application  
- Flexible pathway to long-term residence  
- Enjoy Spain’s world-class culture, food, and climate  
- Benefit from favourable tax treatment under Spain’s startup law
