### Overview
Italy’s **Digital Nomad Visa** lets non-EU freelancers and professionals live in Italy while working for foreign clients or companies.  
It offers a simple path to long-term residence, access to Italy’s healthcare and lifestyle, and the freedom to work from one of the world’s most beautiful countries.

### Why Choose Italy’s Digital Nomad Visa
- Live and work remotely in Italy without a local contract  
- Access healthcare, education, and family reunification benefits  
- Enjoy Italy’s vibrant culture and high quality of life  
- Renewable annually and convertible to permanent residence after 5 years  
- Supported end-to-end by **Xiphias Immigration**
