### Overview
The **EB-1B (Outstanding Professors and Researchers)** visa offers a direct path to a **U.S. Green Card** for internationally recognized academics and researchers with proven excellence in their field and a **permanent job offer** from a U.S. institution.

### Eligibility Overview
Applicants must have **at least three years of teaching or research experience** and show **international recognition** by meeting **two of six USCIS criteria** for outstanding achievement.

### Why EB-1B
- Fast-track **U.S. Green Card** for professors and researchers  
- **No labor certification (PERM)** requirement  
- Priority processing under the **first-preference employment category**
