### Overview
The **EB-1A visa** is a U.S. employment-based immigration pathway for individuals with **extraordinary ability** in **science, arts, education, business, or athletics**. It offers a **direct route to permanent residency** through **self-petition**, without the need for employer sponsorship or labor certification.

### Eligibility Overview
Applicants must demonstrate that they are among the **top professionals in their field** and meet at least **three of ten USCIS criteria**, or hold a **major internationally recognized award** such as a **Nobel Prize, Olympic medal, or Academy Award**.

### Why Choose EB-1A
- **No employer or sponsor required**  
- **Faster processing** under the first-preference category  
- **Direct Green Card eligibility**  
- **Global recognition** of professional excellence
