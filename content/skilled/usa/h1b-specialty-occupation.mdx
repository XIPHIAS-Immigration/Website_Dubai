### Overview
The **H-1B visa** is a U.S. non-immigrant work visa that allows **employers to hire foreign professionals** in **specialty occupations** requiring **highly specialized knowledge** and at least a **bachelor’s degree or equivalent**. It’s one of the most common pathways for skilled professionals to work legally in the United States.

### Eligibility Overview
To qualify, the applicant must have a **valid job offer** from a **U.S. employer** in a specialty field such as IT, engineering, finance, healthcare, or science.  
The employer must file a **Labor Condition Application (LCA)** with the Department of Labor and submit **Form I-129 (Petition for a Nonimmigrant Worker)** to USCIS.  
The H-1B is typically granted for **three years**, extendable to **six years**, and allows **dual intent** for future Green Card applications.
