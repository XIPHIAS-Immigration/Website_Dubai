### Overview
The **J-1 visa** is a U.S. non-immigrant exchange visitor visa created to **promote cultural, educational, and professional exchange**. It allows individuals such as **students, teachers, interns, researchers, and professionals** to live and train in the U.S. through **approved sponsor programs**.

### Eligibility Overview
Applicants must have a **designated sponsor organization**, **sufficient financial resources**, and **a clear intent to return to their home country** after program completion.  
Depending on their category, participants must meet **academic, professional, or training requirements** and hold a **valid DS-2019 form** issued by the sponsor organization.
