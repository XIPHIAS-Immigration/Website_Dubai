### Overview
The **EB-1C (Multinational Executives and Managers)** visa offers a **direct pathway to a U.S. Green Card** for senior executives and managers transferring from a **foreign company to its U.S. branch, subsidiary, or affiliate** under the same organization.

### Eligibility Overview
To qualify, applicants must have **worked abroad for at least one year** in a **managerial or executive capacity** within the past three years, and the **U.S. employer must have a qualifying corporate relationship** with the foreign entity.

### Why EB-1C
- **No labor certification (PERM)** required  
- Direct **Green Card route** for multinational executives and managers  
- Ideal for **L-1A visa holders** transitioning to permanent residency  
- Designed for **business expansion and intra-company transfers**
