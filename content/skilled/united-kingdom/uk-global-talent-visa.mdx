### Overview
The **UK Global Talent Visa** is designed for individuals who are **leaders or emerging leaders** in their respective fields — whether in science, technology, arts, academia, or culture. It provides an opportunity to **live and work in the UK without a job offer**, making it one of the most flexible immigration routes.
