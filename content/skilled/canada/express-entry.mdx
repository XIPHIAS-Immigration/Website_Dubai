### Overview
The **Canada Express Entry** system manages skilled immigration under the **Federal Skilled Worker**, **Federal Skilled Trades**, and **Canadian Experience Class** programs.  
It’s a **points-based system (CRS)** that ranks candidates based on age, education, language skills, and work experience.  
Applicants with the highest CRS scores receive **Invitations to Apply (ITA)** for **permanent residency** in Canada.

### Why Choose Express Entry
- Fastest route to Canadian permanent residency  
- No job offer required (CRS-based selection)  
- Transparent and merit-driven points system  
- Access to world-class healthcare and education  
- Family can migrate together under one PR application  
- Pathway to citizenship after 3 years of residence
