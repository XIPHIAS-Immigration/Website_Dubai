## Overview  
The **Global Talent Stream (GTS)** allows **Canadian employers** to hire **highly skilled foreign workers** in **tech and in-demand roles**.  
It offers **2-week processing** under **Category A (referral)** or **Category B (occupation list)** — a **fast-track pathway to Canadian PR**.  

## Why Choose the Global Talent Stream  
- **2-week work permit approval**  
- **Top Canadian employer opportunities**  
- **Direct PR pathway via Express Entry or PNP**  
- **Spouse and dependents included**
