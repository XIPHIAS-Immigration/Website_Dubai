applicationProcess:
  - Check eligibility for Express Entry, PNP, or GTS.
  - Take IELTS, CELPIP, TEF, or TCF language test.
  - Get Educational Credential Assessment (ECA).
  - Create an Express Entry profile and get CRS score.
  - Wait for an Invitation to Apply (ITA) for PR.
  - Submit PR application within 60 days.
  - For GTS, secure LMIA and apply for work permit.
  - Complete medical and police verification.
  - Receive visa or PR approval and move to Canada.

requirements:
  - Minimum 67 points under FSWP criteria
  - Valid English/French language test results
  - Skilled work experience in NOC TEER occupations
  - Educational Credential Assessment for foreign degrees
  - Proof of funds, medical, and police clearance
  - For GTS: employer sponsorship under Category A or B

faq:
  - q: What is Express Entry?
    a: It is an online, points-based system managing applications for Canada PR under FSWP, FSTP, and CEC.
  - q: Do I need a job offer for Express Entry?
    a: No, but it boosts your CRS score. Some PNP and GTS streams require one.
  - q: How long does PR processing take?
    a: Most Express Entry PR cases are processed within 6 months of receiving an ITA.
  - q: Can provinces select candidates directly?
    a: Yes, under the Provincial Nominee Program (PNP).
  - q: What is the Global Talent Stream?
    a: A fast-track work permit for highly skilled professionals in tech and specialized sectors.
  - q: Can I include my family?
    a: Yes, spouses and dependent children can be added to most PR and work permit applications.
