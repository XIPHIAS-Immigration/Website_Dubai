### Overview
The **Canadian Experience Class (CEC)** is a fast and reliable Express Entry stream for skilled workers who have gained **eligible Canadian work experience**.  
It rewards in-country experience, language ability, and workforce integration as primary pathways to permanent residency.

### Why Choose CEC
- Fast processing for in-country skilled workers  
- No job offer or proof of funds required (if working in Canada)  
- Recognises Canadian experience, improving success chances  
- Include family members in the application  
- Clear route to citizenship after meeting residency obligations
