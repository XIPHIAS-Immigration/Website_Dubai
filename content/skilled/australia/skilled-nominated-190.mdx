### Overview
The **Skilled Nominated Visa – Subclass 190** is an **Australia skilled migration program** for **qualified professionals** seeking **permanent residency** through **state or territory nomination**.  
Applicants gain **extra points** and **priority invitations** under Australia’s **points-tested system**, based on **age**, **English proficiency**, **skills assessment**, and **work experience**.  
This visa offers a **direct PR pathway** for skilled workers in **high-demand occupations**, allowing them to **live, work, and include family members** in Australia with long-term settlement and citizenship prospects.

### Why Choose Subclass 190
- State nomination adds points and boosts invitation chances  
- Permanent residency with commitment to nominated state or territory  
- Targets occupations on state and regional skills lists  
- Include eligible family members under one PR application  
- Full work, study, and Medicare rights  
- Pathway to Australian citizenship after required residency
