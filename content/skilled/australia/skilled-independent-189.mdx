### Overview
The **Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189)** is an **Australia PR pathway** for **qualified professionals** who want to **live and work permanently** in Australia **without employer or state sponsorship**.  
It operates under a **points-based system** that values **age, education, English skills, and work experience**, offering a **transparent and merit-based** route to permanent residency and citizenship.

### Why Choose Subclass 189
- **No sponsorship needed** — apply independently through SkillSelect  
- **Permanent residency** from visa grant  
- **Points-based selection** favouring skilled and high-demand professionals  
- **Freedom to live and work anywhere in Australia**  
- **Family inclusion** with full work and study rights  
- **Clear PR-to-citizenship pathway** after residency period
