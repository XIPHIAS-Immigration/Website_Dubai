### Overview
The **Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme (RSMS) Visa – Subclass 187** provides a **permanent residency pathway** for **skilled professionals** nominated by **regional Australian employers**.  
This visa allows workers to **live and work permanently** in regional areas, helping to address **local skill shortages** while offering a **secure future for professionals and their families**.

### Why Choose Subclass 187
- **Employer-sponsored PR** for regional skilled workers  
- **Incentive to live and work** in designated regional areas  
- **Full-time job offer** required under an approved employer  
- **Include family members** for PR, work, and study rights  
- **Pathway to Australian citizenship** after PR  
- **Boosts regional growth** and skill retention
