### Overview
The **Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa – Subclass 491** is an **Australia skilled migration program** offering a **pathway to permanent residency** for qualified professionals.  
It allows skilled workers to **live, work, and study in designated regional areas**, supporting Australia’s **regional workforce needs** through **state nomination or family sponsorship**.  
The visa promotes long-term settlement and provides a clear route to permanent residency via **Subclass 191**.

### Why Choose Subclass 491
- Regional nomination or family sponsorship for five-year stay  
- Pathway to permanent residency via Subclass 191  
- Priority for skills in demand across regional Australia  
- Include eligible family members in one application  
- Work, study, and live in designated regional areas  
- Incentives for long-term settlement and citizenship
