### Overview

The **Dubai Investor / Partner Visa** allows foreign investors and entrepreneurs to obtain **UAE residency** through verified **company shareholding** or **business investment**.  
This visa enables holders to **live, work, and manage their business in Dubai**, while also **sponsoring family members** under their residence permit.  
Valid for **2–3 years** and renewable, the Investor Visa offers long-term stability, access to **UAE banking**, **utilities**, and eligibility for an upgrade to the **Golden Visa** for larger investments.
