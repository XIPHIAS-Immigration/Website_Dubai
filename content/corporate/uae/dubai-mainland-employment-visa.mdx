## Overview

The **Dubai Mainland Company + Employment Visa** allows entrepreneurs to operate under the **Department of Economy & Tourism (DED)** with **100% foreign ownership**. Businesses can trade across the UAE, sponsor staff, and obtain **residence visas** valid for 2–3 years.
