## Overview
The **UK Self-Sponsorship Visa** lets entrepreneurs **start and manage their own UK business** while sponsoring themselves under the **Skilled Worker route**. With **100% foreign ownership**, this program offers a direct path to **UK residency and Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)** without needing a UK partner or investor. It’s the ideal option for professionals seeking long-term settlement through genuine business activity.
