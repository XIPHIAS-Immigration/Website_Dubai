## Overview
The **USA O1 Entrepreneur Visa** is designed for **exceptional entrepreneurs, founders, and professionals** with extraordinary ability in business, science, arts, or technology. It allows you to **live and work in the U.S. without investment or lottery**, supported by a U.S. sponsor or agent. With strong recognition through awards, media, or expert achievements, O1 visa holders can also **transition to a Green Card** under the **EB1A category**.
