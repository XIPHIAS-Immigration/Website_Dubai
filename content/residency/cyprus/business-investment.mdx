## Overview
The **Cyprus Golden Visa – Business Investment Route** allows non-EU nationals to obtain permanent residency by investing in a Cyprus-based company employing Cypriot or EU nationals. Dependents including spouse, children under 25, and parents are eligible. Only one visit every two years is required to maintain status.

### Investment Overview
Investors must contribute a minimum of **€300,000** in a Cyprus-based company with at least five full-time employees. Funds must be transferred from abroad in compliance with Cyprus regulations.
