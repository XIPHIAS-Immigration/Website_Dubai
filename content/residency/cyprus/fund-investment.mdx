## Overview
The **Cyprus Golden Visa – Fund Investment** program allows non-EU investors to obtain **permanent residency** by subscribing **EUR 300,000** to **regulated collective investment schemes** in Cyprus. Dependents, including spouse, children under **25**, and parents, are included. Residency requires only a **single visit every two years** to maintain status.

## Investment Overview
Applicants must invest a minimum of **EUR 300,000** in approved collective investment schemes, sourced from **lawful funds abroad**. The program allows residency without local employment while providing dependents access to **healthcare** and **education**.
