## Overview
The UAE Golden Visa through Real Estate Investment allows investors to obtain long-term residency by purchasing property with a minimum threshold of **€500,000**. This route provides stability, family inclusion, and access to the UAE’s thriving business and lifestyle ecosystem.

## Investment Overview
- **2-Year Residency Visa:** Minimum investment **€187,000**  
- **10-Year Residency Visa:** Minimum investment **€500,000**  

Properties must be fully owned and maintained for at least 2 years. Both off-plan and completed properties are eligible.
