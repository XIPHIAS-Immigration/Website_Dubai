## Overview
The UAE Specialized Talent / Influencer / Student route grants long-term residency to individuals who can **evidence national or international recognition** in their field. Spouse and **unmarried children up to age 25** can be included (children above age 25 may qualify where disability applies). The pathway is **achievement-based** — **no monetary investment** is required. No physical presence is needed to maintain your residency.

## Eligibility Categories

### 1. Scientists & Researchers
- Nomination from the **UAE Scientists Council**
- FWCI score ≥ 1.0 and h-index ≥ 10
- Verified publications, patents, or research outputs

### 2. Healthcare Specialists
- Nomination from the **Ministry of Health and Prevention**
- Active professional license or internationally recognized equivalence

### 3. Education Specialists
- Nomination from the **Ministry of Education**
- Recognized academic or teaching credentials

### 4. Industrial Specialists
- Nomination from the **Ministry of Industry and Advanced Technology**

### 5. Skilled Professionals (High Salary)
- Valid UAE employment contract
- Monthly salary **≥ AED 30,000**

### 6. Arts & Culture
- Recommendation from a competent UAE cultural authority (e.g., Dubai Culture and Arts Authority)
- Portfolio of notable exhibitions, awards, press coverage, or industry endorsements

### 7. Digital Technology & AI
- Recommendation from the **UAE Council for Artificial Intelligence and Digital Transactions**

### 8. Content Creators & Influencers
- Valid **UAE Influencer License** issued by the National Media Council (NMC)
- Endorsement by Dubai Culture and Arts Authority / **Creators HQ**
- Minimum annual income from digital content: **AED 360,000** (AED 30,000/month)
- Evidence of positive UAE portrayal, engagement quality, and brand collaborations
- Approval typically takes **30–45 working days** after Creators HQ endorsement

### 9. Outstanding Students
| Student Type | Institution | Minimum Score |
|---|---|---|
| High school graduate | UAE licensed institution | Cumulative ≥ **95%** |
| University graduate | Class A UAE university | GPA ≥ **3.5 / 4.0** |
| University graduate | Class B UAE university | GPA ≥ **3.8 / 4.0** |
| International graduate | Top-100 globally ranked (QS / THE / Shanghai) | GPA ≥ **3.5** |

Must apply within **2 years of graduation**.
