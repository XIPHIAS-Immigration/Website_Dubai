overview: 
  The Greece Golden Visa program, enacted under Law 4146/2013, provides non-EU investors with a renewable five-year residency permit through qualifying investments in Greece. The permit allows visa-free travel across the Schengen Area for up to 90 days within any six months and can be renewed indefinitely in five-year increments. Investors can choose between real estate and capital investment options to qualify. The program also extends to immediate family members, including spouse, children up to 21, and dependent parents. Greece offers a unique combination of rich history, scenic islands, vibrant cities, and a robust economy, making it an ideal destination for investors seeking both lifestyle and opportunity.
