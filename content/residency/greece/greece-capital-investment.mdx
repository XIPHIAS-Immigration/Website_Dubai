## Overview
The **Greece Golden Visa – Capital Investment** route enables non-EU investors to obtain a renewable **five-year residency permit** by making qualifying capital investments in regulated Greek instruments, companies, REICs, funds, bank deposits, or eligible startups. Thresholds range from **€250,000** to **€800,000** depending on the vehicle. The permit includes **Schengen** mobility and family inclusion, with potential **naturalization after 7 years** (subject to **183 days** annual residence and language proficiency).

## Investment Overview
**Qualifying vehicles & thresholds**
- **€800,000** — Market-traded **shares, corporate bonds, or Greek government bonds** (regulated markets, where applicable).  
- **€500,000** — Investment in a **Greek company** (shares or bonds); **REIC** subscription; **fixed-term bank deposit**.  
- **€350,000** — **Mutual funds** or **AIFs** investing primarily/exclusively in Greece.  
- **€250,000** — Eligible **startup investments** listed on the Elevate Greece National Register.  

**Notes:** Liquidity, lock-ins and regulation vary by product; verify fees and investor-suitability requirements before subscribing.
