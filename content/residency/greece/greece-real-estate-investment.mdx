## Overview
The **Greece Golden Visa – Real Estate Investment** route allows non-EU investors to obtain a renewable **five-year residency permit** by purchasing qualifying property in Greece. Investors may select from regional or major-city/island options with thresholds ranging from **€250,000** to **€800,000**. The permit allows **visa-free travel within the Schengen Area**, family inclusion (spouse, children up to **21**, dependent parents), and the ability to rent the property. Retain ownership of the qualifying asset to renew the permit; eligibility for citizenship is possible after **7 years** with a **183-day** annual stay and Greek language proficiency.

## Investment Overview
* **Thresholds & options**
  - **€800,000** — Single property (min **120 sqm**) in Athens, Thessaloniki, Mykonos, Santorini, or islands with population > 3,100.  
  - **€400,000** — Single property (min **120 sqm**) in other areas of Greece.  
  - **€250,000** — Purchase of a commercial property converted to residential (conversion completed before applying).  
  - **€250,000** — Purchase and full restoration of a listed historic/culturally significant property.
