The Balanced Category of the New Zealand Active Investor Plus Visa requires a minimum investment of NZD 10 million maintained for 5 years in approved assets such as bonds, listed equities, managed funds, and property development projects supporting housing or infrastructure in New Zealand.
This New Zealand investor visa option is designed for individuals seeking stable long-term investment opportunities through the New Zealand residency by investment program. Investors must spend at least 105 days in New Zealand during the investment period while maintaining their diversified investment portfolio.
