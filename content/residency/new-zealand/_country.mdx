## Citizenship Timeline and Requirements

The Active Investor Plus Visa provides a residency pathway for investors seeking long-term settlement in New Zealand.
- **Growth Category (NZD 5 million):** Investors must spend at least 21 days in New Zealand during the 3-year investment period.
- **Balanced Category (NZD 10 million):** Investors must spend at least 105 days in New Zealand during the 5-year investment period.
- **Additional investment flexibility:** This requirement may be reduced if additional investment is made above NZD 10 million.
- **Permanent Residency:** Shortly before the temporary residence expires, investors may apply for permanent residency. Once permanent residency is granted, there is no minimum stay requirement in New Zealand.
- **Citizenship eligibility:** After five years of continuous residency, investors may become eligible to apply for New Zealand citizenship.
- **Physical presence requirement:** Applicants must spend at least 1,350 days in New Zealand within five years, with no more than 125 days of absence in any single year.
