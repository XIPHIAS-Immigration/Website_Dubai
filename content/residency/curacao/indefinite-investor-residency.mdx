### Overview
The Curaçao Indefinite Investor Residency program offers lifetime residency to investors contributing a minimum of USD 850,000 in approved real estate, business, or government bonds. It is the highest-tier route, eliminating renewal requirements and providing a pathway to Dutch citizenship after 5 years.

### Investment Overview
Investors can choose among government-approved luxury properties, large-scale business projects, or Dutch Caribbean Securities Exchange bonds. Investments must be held for at least 5 years and meet strict due diligence standards.
