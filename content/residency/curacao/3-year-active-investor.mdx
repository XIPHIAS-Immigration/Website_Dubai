### Overview
The Curaçao 3-Year Renewable Investor Residency allows investors to obtain a residency permit by committing a minimum of USD 280,000 in approved bonds, business, property, or public shares. Applicants may include their spouse and dependent children under 25 years. A physical presence of 1 day per year is required to maintain residency, with eligibility for Dutch citizenship after 5 years of residence within the Dutch Kingdom territories.
---
### Investment Overview
Investors can select from three main investment options: government-approved bonds, property acquisitions, or business investments. The minimum investment is USD 280,000, and the investment must be maintained for at least 36 months. Proof of source of funds and compliance with Curaçao regulations are required. The permit is renewable every 3 years upon continued investment and compliance.
