## Overview
The **Latvia Business Investment** route lets non-EU investors obtain a temporary residence permit by making an equity investment in a Latvian company — a cost-efficient entry into **EU/Schengen** residency with **family inclusion** and a path to permanent residency and citizenship with continued legal residence.

## Investment Overview
- **Route:** Business Investment (equity)  
- **Minimum investment:** **€50,000**  
- **Company criteria:** Typically **< 50 employees**, turnover **< €10,000,000**, and local tax contribution (benchmark **≥ €40,000**/year)  
- **Holding:** Maintain the investment per OCMA rules to qualify for renewals and progression
