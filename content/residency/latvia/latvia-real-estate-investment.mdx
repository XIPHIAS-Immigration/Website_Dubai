## Overview
The **Latvia Real Estate Investment** route grants non-EU investors a temporary residence permit in exchange for purchasing qualifying property in Latvia. It offers **Schengen mobility**, family inclusion, and a clear progression toward permanent residency and citizenship once statutory residence and integration conditions are met.
