## Overview
The **Latvia Bank Deposit Residency** route lets investors obtain a Latvian residence permit via a **fixed-term deposit of €280,000** in a Latvian bank. It suits investors seeking **EU residency**, minimal physical presence, and **family inclusion**.

## Investment Overview
Invest **€280,000** for a **5-year term** in a Latvian bank. The deposit is **interest-free** and must be maintained to keep eligibility. The residence permit provides **Schengen Area** mobility.
