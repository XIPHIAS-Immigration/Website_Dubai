## Overview
The **USA EB-5 Non-TEA Investment route** provides a direct path to U.S. residency and Green Card through a higher investment of **$1,050,000** in a non-targeted employment area. This route allows flexibility in project choice and direct enterprise involvement.

## Investment Overview
- **Minimum Investment:** $1,050,000  
- **Type:** Direct investment in a U.S. business (Non-TEA)  
- **Requirement:** Create at least **10 full-time jobs**  
- **Risk:** Investment must remain "at risk" with no guaranteed returns  

## Comparison Between Investment Options
| Feature                  | Non-TEA Investment ($1,050,000) | TEA Investment ($800,000)       |
|---------------------------|---------------------------------|---------------------------------|
| Minimum Investment        | **$1,050,000** (higher capital) | **$800,000** (lower entry)     |
| Eligible Locations        | Any U.S. location               | Rural / High unemployment      |
| Risk Level                | Higher capital exposure         | Moderate, location-limited     |
| Job Creation Requirement  | 10 full-time jobs               | 10 full-time jobs              |
