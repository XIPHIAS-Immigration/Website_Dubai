## Overview
Monaco Residency by Bank Deposit allows individuals to secure residency without purchasing property. Applicants maintain a minimum bank deposit in Monaco and benefit from tax-free living, healthcare, education, and visa-free travel. This option suits those preferring liquidity and financial flexibility while gaining family inclusion.

## Investment Overview
This program requires a minimum **€500,000** held in a Monaco bank account. Investment types include fixed deposit accounts and flexible savings or investment accounts. Holding periods generally start at **1 year** for fixed deposits. Compliance with local banking rules is mandatory.

## Comparison between the investment options
| Feature | Bank Deposit | Property Investment |
|---------|-------------|--------------------|
| Minimum Investment | €500,000 | €500,000 + property |
| Liquidity | High, funds remain in bank | Low, tied to real estate |
| Family Inclusion | Children up to 25, spouse, parents case-by-case | Children up to 25, spouse, parents case-by-case |
| Tax Benefits | Full exemption | Full exemption |
| Complexity | Simpler banking arrangement | Requires property purchase/rental |
