## Overview
The **Hungary Donation to Public Trust** route grants a **10-year renewable residence permit** to non-EU investors who donate **€1,000,000** to a government-approved public trust. The permit enables **Schengen** travel (90/180 rule), includes **spouse and children under 18**, and offers a path to **permanent residency after 3 years** and **citizenship after 8 years**.

## Investment Overview
Applicants must donate a minimum of **€1,000,000** to an **approved public trust** that supports higher education or public functions. The donation must be completed **within 93 days** of the residence application. The donation is **non-refundable**.
