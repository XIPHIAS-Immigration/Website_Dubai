### Overview
The Malta Permanent Residence Programme (MPRP) through the **Government Contribution Route** allows non-EU, non-EEA, and non-Swiss nationals to secure permanent residence in Malta by making the mandatory government contribution and NGO donation. This route does **not** require property purchase or lease, offering flexibility and fast access to Schengen travel privileges. Applicants can include their spouse, dependent children, and eligible parents or grandparents. The program ensures a smooth process with minimal stay requirements, providing a stable Mediterranean lifestyle and access to high-quality healthcare and education.

### Investment Overview
- **Government Contribution:** €37,000 (non-refundable)  
- **NGO Donation:** €2,000 (non-refundable)  
- **Property:** Not required  
- **Family:** Spouse, dependent children (up to 26), and eligible parents/grandparents
