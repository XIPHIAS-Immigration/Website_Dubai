## Overview
The **Singapore Global Investor Programme – Business Investment Route** provides a direct path to Permanent Residency for investors willing to establish or expand a Singapore-based business. The program targets experienced business owners and entrepreneurs seeking a stable, business-friendly environment with excellent infrastructure, healthcare, and education. PR through this route offers family inclusion and a potential pathway to Singapore citizenship after holding PR for two years.

## Investment Overview
- **Route:** Business Investment  
- **Minimum Investment:** **SGD 10 million**  
- **Investment Options:** Establish a **new business** or expand an **existing Singapore-registered company**  
- **Preferred Sectors:** Technology, healthcare, advanced manufacturing, renewable energy  
- **Holding:** Maintain the investment throughout the validity of the Re-Entry Permit
