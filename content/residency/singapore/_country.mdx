overview: 
  The Singapore Global Investor Programme (GIP) provides high-net-worth individuals with an opportunity to obtain Permanent Residency by investing in a new or existing Singapore-based business, or through a family office. The program targets experienced entrepreneurs and business owners who wish to establish or expand operations in Singapore. PR through this route grants family inclusion, access to Singapore’s business ecosystem, and a potential pathway to citizenship after holding PR for two years. Singapore’s political stability, low crime rates, and world-class infrastructure make it an ideal destination for investors seeking both lifestyle and business opportunities.
