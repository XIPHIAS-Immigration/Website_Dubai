## Overview
The Singapore GIP Fund Investment Route offers a direct pathway to Permanent Residency through investment in government-approved funds. Family members, including spouse and children under 21, can be included, with a potential pathway to Singapore citizenship after 2 years.

## Investment Overview
- **Minimum Investment:** SGD 10–25 million  
- **Approved Funds:** Private equity, venture capital, growth funds  
- **Sectors:** Technology, healthcare, fintech, renewable energy, advanced manufacturing  
- **Management:** Professionally managed by fund managers  

## Comparison of Investment Options
| Feature | GIP Fund Investment | Business Investment | Single-Family Office (SFO) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Minimum Investment | SGD 10–25 million | SGD 10 million | SGD 200 million |
| Investment Type | Pre-approved funds | Direct business ownership | Family office management |
| Family Inclusion | Spouse + children under 21 | Spouse + children under 21 | Spouse + children under 21 |
| Citizenship Pathway | After 2 years PR | After 2 years PR | After 2 years PR |
| Management Role | Passive/Advisory | Active | Active/Managed by SFO team |
