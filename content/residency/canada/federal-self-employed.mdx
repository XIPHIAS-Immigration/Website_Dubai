## Overview
The **Federal Self-Employed Persons Program** enables experienced professionals in **cultural, artistic, or athletic fields** to obtain **Canadian Permanent Residency** by contributing to the country’s cultural or athletic development. It’s an ideal route for individuals who can demonstrate self-employment experience and intend to continue their profession in Canada.

## Investment Overview
This program **does not require a monetary investment**. Instead, eligibility is based on professional achievements, recognition, and intent to be self-employed. Applicants must demonstrate **financial capacity to settle** and support their families.
