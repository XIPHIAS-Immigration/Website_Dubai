### Overview  
The **BC PNP Entrepreneur Immigration – Base Category** allows experienced business owners and senior managers to **invest in and actively manage a business in British Columbia (BC), Canada**.  
Successful applicants can **obtain Canadian Permanent Residency (PR)** after meeting investment and job creation goals under a signed **Performance Agreement**.

### Investment Highlights  
- **Minimum Investment:** CAD $200,000  
- **Net Worth:** CAD $600,000 (verified)  
- **Job Creation:** At least 1 full-time position
