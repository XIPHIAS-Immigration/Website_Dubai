## Overview
The **Manitoba Entrepreneur Pathway** under the **Manitoba PNP** allows experienced investors and business owners to **start, buy, or manage a business** in the province. This program offers a **direct route to Canadian Permanent Residency (PR)** through active business performance — not refundable deposits.

## Program Highlights
- **Investment:** CAD $150,000–$250,000 depending on location  
- **Pathway to PR:** Achieve nomination after meeting business goals  
- **Family Benefits:** Spouse and dependents can live, work, and study in Canada  
- **Economic Growth:** Build your venture in Manitoba’s expanding economy  
- **No Deposit:** Performance-based, not capital-holding
