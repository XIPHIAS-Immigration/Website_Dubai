**Overview**
The **BC PNP Entrepreneur Immigration – Regional Pilot** allows experienced entrepreneurs to **start a new business in a smaller BC community** and work toward **Canadian Permanent Residency (PR)**.  
This program promotes **regional economic growth**, **job creation**, and offers **lower investment requirements** with strong local support.

**Investment Highlights**
- **Investment:** Minimum **CAD $100,000**  
- **Net worth:** Minimum **CAD $300,000 (verified)**  
- **Job creation:** At least **1 full-time local job**

**Why XIPHIAS Immigration**

**XIPHIAS Immigration** guides you through the **BC PNP Regional Pilot process** — from **selecting communities and securing referrals** to **business planning and PR application**, ensuring a **smooth path to Canadian residency**.
