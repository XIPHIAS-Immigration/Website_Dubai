## Overview  
The **Prince Edward Island (PEI) Work Permit Stream** allows experienced entrepreneurs to start or manage a business in PEI and gain **Canadian Permanent Residency**. It’s designed for investors ready to actively run a business in one of Canada’s most business-friendly provinces.

## Program Highlights  
- PR pathway through business investment in PEI  
- Minimum investment: CAD $150,000  
- No refundable deposit required  
- Family can live, work, and study in PEI  
- Government support for business setup and settlement
