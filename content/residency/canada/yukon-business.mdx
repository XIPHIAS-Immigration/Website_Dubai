## Overview
The **Yukon Nominee Program (YNP) – Business Stream** allows entrepreneurs and investors to establish or acquire a business in the Yukon Territory and obtain **Canadian Permanent Residency**.  
This program encourages economic growth in key sectors such as tourism, renewable energy, mining support, and trade.

## Program Highlights
- **Investment Requirement:** CAD $300,000 minimum.  
- **Direct Pathway to PR** after successful business operation.  
- **Lower Investment Threshold** than most Canadian provinces.  
- **Government Support** for immigrant entrepreneurs.  
- **Family Inclusion:** Spouse and dependents can live, work, and study in Canada.  
- **Operate in Scenic and Strategic Northern Markets.**
