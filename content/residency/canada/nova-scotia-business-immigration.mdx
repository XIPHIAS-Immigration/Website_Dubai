## Overview
The **Nova Scotia Entrepreneur Stream** under the **Nova Scotia Nominee Program (NSNP)** is aimed at experienced business owners and senior managers who wish to establish, purchase, or manage a business while living in Nova Scotia. This stream provides a clear route to **Canadian Permanent Residency (PR)** through active business operation and contribution to the local economy.

## Investment Overview
Applicants must invest a minimum of **CAD 150,000** into a new or existing Nova Scotia business and own at least **33.3%** of it. They must demonstrate a **net worth of at least CAD 600,000**, verified by a professional. The business must create **one full-time job** for a Canadian citizen or permanent resident.
