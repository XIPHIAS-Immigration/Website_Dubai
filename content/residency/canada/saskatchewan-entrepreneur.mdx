## Overview
The **Saskatchewan Immigrant Nominee Program (SINP) – Entrepreneur Category** enables experienced business owners and investors to establish or acquire a business in Saskatchewan and gain **Canadian Permanent Residency**.

## Investment Highlights
- **Investment:** CAD $200,000–$300,000  
- **Net worth:** CAD $500,000 (verified)  
- **Job creation:** Minimum two local full-time jobs  

## Why XIPHIAS Immigration
**XIPHIAS** assists entrepreneurs throughout the SINP process — from EOI submission, BEP drafting, and financial verification to post-landing support and PR filing — ensuring smooth business establishment and successful nomination.
