## Overview
The **New Brunswick Entrepreneurial Stream** helps business owners invest and manage a business in New Brunswick to gain **Canadian PR**.  

## Investment Highlights
- **Investment:** CAD $250,000  
- **Deposit:** Refundable CAD $100,000  
- **Net worth:** CAD $600,000 (CAD $300k liquid)
