## Overview
The **New Brunswick Entrepreneurial Stream** under the **New Brunswick Provincial Nominee Program (NBPNP)** is designed for experienced entrepreneurs ready to establish, acquire, or operate a business in the province. This program provides a clear pathway to **Canadian Permanent Residency (PR)** for applicants who actively manage their enterprise and contribute to the province’s economy.

## Investment Overview
Applicants are required to make a minimum eligible investment of **CAD 250,000** in a qualifying New Brunswick business and demonstrate a **minimum net worth of CAD 600,000**. An **exploratory visit** and an **approved business plan** are mandatory before applying.
