## Overview
The **Newfoundland & Labrador Entrepreneur Stream** allows experienced business owners and managers to invest and manage a business in the province for **Canadian PR**.

## Investment Highlights
- **Investment:** CAD $200,000  
- **Net worth:** CAD $600,000 (verifiable)  
- **Job creation:** 1 local full-time job  

## Why XIPHIAS Immigration
**XIPHIAS** helps with EOI drafting, document verification, exploratory visits, interview prep, and PR filing for smooth program approval.
