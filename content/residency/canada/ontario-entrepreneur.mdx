## Overview
The **OINP Entrepreneur Stream** lets experienced entrepreneurs start or buy a business in Ontario and gain **Canadian Permanent Residency**. Applicants begin on a **temporary work permit** and can apply for **PR** after meeting their business goals.  
Ontario offers a strong economy, tech hubs, and global connectivity — ideal for business growth and immigration.

## Program Highlights
- **Direct PR Pathway** via entrepreneurship.  
- **Investment Options:** CAD $200,000–$600,000.  
- **Operate in Ontario’s key industries.**  
- **Include Family** in PR application.  
- **No Deposit Needed** — performance-based system.  
- **Access to global business markets.**
