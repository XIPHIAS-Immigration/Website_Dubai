## Overview
The **Saskatchewan Farm Owner & Young Farmer Stream** allows experienced farmers and young agricultural entrepreneurs to establish or operate a commercial farm in Saskatchewan and achieve **Canadian Permanent Residency**.

## Investment Highlights
- **Investment:** CAD $150,000 minimum  
- **Net worth:** CAD $500,000 (Farm Owner) / CAD $300,000 (Young Farmer)  
- **Deposit:** CAD $75,000 refundable good faith amount  

## Why XIPHIAS Immigration
**XIPHIAS** guides farmers through every step — from exploratory visit planning and Farm Establishment Plan preparation to SINP filing and PR application — ensuring a successful transition to agricultural life in Canada.
