## **Overview**
The **Hong Kong Business Investment route** under the **Capital Investment Entrant Scheme (CIES)** allows investors to obtain residency by investing **HKD 30,000,000** in an approved Hong Kong business. Family members (spouse and dependent children) are included. No physical presence is required. Permanent residency can be applied for **after 7 years**.

## **Investment Overview**
Invest a minimum of **HKD 30,000,000** in a qualifying Hong Kong business and maintain it for **7 years**. Applicants must demonstrate **net assets ≥ HKD 30,000,000** and a **lawful source of funds**. Annual reporting and compliance are required throughout the visa period.
