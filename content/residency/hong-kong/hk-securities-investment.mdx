## **Overview**
The **Hong Kong Securities Investment route** under the **Capital Investment Entrant Scheme (CIES)** offers residency to investors who place **HKD 30 million** in **approved Hong Kong securities**. Family members can be included, and **no physical stay** is required. Permanent residency may be obtained after **7 years**.

## **Investment Overview**
Invest **HKD 30 million** in **CIES-approved securities** and maintain it for **7 years**. Applicants must show **HKD 30 million in net assets** and verify the **lawful source of funds**.
